Single Gloucester

This is made from full cream milk from the morning milking, and skimmed milk from the evening milking. All the milk is unpasteurised, and vegeterian rennet is used. The cheese is aged for at least three weeks.

Protected Designation of Origin (PDO)

It has been described by The Real Cheese Companion as having:
"a deliciously light texture and beautifully balanced taste, with a cool, clean tang and grassy sub-flavours."
