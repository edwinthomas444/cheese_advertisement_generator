Double Gloucester

This is made from full cream milk from both the morning and evening milkings, with annatto (traditional dye). All the milk is unpasteurised, and vegeterian rennet is used. It is aged for a minimum of 6 months.

It has been described by The Real Cheese Companion as:
"Rich, mellow, powerful, earthy, almost smoky", "the texture is slightly crumblier than Cheddar".

The price (retail) is £12.50 per kg
