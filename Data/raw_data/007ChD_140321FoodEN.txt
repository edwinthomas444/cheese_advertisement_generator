Harefield

This is a Smart's speciality cheese, and may be considered to be the Gloucester version of parmesan. It is made as the single Gloucester, but is aged for at least one year.

It has been described by The Real Cheese Companion as having:
"a hard, dry texture and a tang as salty/sharp but grassier and without the smoky tones of Parmesan".

The price (retail) is £15.80 per kg

Whole cheeses come in size*:
Six pounds (2.75 kg; £41.75)
