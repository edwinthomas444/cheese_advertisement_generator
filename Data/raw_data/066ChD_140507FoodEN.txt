Shropshire Red
 Shropshire Red is made by Belton Farm as part of their range of coloured cheeses -which includes Double Gloucester, Red Leicester and Red Fox.

Shropshire Red is a fabulous handmade cheese with an intense and complex flavour profile being a mix of sweet and savoury flavours with a hint of caramel. It has a rugged texture and a long lasting finish.

Currently only available in Sainsbury in Taste the Difference pre-packs and on the deli counter.
