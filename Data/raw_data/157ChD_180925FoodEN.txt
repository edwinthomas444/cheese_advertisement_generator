Ailsa Craig

Made from pasteurized goat's milk
Country of origin: Scotland and United Kingdom
Region: Stewarton
Type: semi-soft, artisan
Texture: creamy and fluffy
Rind: rindless
Colour: white
Flavour: creamy
Aroma: goaty
Vegetarian: yes
Producers: Dunlop Dairy
Ailsa Craig named after the landmark island in the Firth of Clyde is a Scottish cheese made from pasteurized goat's milk. Produced by Dunlop Dairy, at Clerkland Farm, Stewarton, Scotland, this rock shaped cheese is wrapped like a parcel. This fluffy, beautifully crafted cheese is creamy in taste with goaty flavour and instantly melts in the mouth.
