TRELAWNY
Made from pasteurized cow's milk
Country of origin: England
Region: North Cornwall
Family: Cornish
Type: semi-soft, artisan
Texture: crumbly
Rind: washed
Colour: ivory
Flavour: citrusy, creamy, lemony
Aroma: nutty
Vegetarian: yes
Producers: Whalesborough Farm Foods
Trelawny is a traditional farmhouse cheese and is the first of Sue Proudfoot's creations. It is made with pasteurized cow's milk and vegetable rennet. This Cornish cheese is mould ripened with a citrus flavour and a creamy texture.

Trelawny has won several accolades over the years including a gold at British Cheese Awards 2011 and two-star gold at Great Taste Awards 2013/2014.
