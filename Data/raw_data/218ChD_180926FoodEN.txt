TYNING
Made from unpasteurized sheep's milk
Country of origin: England
Region: Somerset
Family: Parmesan
Type: hard, artisan
Texture: brittle and dense
Rind: natural
Colour: yellow
Flavour: acidic, fruity, nutty
Aroma: fruity
Vegetarian: no
Producers: Mary Holbrook
Tyning is an artisan cheese made by the British cheesemaker Mary Holbrook. It is a hard cheese made from unpasteurised sheep's milk. The shape is that of a basket with a brittle and dense texture. It has a fruity, nutty and acidic flavour. The ageing for the cheese ranges from anywhere between three to twelve months. This cheese is best paired with a white wine.
