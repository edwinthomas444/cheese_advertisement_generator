PEMBROKESHIRE EXTRA MATURE CHEDDAR
Pembrokeshire Extra Mature Cheddar

Made from cow's milk
Country of origin: United Kingdom
Region: Pembrokeshire
Family: Cheddar
Type: semi-soft, artisan
Texture: crumbly and dense
Colour: yellow
Flavour: strong, tangy
Aroma: rich
Vegetarian: no
Producers: Pembrokeshire Cheese Company
Synonyms: Extra Mature Welsh Cheddar
Pembrokeshire Extra Mature Cheddar is a Welsh cow's milk cheese made by the Pembrokeshire Cheese Company in Pembrokeshire and is a part of First Milk, a largest dairy farmer co-operative in the United Kingdom.  This firm textured cheese is matured  for little longer time that gives a crumbly texture and more sweet and savory taste.
