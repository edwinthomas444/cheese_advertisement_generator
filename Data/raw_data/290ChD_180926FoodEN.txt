Pembrokeshire Mature Cheddar

Made from cow's milk
Country of origin: United Kingdom
Region: Pembrokeshire
Family: Cheddar
Type: semi-soft, artisan
Texture: dense
Colour: pale yellow
Flavour: smooth, tangy
Aroma: strong
Vegetarian: yes
Producers: Pembrokeshire Cheese Company
Synonyms: Mature Welsh Cheddar
Pembrokeshire Mature Cheddar is a Welsh cow's milk cheese made by the Pembrokeshire Cheese Company in Pembrokeshire and is a part of First Milk, a largest dairy farmer co-operative in the United Kingdom. This firm textured cheese has a delicious, creamy, sweet and savory taste and delicate sweetness.
