Flower Marie, made in East Sussex by Kevin and Alison Blunt, 
is a delicate, ripened, ewes' milk cheese, with a white bloomy rind.  
The cheese was created by the late and great James Aldridge, 
and was named after Ann-Marie Dyas, founder of The Fine Cheese Co.

Gold winner at the 2017 Artisan Cheese Awards.
