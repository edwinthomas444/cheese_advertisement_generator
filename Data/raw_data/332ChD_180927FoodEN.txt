Stinking Bishop Baby
An aromatic cheese, with a rich and powerful flavour.

Country of Origin	British Isles
Milk Variety	Cows'
Style of Cheese	Washed Rind & Aromatic
Vegetarian?	Yes
The rind is washed with perry (fermented pear juice) made from Stinking Bishop pears; and it is this process that gives the cheese its rich, powerful aroma and flavour. Made by Charles Martell on his farm in Gloucestershire.
