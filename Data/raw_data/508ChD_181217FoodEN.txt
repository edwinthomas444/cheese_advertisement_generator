TASTING NOTES
A Semi – Firm cheese with a sharp engaging almost citrus, buttery flavour. Liberally coated with dried Hereford hops which impart a rich toasty flavour to the cheese. Perfect with a glass of real ale to complement the hoppy flavour and a great present for ale enthusiasts.
