Coverdale
A creamy buttery next door neighbour to Wensleydale.
TASTING NOTES
A creamy buttery next door neighbour to Wensleydale, with a slightly sharper flavour. Medium strength, smooth in style, this is a very well made cheese. Coverdale is made in North Yorkshire by the Wensleydale Creamery, an ideal choice if you enjoy creamy, buttery, crumbly cheeses.
