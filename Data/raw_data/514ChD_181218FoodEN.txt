TASTING NOTES
A firm textured, robust black waxed cheddar with a good bite and acidity. Full flavoured, rich and creamy with good keeping qualities – extremely tasty. A must-try, versatile cheese for all cheddar conoisseurs. A cheese in honour of the RAF 617 squadron of Scampton, Lincolnshire. Great with a fruit chutney or fruit bread.

The individual cheese weighs 200g.
