Double Barrel Lincolnshire Poacher
An aged Lincolnshire Poacher with a full strong mellow flavour.
TASTING NOTES
An aged Lincolnshire Poacher with a full strong mellow flavour. Made on the farm by the Jones brothers to traditional methods from unpasteurised milk from their own 230 pedigree Holstein cows. The cheese has been taste selected and matured on for over 24 months and has great depth and complexity with a lovely smooth lingering finish and a hint of sweetness.
