Kit Calvert Wensleydale
Kit Calvert Wensleydale is a handcrafted buttery, textured Dales cheese made with natural rennet.
TASTING NOTES
Kit Calvert Wensleydale is a handcrafted buttery, textured Dales cheese made with natural rennet. Named after the father of Wensleydale cheese ‘KIT CALVERT’, whose efforts helped to save the Wensleydale Creamery from closure in 1935.
