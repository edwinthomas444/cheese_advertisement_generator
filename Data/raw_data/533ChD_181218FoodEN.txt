Smoked Lincolnshire Poacher
Smooth textured cheese with a cheddar character. Lightly oaked smoked.
TASTING NOTES
Hand-made at Ulceby Cross near Louth by the Jones brothers. Traditional Lincolnshire Poacher cheese is oak smoked to make smoked Lincolnshire Poacher. Smooth, silky textured and lightly oaked smoked. Rounded and full of flavour. Matured for fourteen to sixteen months. This cheese has been a Gold Medal winner at the British Cheese Awards.
