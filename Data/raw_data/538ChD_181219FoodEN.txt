TASTING NOTES
A Red Leicester style cheese with a crunch, Red Fox has a smooth, mellow flavour and a flaky, open texture. Like other red cheeses, Red Fox gets its colour from the flavourless dye from the South American plant annato. Red Leicester style cheeses go especially well in cheesy breads or sprinkled over a potato gratin.
