Sharpham Brie
Sharpham Brie is made is made on the Sharpham Estate in Totnes, South Devon since 1980 and is actually more like a cross between brie and coulommiers than a straight up brie. Made with the milk of Jersey cows which impart a rich, luscious texture to the creamy, mushroomy flavours.

Ideally paired with Sharphams own wines for a true taste of a micro terroir.

Region/ Country	United Kingdom
Pasteurised/ Unpasteurised	Unpasteurised
Rennet	Vegetarian
Milk	Cow
