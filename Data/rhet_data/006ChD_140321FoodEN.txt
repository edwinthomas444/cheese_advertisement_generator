

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing ﻿Double Gloucester, a rich and mellow cheese crafted from full cream milk from both morning and evening milkings. This traditional cheese is made with annatto dye and is produced by skilled artisans. Its name is derived from the historic region of Gloucestershire, England, known for its high-quality dairy products.

2) Description of Product:
﻿Double Gloucester is a semi-hard cheese with a slightly crumbly texture, making it perfect for slicing and grating. It comes in three sizes, ranging from 0.34 kg to 3.4 kg, catering to different needs and occasions. This cheese is made with unpasteurized milk and uses vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of Process:
The making of ﻿Double Gloucester involves a meticulous process, starting with the collection of milk from both morning and evening milkings. The milk is then combined with annatto dye and vegetarian rennet and left to age for a minimum of 6 months. This aging process gives the cheese its distinct earthy and smoky flavor.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The Real Cheese Companion describes ﻿Double Gloucester as having a powerful and rich aroma, with hints of earthiness and smokiness. Its flavor is mellow yet robust, making it a favorite among cheese lovers. The slightly crumbly texture adds to its unique taste, making it a must-try for all cheese connoisseurs.

5) Serving Suggestions:
To fully enjoy the flavors of ﻿Double Gloucester, it is recommended to serve it at room temperature. This cheese is best presented on a cheese board, accompanied by crackers, fruits, and nuts. It pairs well with a variety of foods, from sandwiches to pasta dishes. For a complete experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine, such as Cabernet Sauvignon.

6) Quality Assurance:
The quality of ﻿Double Gloucester is evident in its numerous accolades and awards, including recognition by The Real Cheese Companion. Its rich and mellow taste has been praised by cheese experts and consumers alike. Additionally, this cheese undergoes strict quality tests to ensure its freshness and authenticity. As a result, it has gained a reputation for being one of the finest cheeses in the market.
