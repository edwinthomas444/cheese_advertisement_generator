

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Harefield, a Smart's speciality cheese from the Gloucester region. This cheese has a rich history and is known for its similarities to parmesan.

2) Description of Product:
Harefield is a hard cheese with a dry texture, coated in a tangy, salty and sharp flavor. It is made with single Gloucester milk and aged for a minimum of one year. The cheese is available in two sizes - retail at £15.80 per kg and whole cheese weighing six pounds (2.75 kg) for £41.75.

3) Description of Process:
This cheese is made using the traditional single Gloucester method, but with an aging process of at least one year. This allows the cheese to develop its unique tangy and grassy flavor, without any smoky tones.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
According to The Real Cheese Companion, Harefield has a distinct aroma and taste - salty, sharp, and grassy. The cheese has a firm and dry texture, making it perfect for grating and adding to dishes.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving Harefield at room temperature. This allows the cheese to fully develop its flavors. It can be served on a cheese board, grated over pasta or salads, or paired with crackers and fruits. For a complete experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine.
