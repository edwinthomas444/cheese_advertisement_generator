

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Bedale Cheese, a handcrafted, hard pressed yet moist and crumbly cows milk cheese. Made by the Reed family in Richmond, Yorkshire, this cheese has a rich history dating back to the 11th century when it was first brought to the Yorkshire dales by Cistercian monks from Normandy.

2) Description of Product:
Bedale Cheese is a 200g cheese with a crumbly, moist texture and a tang similar to Wensleydale. Its yellow color and crumbly center are coated with a natural wax, giving it a unique appearance. Made with locally sourced milk and vegetarian rennet, this cheese is a true farmhouse delicacy.

3) Description of the Process:
Each batch of Bedale Cheese is carefully handcrafted using traditional cheese making techniques passed down from the Cistercian monks. It is then aged for four weeks, allowing it to develop its distinct flavor and texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Upon opening the wax coating, the aroma of Bedale Cheese is sure to tantalize your senses. Its crumbly yet moist texture is complemented by a tangy and slightly sweet taste, making it a true delight for cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving Bedale Cheese at room temperature. Its crumbly texture and tangy taste are perfect when paired with a rich fruit cake and a glass of aged port. However, feel free to experiment and find your own perfect pairing.

6) Quality Assurance:
Bedale Cheese is not just any ordinary cheese, it is a cherished treasure of the Yorkshire dales. It is accredited with Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) status, ensuring its quality and authenticity. Don't just take our word for it, try Bedale Cheese and taste the difference for yourself.
