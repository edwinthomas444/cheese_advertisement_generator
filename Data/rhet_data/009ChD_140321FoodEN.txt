

1) Description of Cheese: Swaledale Mature Cheddar Cheese, handcrafted by the Reed family in Richmond, Yorkshire. This cheese has a rich history, dating back to the 11th century when Cistercian monks brought their cheese-making techniques from Normandy to the Yorkshire dales.

2) Description of Product: This cheese is a hard pressed, yet moist cow's milk cheese, handmade in the traditional farmhouse style. It is coated in black wax and comes in a 220g truckle size. The ingredients used are locally sourced milk and vegetarian rennet.

3) Description of Process: The Swaledale cheese is carefully prepared and aged to perfection. The recipe and methods used have been passed down for generations and are now protected with PDO status. This ensures the quality and authenticity of this cherished cheese.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The Swaledale Mature Cheddar has a distinct mature flavor, with a buttery texture and appearance. Its aroma is rich and inviting, making it a delight for the senses.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, we recommend serving this cheese at room temperature, between 4-6 weeks old. It pairs perfectly with a traditional Yorkshire Ploughman's, and for a drink, we suggest Theakston's Old Peculier Ale.

6) Quality Assurance: The Swaledale cheese has received numerous awards and recognition for its exceptional quality and taste. It is accredited with PDO status, ensuring its authenticity and safeguarding its future production. As one of the most cherished treasures of the dales, this cheese is sure to impress even the most discerning cheese lovers.
