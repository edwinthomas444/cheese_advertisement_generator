

1) Description of Cheese:
"Indulge in the rich and flavorful Old Peculier Swaledale Cheese, handcrafted by the Reed family in Richmond since 1987. This traditional blue-grey mould cheese has a rich history, dating back to the 11th century when Cistercian monks from Normandy settled in the Yorkshire dales and passed on their cheese making techniques to the local farmers of Swaledale."

2) Description of Product:
"Our Old Peculier Swaledale Cheese is a hard pressed, yet incredibly moist cow's milk cheese. Each 1.8Kg wheel is coated with a natural rind, giving it a creamy yellow appearance and a distinct beer flavor. The cheese is made using locally sourced milk and vegetarian rennet, ensuring the highest quality and taste."

3) Description of Process:
"Our cheese is carefully crafted using traditional methods and aged to perfection. The curds are made with four fluid ounces of Theakstons Old Peculier ale per pound, giving it a unique and delicious flavor. The cheese is then aged for 4-6 weeks, resulting in a smooth and open texture that is sure to please your taste buds."

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
"Take a whiff of our Old Peculier Swaledale Cheese and you'll be greeted with a rich and inviting aroma. Upon first bite, you'll experience a creamy and moist texture, complimented by the distinct taste of beer. The perfect balance of flavors will leave you wanting more."

5) Serving Suggestions:
"For the ultimate cheese experience, we recommend serving our Old Peculier Swaledale Cheese at room temperature. It pairs perfectly with crusty bread, sliced pear, and a pink peppercorn and honey dressing. For a truly indulgent treat, try it in a classic Yorkshire rarebit. This cheese also pairs well with a variety of wines, making it a versatile addition to any cheese board."

6) Quality Assurance:
"Rest assured, our Old Peculier Swaledale Cheese is of the highest quality. It has been accredited with Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) status, ensuring that the traditional recipe and methods are preserved and recognized worldwide. Don't just take our word for it, our cheese has also won numerous awards and passed rigorous quality tests, solidifying its place as one of the most cherished treasures of the Yorkshire dales."
