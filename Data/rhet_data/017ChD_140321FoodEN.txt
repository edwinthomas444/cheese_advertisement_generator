

1) Description of Cheese: Swaledale with Chives and Garlic Cheese is a beloved treasure of the Yorkshire Dales, with a history dating back to the 11th century. Handmade by the Reed family in Richmond since 1987, this cheese is a testament to the region's rich cheese-making tradition.

2) Description of Product: This 1.8kg cheese is hard-pressed yet incredibly moist, with a creamy yellow color and a soft texture. The addition of chives and garlic creates a perfect balance of flavors, making it a unique and delicious choice for any cheese lover. It is coated in natural color wax, adding to its visual appeal. Made with locally sourced cow's milk and vegetarian rennet, this cheese is a true artisanal product.

3) Description of Process: Swaledale with Chives and Garlic Cheese is made using traditional methods and aged for 4-6 weeks. This allows the flavors to develop and the texture to become even more creamy and delicious. The cheese is carefully monitored throughout the aging process to ensure the highest quality.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Upon opening the wax coating, you will be greeted with a delightful aroma of chives and garlic. The taste is equally pleasing, with a perfect balance of these two flavors. The cheese itself has a soft and moist texture, making it a delight to eat.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of Swaledale with Chives and Garlic Cheese, we recommend serving it at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheeseboard as a centerpiece, or sliced and served on crackers or bread. For the perfect pairing, we suggest a dry Riesling, which complements the cheese's flavors and textures.

6) Quality Assurance: Swaledale with Chives and Garlic Cheese has been recognized globally for its exceptional quality and taste. It holds Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) status, ensuring that the traditional recipe and methods are safeguarded for future generations to enjoy.
