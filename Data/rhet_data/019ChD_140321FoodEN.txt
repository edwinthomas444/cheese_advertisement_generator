

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Richmond Smoked Cheese, a delicious creation from the Reed family in Richmond, Yorkshire. This cheese has a rich history dating back to the 11th century, when Cistercian monks brought cheese making techniques from Normandy to the Yorkshire dales. Today, the Reed family continues this tradition, using locally sourced milk to create their famous Swaledale cheese.

2) Description of Product:
Richmond Smoked Cheese is a hard pressed, handmade, farmhouse cheese made from cows milk. It has a creamy yellow color and a close, moist texture. The cheese is coated in a protective wax and weighs approximately 1.8Kg. It is made using vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of Process:
The process of making Richmond Smoked Cheese is a carefully crafted one. The cheese is aged for 4-6 weeks, allowing it to develop its unique flavor. The smoking technique used is gentle, creating a mild nutty smoked flavor that sets this cheese apart from others.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Upon opening the wax coating, you will be greeted with a delightful aroma of smoked cheese. The taste is equally delicious, with a creamy and moist texture and a mild nutty flavor that is balanced and not overpowering.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving Richmond Smoked Cheese at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board, sliced and served with crackers or bread. For a delicious meal, try stuffing it in a chicken breast with spinach and wrapping it in Parma ham. This cheese pairs well with a variety of wines, such as Pinot Noir or Chardonnay.

6) Quality Assurance:
Richmond Smoked Cheese has been recognized for its superior quality, with Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) status. This ensures that the cheese is made using traditional methods and protects its future production and quality.
