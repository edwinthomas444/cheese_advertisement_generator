

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Swaledale Blue Cheese, handcrafted by the Reed family in Richmond since 1987. This award-winning cheese has a rich history dating back to the 11th century when Cistercian monks brought cheese-making techniques from Normandy to the Yorkshire Dales. Made with locally sourced milk, Swaledale cheese is a true treasure of the dales and holds Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) status.

2) Description of Product:
Swaledale Blue Cheese is a hard-pressed, yet moist cow's milk cheese. Its distinctive blue veins running throughout give it a creamy appearance. This handmade, farmhouse cheese is coated in a blue wax and weighs approximately 500g. It is made using vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for all cheese lovers.

3) Description of Process:
The preparation of Swaledale Blue Cheese follows traditional techniques passed down for centuries. The cheese is aged for 4-6 weeks, allowing it to develop its soft, open texture and smooth nutty flavor with a slight tang. Each batch is carefully crafted to ensure the highest quality and consistency.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Upon opening, Swaledale Blue Cheese greets you with a rich, earthy aroma. The taste is a perfect balance of creamy and tangy, with a hint of nuttiness. The distinctive blue veins add a depth of flavor that is sure to please even the most discerning palates.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the ultimate cheese experience, serve Swaledale Blue Cheese at room temperature. Its beautiful appearance and unique flavor make it an excellent addition to any cheese board. Pair it with a beefy red wine for a heavenly combination. This cheese also makes a decadent blue cheese sauce, perfect for elevating any dish.

6) Quality Assurance:
Swaledale Blue Cheese has been recognized as one of the best in the world, placing second in the World Cheese Awards 2011. Its PDO status ensures that the cheese is made with the highest quality ingredients and follows strict production methods. As the saying goes, "you get what you pay for," and with Swaledale Blue Cheese, you are getting a truly exceptional cheese.
