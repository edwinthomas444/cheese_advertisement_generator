

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing the award-winning ﻿Swaledale Ewe Cheese PDO, a true treasure of the Yorkshire Dales. Handcrafted by the Reed family in Richmond since 1987, this cheese is steeped in history and tradition. Its origins can be traced back to the 11th century, when Cistercian monks brought their cheese-making techniques from Normandy to the local abbeys. Today, Swaledale cheese is recognized worldwide and holds Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) status, ensuring its quality and authenticity.

2) Description of Product:
This 1kg (approx) wheel of Swaledale Ewe Cheese PDO boasts a natural rind, giving it a traditional blue-grey coating. Its hard-pressed yet moist texture is achieved by using locally sourced ewes milk and vegetarian rennet. The cheese is shaped into a round wheel, perfect for slicing and serving.

3) Description of Process:
The preparation of Swaledale Ewe Cheese PDO is a labor of love, with each wheel being handcrafted to perfection. The cheese is then aged for 4-6 weeks, allowing its flavors to develop and mature. This process is closely monitored to ensure the highest quality and taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Upon first whiff, you'll be greeted with a mild and inviting aroma. The cheese's soft, moist open texture is complemented by a slight tang, making for a perfectly balanced flavor. Its smoothness and delicate taste make it a favorite among cheese connoisseurs.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the ultimate cheese experience, serve Swaledale Ewe Cheese PDO at room temperature. Its presentation is best enjoyed in slices, showcasing its beautiful blue-grey rind. This cheese pairs perfectly with a dry Champagne, enhancing its flavors. It also makes for a delicious cooking cheese, adding a unique touch to dishes like Ewes cheese and spinach soufflé.

It has also undergone rigorous quality tests, ensuring its consistency and excellence.
