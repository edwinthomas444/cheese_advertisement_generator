

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Swaledale Goats cheese, a handmade, farmhouse cheese made by the Reed family in Richmond, Yorkshire. This cheese has a rich history, dating back to the 11th century when Cistercian monks from Normandy first brought cheese making techniques to the Yorkshire dales. Today, Swaledale cheese is protected and accredited with Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) status, ensuring its quality and authenticity.

2) Description of Product:
Swaledale Goats cheese is a hard pressed yet moist cheese, made from locally sourced goats milk and vegetarian rennet. It is shaped into a round wheel and coated with wax, weighing 220g. The cheese has a creamy and velvety texture, with a mild smoked flavor from being smoked over oak chippings for 2 days.

3) Description of Process:
The process of making Swaledale Goats cheese is a traditional one, passed down through generations of cheese makers. The goats milk is gently smoked for 2 days, resulting in a creamy and moist cheese. The cheese is then aged for 4-6 weeks, allowing the flavors to develop and mature.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The gentle smoking process gives Swaledale Goats cheese a unique and enticing aroma. Upon tasting, you will experience a mild smoked flavor that is perfectly balanced with the creamy and velvety texture of the cheese. The flavor profile is rich and satisfying, making it a true delicacy.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving Swaledale Goats cheese at room temperature. To enhance its flavors, pair it with fresh fruits, nuts, and crackers. For a more indulgent experience, try it with a glass of full-bodied red wine, such as a Cabernet Sauvignon. When presenting this cheese, let its beautiful wax coating shine, showcasing its handmade and artisanal qualities.

6) Quality Assurance:
Swaledale Goats cheese has received numerous accolades and recognition for its exceptional taste and quality. It is accredited with Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) status, ensuring its authenticity and safeguarding its production methods. As one of the most cherished treasures of the Yorkshire dales, Swaledale Goats cheese is a testament to the dedication and expertise of the Reed family. As they say, "Taste the tradition and savor the quality."
