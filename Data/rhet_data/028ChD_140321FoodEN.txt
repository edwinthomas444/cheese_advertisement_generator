2) Description of Product:
This hard pressed yet moist goats milk cheese is coated with a smooth wax and weighs approximately 1.8kg. Its white color and soft, open texture make it stand out from its 'goaty' peers. Made using locally sourced milk and vegetarian rennet, this farmhouse type cheese is both delicious and ethical.

3) Description of Process:
The Swaledale cheese making process is protected and accredited with Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) status. This ensures the highest quality and consistency in every batch. The cheese is aged for 4-6 weeks, giving it a perfect balance of tanginess and mildness.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Upon opening, the Swaledale Goats Milk Cheese releases a tantalizing aroma that hints at its smooth and tangy taste. Its soft texture melts in your mouth, leaving a satisfying and creamy finish. Unlike other goat cheeses, Swaledale has a milder flavor that appeals to all palates.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For optimal enjoyment, serve the Swaledale cheese at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. It makes a stunning addition to any cheese platter, paired with crackers and fresh fruit. For a unique twist, use it as a cooking cheese in your favorite recipes. Its ability to hold its shape makes it perfect for grilling or baking. For those with dairy intolerance, Swaledale can be used as an alternative. Pair it with a crisp white wine or a fruity red for a delightful taste experience.
