

1) Description of Cheese:
British Camembert is a delicious and indulgent cheese, made by renowned cheese makers from three different regions in the UK. The Cornish Country Larder near Newquay, Lubborn Creamery near Chard in Somerset, and Carmarthenshire Cheese Company with their Pont Gar range all contribute to the unique and exceptional taste of this cheese. With a rich history and geographical provenance, British Camembert is a true representation of the best of British cheese.

2) Description of Product:
This cheese has a slightly fuller aroma and flavour than British Brie, making it a real treat for the taste buds. It has a soft and creamy texture that develops as it ripens, adding depth and complexity to its already delicious taste. The cheese comes in a round shape, with a smooth and creamy coating on the outside. Made from high-quality cow's milk and rennet, British Camembert is a premium cheese that guarantees a delightful experience with every bite.

3) Description of Process:
British Camembert is made using traditional methods, ensuring that every step of the process is carefully executed. The cheese is prepared by hand and aged to perfection, allowing the flavours to develop and mature. This attention to detail and dedication to the craft is what sets British Camembert apart from other cheeses on the market.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of British Camembert is rich and inviting, with hints of earthiness and creaminess. The taste is equally delightful, with a perfect balance of savoury and tangy notes. As the cheese ages, the flavours become more pronounced, making it a true delicacy for cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve British Camembert at room temperature to allow the flavours to fully develop. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers or served alongside a fresh green salad. For a unique twist, try deep-frying the cheese in breadcrumbs for a crispy and decadent treat. Pair it with a bold and robust Rioja for a perfect balance of flavours.

6) Quality Assurance:
British Camembert has received numerous accolades and awards, solidifying its position as one of the best cheeses in the UK. It has been praised for its exceptional taste and quality, with renowned chefs and food critics raving about its unique flavour profile. With strict quality tests and a commitment to excellence, you can trust that every bite of British Camembert will be a truly unforgettable experience.
