

1) Description of Cheese: Keens Farmhouse Cheddar
Made by Keens Cheddar in Somerset, England, this cheese has a rich history dating back to the late 12th century. It takes its name from the town of Cheddar and is made using traditional techniques.

2) Description of Product: Handcrafted and Full of Flavor
This award-winning cheese is made from locally produced milk and uses traditional Cheddar making techniques, including hand Cheddaring. It is available in a variety of sizes and weights, ranging from mild to extra mature. Made with pasteurized cow's milk and vegetarian rennet.

3) Description of Process: Aged to Perfection
Keens Farmhouse Cheddar is aged in the caves of Cheddar, providing the perfect environment for maturing the cheese. The process of Cheddaring involves turning and piling the curd to create a harder cheese with a firm body. The cheese is aged for a minimum of 9 months and is subject to regular grading throughout its life.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Complex and Nutty
As Cheddar matures, its taste develops from a young creamy flavor to complex, lasting, and slightly nutty flavors. The Farmhouse variety, made with unpasteurized milk, has a stronger and more complex flavor compared to the creamery counterparts. The extra mature and vintage Cheddars have a characteristic sweet and nutty flavor with a long finish.

5) Serving Suggestions: Perfect for Any Occasion
For the best flavor, serve Keens Farmhouse Cheddar at room temperature. This cheese can be enjoyed on its own or paired with fruits, nuts, and crackers. It also makes a great addition to sandwiches, salads, and cheese boards. For a complete experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Award-Winning and Highly Praised
Keens Farmhouse Cheddar has received numerous awards and accolades, including the EU Protected Designation of Origin "West Country Farmhouse Cheddar". It is made on a farm in the West Country using locally produced milk and traditional techniques. Customers rave about its complex flavors and high quality, making it a top choice for cheese lovers.
