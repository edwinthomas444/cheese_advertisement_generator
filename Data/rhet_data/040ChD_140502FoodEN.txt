

1) Description of Cheese: Shropshire Blue by Belton Farm
Originating from Scotland, Shropshire Blue is a unique cheese with a twist. It is now made in Shropshire by Belton Farm, along with other reputable dairies such as Colston Bassett Dairy and Long Clawson Dairy. 

2) Description of Product: A Colorful and Creamy Blue Cheese
Shropshire Blue is a soft and creamy cheese with a subtle orange hue, thanks to the addition of annatto to the milk during production. It has a smooth texture and is characterized by delicate blue veins running throughout. Made with cow's milk and rennet, this cheese is available in various sizes and weights.

3) Description of Process: Crafted with Care and Aged to Perfection
The production of Shropshire Blue involves a careful process of adding annatto to the milk, followed by aging for several weeks. This results in a rich and flavorful cheese with a perfect balance of colors and textures.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: A Delight for the Senses
The smell of Shropshire Blue is enticing, with a hint of earthiness and a tangy aroma. Its taste is equally delightful, with a mellow and creamy flavor that is complemented by the sharpness of the blue veins. 

5) Serving Suggestions: Perfectly Paired for Any Occasion
For the best experience, serve Shropshire Blue at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. It can be beautifully presented on a cheese board, paired with crackers, fruits, and nuts. This versatile cheese also pairs well with a variety of wines, such as a full-bodied red or a crisp white.

6) Quality Assurance: Recognized and Award-Winning Cheese
Shropshire Blue has received numerous accolades, including being made by award-winning dairies and being featured in top cheese selections. It has also been praised by cheese lovers and experts alike, with its unique taste and quality. Try it for yourself and discover the excellence of Shropshire Blue.
