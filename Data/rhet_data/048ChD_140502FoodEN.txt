

1) Description of Cheese:
Capricorn Goats Cheese is a delectable cheese made from soft goat's milk at the renowned Lubborn Creamery, located on the Cricket St Thomas estate in Somerset. This estate is also home to the famous TV series "To the Manor Born", adding to the prestige and history of this cheese.

2) Description of Product:
This cheese boasts a unique shape and size, with a thin white bloomy rind that adds to its visual appeal. It has a soft and runny texture as it matures, similar to a young Camembert, and is made using high-quality rennet and goat's milk.

3) Description of Process:
Capricorn Goats Cheese is carefully prepared at the Lubborn Creamery, using traditional techniques to ensure its superior taste and quality. It is then aged to perfection, allowing the curd to become soft and runny, resulting in a complex and irresistible flavor.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of Capricorn Goats Cheese is delicate and enticing, with hints of earthiness and creaminess. Its taste is equally delightful, with a perfect balance of tanginess and creaminess that will leave your taste buds craving for more.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Capricorn Goats Cheese warm with a sweet pickle as a starter. It also pairs well with salads, adding a creamy and tangy element to your dish. For a more indulgent experience, add it to your cheeseboard and enjoy with a glass of your favorite wine.

6) Quality Assurance:
Capricorn Goats Cheese has received numerous accolades and awards, solidifying its position as one of the best goat's milk cheeses in the market. With its superior taste and quality, it has been praised by cheese connoisseurs and customers alike, making it a must-try for all cheese lovers.
