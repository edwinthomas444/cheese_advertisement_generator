

1) Description of Cheese: Chatel, produced by Cornish Country Larder near Newquay, is a baby cheese of the Camembert family. This cheese has a rich history and is made in the beautiful region of Cornwall, known for its high-quality dairy products.

2) Description of Product: Chatel has a round shape and a small size, making it perfect for sharing or enjoying as a personal treat. Its texture is soft and runny when fully ripe, with a thin coating on the outside. This cheese is made from cows' milk and uses traditional rennet in its production.

3) Description of Process: Chatel is carefully prepared and aged by the skilled cheesemakers at Cornish Country Larder. The cheese is aged for the perfect amount of time to develop its unique flavor and texture, resulting in a truly exceptional product.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Chatel is rich and inviting, with hints of earthy and nutty notes. When tasted, this cheese has a complex and indulgent flavor, with a buttery and creamy texture that melts in your mouth.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Chatel at room temperature to allow its full flavor to develop. This cheese is perfect for a cheeseboard, accompanied by crackers, fruits, and nuts. It also pairs well with a crisp white wine, such as Sauvignon Blanc.
