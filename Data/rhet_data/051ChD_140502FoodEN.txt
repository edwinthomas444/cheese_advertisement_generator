

1) Description of Cheese:
Chevington is a delicious cheese made by the renowned Northumberland Cheese Company. It has a rich history and is produced in the beautiful region of Northumberland, known for its high-quality dairy products.

2) Description of Product:
This cheese is made from locally produced Jersey milk, giving it a unique and distinct flavor. It is crafted into shallow wheels, weighing around 5lbs, and has a hard, knobbly coat. The cheese is matured for 10-12 weeks, allowing it to develop its creamy texture and deep yellow color. We use only the finest ingredients, including traditional rennet, to create this exceptional cheese.

3) Description of Process:
Our cheese is carefully prepared using traditional techniques, ensuring the highest quality. It is then aged for 10-12 weeks, allowing it to develop its unique flavor and texture. Each wheel is handcrafted by our skilled cheese makers, ensuring that every bite is a true delight.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Chevington has a mild and creamy flavor, with a hint of nuttiness. It has a pleasant aroma that is sure to awaken your taste buds. The cheese has a smooth and creamy texture, making it perfect for any occasion.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving Chevington at room temperature. Its beautiful appearance makes it a perfect addition to any cheese board, and it pairs well with a variety of foods. We suggest serving it with crackers, fruits, and nuts for a delicious snack. For a more sophisticated pairing, try it with a full-bodied red wine.
