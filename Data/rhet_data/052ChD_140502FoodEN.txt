

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Cornish Blue, a delicious cheese crafted by the Cornish Cheese Company in Cornwall, England. This award-winning cheese has a rich history and is made in the beautiful countryside of Cornwall.

2) Description of Product:
Cornish Blue is a flat wheel-shaped cheese, approximately 6 inches in diameter and 2 inches in height. It has a unique appearance with a natural rind and blue veins running throughout the cheese. Made from pasteurized cow's milk and vegetarian rennet, this cheese is suitable for all cheese-lovers.

3) Description of Process:
Cornish Blue is carefully crafted by piercing the cheese from the top and bottom to allow the blue mould to work its magic. It is then aged to perfection, resulting in a creamy texture and a gentle, yet flavorful taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of Cornish Blue is inviting and the taste is even better. With a creamy and soft texture, it has a mild and slightly tangy flavor. The blue veins add a subtle sharpness, making it a delight for your taste buds.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Cornish Blue at room temperature. Its beautiful appearance makes it perfect for a cheese platter, paired with fruits, nuts, and crackers. It also goes well with salads, sandwiches, and pasta dishes. For a complete indulgence, pair it with a glass of red wine.

6) Quality Assurance:
Don't just take our word for it, Cornish Blue has won numerous awards, including being crowned Champion Cheese at the World Cheese Awards in 2010 and runner-up in 2011. It has also been featured in the prestigious Tesco Cheese Challenge. Rest assured, every bite of Cornish Blue is a testament to its quality and taste.
