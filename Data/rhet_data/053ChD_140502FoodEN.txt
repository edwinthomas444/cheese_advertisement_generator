

1) Description of Cheese:
Duddleswell is a premium cheese made by High Weald Dairy in Sussex. This cheese has a rich history and is made in the beautiful countryside of the High Weald region.

2) Description of Product:
Duddleswell is a full fat hard cheese made from pasteurised sheep milk. It has a well-rounded shape and a smooth, creamy texture. Each batch is carefully aged for up to five months and is available in both standard and organic formats, as well as oak smoked versions.

3) Description of Process:
The cheese making process of Duddleswell involves using high-quality sheep milk and carefully controlled conditions for aging. The cheese is turned daily for up to five months, ensuring the perfect balance of flavors and textures. Each batch is graded by the cheese maker before being ready for sale.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Duddleswell has a mild acidic taste with notes of sweet and buttery flavors. The ewes milk character adds a unique touch to the cheese, making it a well-rounded and rich tasting experience. The cheese also has a delicious nutty flavor and a smooth, creamy texture.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best flavor experience, we recommend serving Duddleswell at room temperature. This allows the cheese to fully develop its flavors and textures. It can be presented on a cheese board or used in various dishes. Pair it with fruits, nuts, and crackers for a delicious snack, or use it in cooking to add a distinct flavor to your dishes. For a perfect wine pairing, we suggest a full-bodied red wine such as a Cabernet Sauvignon or a Chardonnay.
