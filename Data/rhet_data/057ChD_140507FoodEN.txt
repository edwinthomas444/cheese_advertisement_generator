

1) Description of Cheese: High Weald Dairy's Halloumi, made in Sussex, is a traditional Mediterranean cheese originally crafted from sheep milk.

2) Description of Product: This salty, brined cheese comes in a variety of shapes and sizes, with a distinct "squeaky" texture when eaten raw. Made from 100% sheep milk and using traditional rennet, it can be enjoyed as a meat substitute in cooking or on its own.

3) Description of Process: High Weald Dairy's Halloumi is carefully prepared and aged to perfection, resulting in a unique texture and taste. Dry frying or grilling transforms it into a crispy, bacon-like treat.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Upon opening, the smell of High Weald Dairy's Halloumi is reminiscent of a salty sea breeze. Once cooked, it takes on a deliciously crispy exterior and a soft, savory center, with a taste that will leave you wanting more.
