
1) Description of Cheese: High Weald Dairy's Sheep's Milk Ricotta from Horsted Keynes, Sussex, UK. This award-winning cheese has a rich history and is made with care and expertise in the heart of the UK.

2) Description of Product: This Sheep's Milk Ricotta is a soft, fresh cheese with a grainy texture and creamy white appearance. It is made from 100% sheep's milk and uses traditional rennet for a truly authentic taste. Each batch is carefully crafted and weighs approximately 250g.

3) Description of Process: This Ricotta is made using the whey leftover from traditional cheese production. The whey is heated and re-cooked, resulting in a fine curd. The cheese is then carefully prepared and packaged to ensure maximum freshness and flavor.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The Sheep's Milk Ricotta has a subtle, yet delightful aroma and a gentle, slightly sweet flavor. The creamy texture and rich taste make it a perfect addition to any dish.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best taste experience, serve this Ricotta at room temperature. It can be presented as a spread on crackers or bread, or used in a variety of dishes such as pasta, salads, and desserts. Pair it with a light and fruity white wine for a perfect match.
