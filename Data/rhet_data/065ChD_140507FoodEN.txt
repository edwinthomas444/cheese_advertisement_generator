2) Description of Product: Sage Derby is a round-shaped cheese, typically weighing around 2-3 pounds. It has a crumbly texture and is coated with a layer of freshly milled and salted Derby curd, with a sprinkling of fresh sage. The cheese is made from high-quality milk and uses rennet in its production.

3) Description of Process: The process of making Sage Derby involves filling a cheese mould with freshly milled and salted Derby curd, adding a layer of fresh sage, and then topping it with another layer of milled, salted curd. The cheese is then pressed and aged for up to 6 months, giving it a distinct flavor and texture.
