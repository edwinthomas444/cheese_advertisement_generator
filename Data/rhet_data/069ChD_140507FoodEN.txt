

1) Description of Cheese:
Sussex Charmer is a premium cheese produced by Bookham Harrison Foods, a renowned dairy company located in Twineham Grange farm in Sussex. With a rich history dating back to the early 1980s, this cheese is a true representation of the local culture and traditions.

2) Description of Product:
This hard-pressed cheese is a cross between Cheddar and Parmesan, giving it a unique and irresistible flavor. It is available in various sizes and weights, making it suitable for every occasion. Made with locally produced raw milk and genuine Italian cheesemaking equipment, this cheese is a testament to the company's commitment to quality. Rennet used in the production process is also of the highest quality, ensuring a delicious and authentic taste.

3) Description of Process:
The milk, delivered warm to the farm, is carefully selected and used to create the perfect blend of flavors. The cheese is then aged for up to 1 year, using traditional methods. This allows the flavors to develop and mature, resulting in a rich and complex taste that is unmatched.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Sussex Charmer has a distinct aroma that is both inviting and tantalizing. Its flavor profile is a perfect balance between the sharpness of Cheddar and the nuttiness of Parmesan. The texture is firm and slightly crumbly, making it a delight to eat.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Sussex Charmer at room temperature to allow the flavors to fully develop. It can be presented on a cheese board, paired with crackers, or used in cooking to elevate any dish. This versatile cheese pairs well with a variety of foods, including fruits, nuts, and cured meats. For a complete sensory experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine.
