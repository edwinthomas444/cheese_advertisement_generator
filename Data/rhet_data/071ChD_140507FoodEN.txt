
1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Village Green, a delicious cheddar-style cheese made by Milk Link Cheese, now part of ARLA Foods. This cheese is crafted at their Reece's Creamery in the picturesque village of Malpas, located in the heart of Cheshire, known for its rich dairy heritage.

2) Description of Product:
Village Green is a unique cheese, made from the finest goat's milk. Its distinctive green wax coating sets it apart from other cheeses. Available in various ages ranging from 3 to 18 months, this cheese boasts a brilliant white color and a sweet nutty flavor that intensifies with age. Each cheese is carefully crafted and weighs between 3 to 5 pounds.

3) Description of Process:
At Reece's Creamery, Village Green is prepared with precision and aged to perfection. The milk is carefully sourced from local goat farms and combined with traditional rennet to create a creamy and flavorful cheese. The aging process takes place in a controlled environment, ensuring the cheese develops its unique taste and texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Village Green has a delightful aroma that is both tangy and earthy. Its flavor is a perfect balance of sweetness and nuttiness, with a hint of sharpness. As the cheese ages, the flavors become more pronounced, making it a true delight for cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Village Green at room temperature to fully appreciate its flavors and texture. This cheese makes a stunning addition to any cheese board, and its green wax coating adds a pop of color. Pair it with crackers or crusty bread, and for a more indulgent experience, try it with honey or fig jam. For a complete sensory experience, pair it with a crisp white wine or a fruity red.

It has also undergone rigorous quality tests to ensure it meets the highest standards. As one satisfied customer puts it, "If you thought you didn't like goat's cheese, Village Green will change your mind!" Try it for yourself and taste the difference.
