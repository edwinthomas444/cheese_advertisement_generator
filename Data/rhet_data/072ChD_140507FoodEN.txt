
1) Description of Cheese: West Country Goats Cheese by Cornish Country Larder is a delicious and unique cheese with a rich history and geographical provenance. The cheese is made in the Somerset creamery at North Braden, following a similar recipe to the popular Village Green cheese.

2) Description of Product: This younger cheese is devoid of the green wax coat seen on Village Green, and instead boasts a brilliant white color. It is characterized by its sweet and nutty notes, and is made from high-quality British goats' milk. The cheese is crafted using traditional methods and rennet, resulting in a truly authentic and flavorful product.

3) Description of Process: The West Country Goats Cheese is carefully prepared and aged to perfection. The skilled cheesemakers at Cornish Country Larder use their expertise to create a cheese with a unique texture and flavor profile. The aging process allows the cheese to develop its distinct taste and aroma.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The West Country Goats Cheese has a delightful aroma, with hints of sweetness and nuttiness. Its taste is equally impressive, with a perfect balance of tanginess and creaminess. The cheese has a smooth and slightly crumbly texture, making it a delight to eat.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of West Country Goats Cheese, we recommend serving it at room temperature. It pairs perfectly with a variety of foods, such as crackers, fruits, and cured meats. For a complete experience, we suggest pairing it with a crisp white wine, such as Sauvignon Blanc.
