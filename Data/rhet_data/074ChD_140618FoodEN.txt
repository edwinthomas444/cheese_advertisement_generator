

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Brue Valley Vintage Cheddar, a delicious cheese crafted by the Clapp brothers on Brue Valley Farm in the heart of Somerset, England. This cheese has a rich history and geographical provenance, hailing from the famous Somerset levels.

2) Description of Product:
This full-bodied and smooth cheddar has a rich, lasting flavor that sets it apart from other cheeses. It is carefully crafted in a traditional shape, coated with a protective wax, and weighs in at a perfect size for easy slicing. Made with the finest quality milk and rennet, Brue Valley Vintage Cheddar is a true artisanal cheese.

3) Description of Process:
The Clapp brothers take great care in preparing and aging their cheddar. They use a unique process that involves playing classical music to their herd of Friesians and providing them with waterbeds to sleep on. This attention to detail results in a cheese that is truly one of a kind.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Upon opening the wax coating, you will be greeted with a tantalizing aroma that will awaken your senses. The taste is full-bodied and smooth, with a rich and lasting flavor that will leave you wanting more. Brue Valley Vintage Cheddar is truly a cheese lover's dream.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving this cheese at room temperature to allow the flavors to fully develop. To impress your guests, present this cheese on a beautiful cheese board with crackers, fruit, and nuts. It pairs perfectly with a glass of red wine, making it the perfect addition to any dinner party.

6) Quality Assurance:
Don't just take our word for it, Brue Valley Vintage Cheddar has been recognized as a gold medal winner at the World Cheese Awards. The Clapp brothers' dedication to quality is evident in every bite. As one satisfied customer put it, "This cheese is simply perfection." Rest assured, you are getting the best of the best with Brue Valley Vintage Cheddar.
