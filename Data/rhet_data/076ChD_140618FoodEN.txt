

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Keen's Unpasteurised Extra Mature Cheddar, a true farmhouse cheddar crafted by the Keen family on their East Somerset farm. With a rich history and geographical provenance, this cheese is a testament to the quality and tradition of British cheese-making.

2) Description of Product:
This complex farmhouse cheddar boasts a full-bodied flavor and a lingering finish. It is available in a variety of shapes and sizes, with a firm and crumbly texture and a natural rind coating. Made from unpasteurized cow's milk and using traditional rennet, this cheese is a true artisanal product.

3) Description of Process:
Crafted with care and expertise, Keen's Unpasteurised Extra Mature Cheddar is prepared using traditional methods and aged to perfection. The cheese is carefully turned and monitored throughout the aging process, resulting in a rich and complex flavor profile.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of Keen's Unpasteurised Extra Mature Cheddar is a delightful mix of earthy and nutty notes. On the palate, the cheese offers a bold and robust flavor, with a perfect balance of tanginess and creaminess. The lingering finish is a testament to the quality and craftsmanship of this cheese.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving this cheese at room temperature to fully appreciate its rich and complex flavors. The cheese can be presented on a cheese board, paired with crackers or bread, and complemented with fruits or chutneys. For a truly indulgent experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine or a crisp white wine.

6) Quality Assurance:
Keen's Unpasteurised Extra Mature Cheddar has been recognized as the Best Mature Traditional Cheddar in the World in 2005, solidifying its reputation as a top-quality cheese. Our commitment to quality is reflected in every step of the cheese-making process, ensuring that each bite of this cheese is a true delight. As one satisfied customer puts it, "This is the best cheddar I have ever tasted."
