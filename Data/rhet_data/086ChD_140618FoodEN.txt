

1) Description of Cheese: Real Yorkshire Wensleydale Cheese is a product of the renowned Wensleydale Creamery, located in the heart of Yorkshire, England. This cheese has a rich history dating back to the 12th century and is made using traditional methods passed down for generations.

2) Description of Product: The Real Yorkshire Wensleydale Cheese Half Truckle is a 2.5kg wheel of creamy, white cheese. It has a mild, fresh, and clean flavor with a subtle honeyed aftertaste. The cheese has a crumbly and flaky texture, making it perfect for slicing or crumbling. It is made from locally sourced cow's milk and uses vegetarian rennet.

3) Description of Process: The Real Yorkshire Wensleydale Cheese is carefully crafted using the finest quality milk and traditional cheese-making techniques. It is aged for a minimum of 3 months, allowing the flavors to develop and mature, resulting in a deliciously creamy cheese with a unique character.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The Real Yorkshire Wensleydale Cheese has a delicate and pleasant aroma, with hints of fresh grass and herbs. Its taste is smooth and creamy, with a subtle sweetness and tanginess that lingers on the palate.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best flavor experience, serve the Real Yorkshire Wensleydale Cheese at room temperature. It can be presented as a centerpiece on a cheese board, sliced or crumbled onto crackers or bread. This versatile cheese pairs well with fruits, nuts, and honey, and is also a perfect accompaniment to a glass of crisp white wine.
