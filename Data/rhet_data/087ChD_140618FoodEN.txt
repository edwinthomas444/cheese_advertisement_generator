
1) Real Yorkshire Wensleydale & Cranberries: A Delicious Creation from The Wensleydale Creamery
This delectable cheese is the result of years of expertise and tradition from The Wensleydale Creamery, located in the heart of Yorkshire, England. With a rich history dating back to the 12th century, this cheese is a true representation of the region's dairy heritage.

2) Creamy White Cheese with a Burst of Fruity Flavor
Each 1.25kg wheel of Real Yorkshire Wensleydale & Cranberries boasts a creamy white color and a smooth, crumbly texture. The cheese is carefully combined with pure, sweet cranberries, giving it a delicate fruity succulence. Made with high-quality cow's milk and vegetarian rennet, this cheese is a perfect choice for vegetarians.

3) Crafted with Care and Aged to Perfection
The Wensleydale Creamery takes great pride in the preparation and aging process of their cheese. Each wheel is handcrafted using traditional methods, ensuring the highest quality and flavor. The cheese is then aged for a minimum of 3 months, allowing the flavors to develop and mature.

4) Aroma and Taste that Will Leave You Wanting More
Real Yorkshire Wensleydale & Cranberries has a distinct aroma that is both sweet and tangy. The taste is a perfect balance of creamy, tangy cheese and the burst of fruity sweetness from the cranberries. It's a unique and irresistible flavor that will keep you coming back for more.

5) Perfect for Any Occasion

It makes a stunning addition to any cheese board, and pairs perfectly with crackers, crusty bread, and fresh fruits. For a complete taste sensation, pair it with a crisp white wine or a fruity red.
