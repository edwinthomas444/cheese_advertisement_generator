

1) Description of Cheese:
Indulge in the creamy and unique flavor of Real Yorkshire Wensleydale & Ginger cheese, crafted by skilled artisans in the heart of Yorkshire, England. This cheese has a rich history dating back to the 12th century and is made exclusively from locally sourced cow's milk.

2) Description of Product:
Each block of Real Yorkshire Wensleydale & Ginger cheese weighs 1.25kg and is delicately coated with a layer of stem ginger, adding a delightful oriental twist to the traditional Wensleydale cheese. The cheese has a crumbly yet creamy texture, making it perfect for slicing or crumbling. It is made with vegetarian rennet, ensuring it is suitable for all dietary preferences.

3) Description of Process:
Real Yorkshire Wensleydale & Ginger cheese is carefully crafted using traditional methods, with each batch aged for a minimum of 3 months. This allows the cheese to develop its distinct flavor and texture, resulting in a truly unique and delicious product.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of Real Yorkshire Wensleydale & Ginger cheese is a perfect blend of creamy and spicy notes, thanks to the addition of stem ginger. The taste is equally delightful, with the creamy Wensleydale cheese perfectly complemented by the subtle heat of the ginger.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the ultimate taste experience, serve Real Yorkshire Wensleydale & Ginger cheese at room temperature to fully appreciate its flavors. This cheese is perfect for a cheese platter, served with crackers and fruits. It also pairs well with spicy dishes, adding a creamy and spicy kick. For wine pairing, we recommend a crisp white wine such as Sauvignon Blanc or a light red like Pinot Noir.

6) Quality Assurance:
Don't just take our word for it, Real Yorkshire Wensleydale & Ginger cheese has received numerous awards and accolades, including the prestigious Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) status. It has also been praised by cheese connoisseurs for its unique flavor profile and high quality. Try it for yourself and taste the excellence of Real Yorkshire Wensleydale & Ginger cheese.
