

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Garsdale Yorkshire Wensleydale with Garlic & Chives, a delectable creation from the renowned cheese manufacturer in Yorkshire, England. This cheese has a rich history dating back to the 12th century and is named after the picturesque valley of Wensleydale. 

2) Description of Product:
This cheese is a 1.25kg wheel of creamy, white Yorkshire Wensleydale, infused with freshly ground garlic and a hint of chopped green chives. It has a smooth and crumbly texture, with a thin, edible coating. Made with high-quality cow's milk and traditional rennet, this cheese is a true testament to the craft of cheese-making. 

3) Description of Process:
Our cheese is made using a traditional recipe and aged for several months to achieve the perfect balance of flavors. The garlic and chives are carefully blended into the cheese, creating a harmonious and subtle taste that is sure to tantalize your taste buds. 

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of this cheese is a delightful blend of creamy cheese and the freshness of garlic and chives. Upon tasting, you will experience a burst of flavors, starting with the creaminess of the cheese, followed by the zing of garlic and the subtle hint of chives. 

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving this cheese at room temperature to allow the flavors to fully develop. Present it on a cheese board with crackers, grapes, and figs for a stunning presentation. This cheese pairs perfectly with a crisp white wine or a fruity red. For a more indulgent experience, try it melted on a slice of warm bread.
