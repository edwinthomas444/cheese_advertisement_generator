

1) Description of Cheese - Grandma Singleton's Famously Strong Lancashire Cheese is a renowned product from the heart of Lancashire, made by the esteemed cheese makers at Grandma Singletons. With a rich history and geographical provenance, this cheese is a true representation of the region's culinary heritage.

2) Description of Product - This cheese boasts an intensely strong dark flavour, with a smooth and buttery texture that melts in your mouth. It is matured for up to a year, ensuring that it is at its peak of flavour. The cheese comes in a traditional round shape, with a weight of 250g. It is made from locally sourced milk from farms no more than 12 miles away, and uses traditional rennet for its production.

3) Description of Process - The cheese making process at Grandma Singletons is a time-honoured tradition, passed down through generations. Each cheese is hand-crafted with the utmost care and attention to detail. The cheese is prepared using traditional methods and aged for up to a year, allowing the flavours to develop and intensify.

4) Description of Smell and Taste - The aroma of Grandma Singleton's Famously Strong Lancashire Cheese is rich and inviting, with hints of earthy and nutty notes. The taste is bold and robust, with a deep and complex flavour profile. The cheese has a smooth and creamy texture, making it a delight to eat.

5) Serving Suggestions - To truly appreciate the flavours of this cheese, it is best served at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board, accompanied by crackers and a selection of full-flavoured pickles. For a traditional pairing, enjoy it with a pint of Lancashire bitter or a full-bodied glass of red wine. It also makes a perfect addition to a ploughman's lunch or as a topping for a pork pie.

6) Quality Assurance - At Grandma Singletons, quality is of the utmost importance. Our cheese is made with locally sourced milk, ensuring freshness and supporting local farmers. Our commitment to quality has been recognized with numerous awards and accolades, including quotes from satisfied customers and positive results from quality tests. You can trust that each bite of Grandma Singleton's Famously Strong Lancashire Cheese will be a delicious and high-quality experience.
