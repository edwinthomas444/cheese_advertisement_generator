

1) Description of Cheese: Grandma Singletons' Parlick Fell Pure Sheeps' Milk Cheese is an award-winning cheese that is hand-crafted using locally sourced milk from farms within a 12-mile radius. This cheese is named after Parlick Fell, a beautiful hill in Lancashire, England, where the sheep graze and produce the milk for this delicious cheese.

2) Description of Product: This cheese is a fresh and piquant ewes milk cheese with a salty first note, followed by a light, clean flavor. It has a unique character that sets it apart from other cheeses. The cheese is available in a variety of shapes and sizes, and is coated in a chorizo crust for added depth of flavor. It is made with only the finest ingredients, including pure sheeps' milk and traditional rennet.

3) Description of Process: Grandma Singletons' Parlick Fell cheese is made using time-honored traditional methods. The milk is carefully sourced from local farms and then hand-crafted into cheese. The cheese is then aged to perfection, allowing the flavors to develop and mature.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: This cheese has a distinct aroma that is both fresh and savory. The first bite reveals a salty note, followed by a light and clean flavor that lingers on the palate. The texture is smooth and creamy, making it a delight to eat.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of this cheese, it is best served at room temperature. It can be cubed and added to a tomato and onion salad with a splash of rich balsamic vinegar, or paired with fresh juicy grapes and crispy apples. It also makes a great addition to a warm salad. For a complete dining experience, pair this cheese with a crisp white wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Grandma Singletons' Parlick Fell cheese has won numerous awards, including the prestigious 'Prince of Wales Award for Speciality Cheese' at the Great Yorkshire Show. This is a testament to the quality and taste of this cheese. Every cheese is hand-crafted to the highest quality standards, ensuring that each bite is a true delight. As Grandma always says, "Only the best for our customers."
