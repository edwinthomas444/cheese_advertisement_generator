

1) Description of Cheese:
Experience the unique taste of ﻿Beenleigh Blue, a modern, farmhouse cheese made from sheep's milk. Crafted by the renowned Ticklemore Cheese Company and inspired by the Roquefort recipe of founder Robin Congden, this cheese hails from the historic region of Devon in England.

2) Description of Product:
This unpasteurized, organic, vegetarian cheese comes in a cylindrical shape with a rough, crusty, natural rind. Its slightly sticky surface is adorned with patches of blue, gray, and white molds, giving it a distinct appearance. Weighing in at 45-50% fat content, this cheese has a moist yet crumbly texture and is made from the milk of Dorset-Friesland cross sheep from the farm of Terry Perkins in Cornwall.

3) Description of Process:
﻿Beenleigh Blue is carefully crafted between January and July, using traditional techniques and the finest ingredients. The cheese is aged for six months, allowing it to develop its bold flavor and unique characteristics.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Indulge in the steely blue flavor of ﻿Beenleigh Blue, with hints of burnt caramel sweetness that is characteristic of fine sheep's milk. As it melts on your palate, it reveals its strong and spicy character, making it a true delight for the senses.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve ﻿Beenleigh Blue at room temperature to fully appreciate its flavors and textures. Its beautiful ivory color and blue-green streaks make for an eye-catching presentation on any cheese board. Pair it with Mead, Port, Cabernet Merlot, or Sweet Cider for a perfect balance of flavors.

6) Quality Assurance:
Don't just take our word for it - ﻿Beenleigh Blue has received numerous accolades and awards, making it a highly sought-after cheese. With a fat content of 45-50%, it meets the highest quality standards and is loved by cheese connoisseurs around the world.
