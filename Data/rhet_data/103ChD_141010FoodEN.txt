

1) Description of Cheese: Keltic Gold by Sue Proudfoot
Keltic Gold is a distinctive cheese, handcrafted by renowned cheese maker Sue Proudfoot. This cheese is rooted in the rich history and geographical landscape of the United Kingdom.

2) Description of Product: Characteristics and Ingredients
Keltic Gold is a semi-soft cheese with a supple texture, weighing approximately 200 grams. It is coated with local cider, giving it a unique flavor. Made from pasteurized cow's milk and using traditional rennet, Keltic Gold is a vegetarian-friendly cheese.

3) Description of Process: Preparation and Aging
Sue Proudfoot takes great care in preparing Keltic Gold, using different starter cultures from her famous cheese, Miss Muffet. The cheese is then aged to perfection, resulting in a sweet and flavorful taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste
The aroma of Keltic Gold is inviting, with hints of sweetness and earthiness. On the palate, it is a perfect balance of creamy and tangy, reminiscent of the best Dutch Edams and Goudas.

5) Serving Suggestions: Temperature, Presentation, Food and Wine Pairing
For the best experience, serve Keltic Gold at room temperature. Its distinctive flavor and texture make it a versatile cheese, perfect for any occasion. It pairs well with fruits, crackers, and artisan bread. For a complete culinary experience, pair it with a crisp white wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Quotations, Awards, and Quality Tests
Renowned cheese expert Michael Raffael describes Keltic Gold as having "a springiness and cleanness on the palate." This cheese has also won numerous awards, including the prestigious "Best Washed Rind Cheese" at the International Cheese Awards. It has also passed rigorous quality tests, ensuring the highest standards of taste and quality.
