

1) Description of Cheese: Sharpham Savour by Sharpham Cheese, produced in Devon, England. This new cheese is a combination of Sharpham's own Jersey cow milk and goats' milk, creating a unique and exciting flavor.

2) Description of Product: Sharpham Savour is a washed curd cheese, following the method typically used for Dutch cheeses. It has a round shape and a weight of approximately 200g. The texture is smooth and creamy, with a thin coating on the outside. The cheese is made with vegetarian, unpasteurised milk and rennet.

3) Description of Process: Sharpham Savour is made using a traditional process of washing the curd, resulting in a rich and complex flavor. The cheese is then aged to perfection, allowing the flavors to develop and mature.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Sharpham Savour is enticing, with hints of nuttiness and a subtle tanginess. The taste is equally delightful, with a creamy and buttery texture, balanced with a slight sharpness from the goats' milk. It has a long and lingering finish, leaving a pleasant aftertaste.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of Sharpham Savour, it is best served at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers and fruits, or melted on a warm baguette. This cheese pairs well with a variety of foods, such as figs, honey, and cured meats. For a perfect combination, enjoy with a glass of crisp white wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Sharpham Cheese has a reputation for producing exceptional quality cheeses, and Sharpham Savour is no exception. It has received high praise from cheese experts and has won several awards, including the prestigious Gold at the World Cheese Awards.
