

1) Description of Cheese: Colston Bassett Baby Stilton, produced by the Colston Bassett Dairy in Nottinghamshire, is a hand-ladled Stilton with a rich history dating back to 1913.

2) Description of Product: This intensely creamy and rich Stilton is made from pasteurised cows' milk and uses vegetarian rennet. It has a deep, lingering, and complex flavour, and is available in a variety of sizes and weights.

3) Description of the Process: The cheese is hand-ladled and made using traditional methods, with little change since its inception. It is aged to perfection, resulting in a creamy and rich texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Colston Bassett Baby Stilton has a distinct minerally tang and a slightly sweet taste that dissipates on the tongue. Its rich and creamy texture is complemented by its deep, complex flavour.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Colston Bassett Baby Stilton at room temperature and present it on a cheese board with crackers, fruits, and nuts. It pairs perfectly with a variety of wines, such as port or sherry, and can also be used in cooking to elevate any dish.

6) Quality Assurance: Colston Bassett Baby Stilton has been awarded numerous accolades, including being the only hand-ladled Stilton and using vegetarian rennet. Its quality is guaranteed by the co-operative of local dairy farmers who have been supplying milk to the dairy for over a century. As head cheese-maker Billy Kevan says, "little has altered at Colston Bassett since they started in 1913."
