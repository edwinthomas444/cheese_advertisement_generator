

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Cropwell Bishop Stilton, a rich and creamy blue cheese with a strong finish. This cheese is made by the Skailes Family in the British Isles, using a traditional recipe from the old Melton Mowbray North Road Creamery.

2) Description of Product:
Cropwell Bishop Stilton comes in three sizes: Half Moon (1.35kg), Half Whole (4kg), and Whole (8kg). It has a distinct half-moon shape and is coated with a caramel, buttery, and complex texture. The cheese is made from pasteurised cows' milk and uses animal rennet, giving it a unique and artisanal flavour.

3) Description of Process:
This special Stilton is hand-ladled, following a traditional recipe and using animal rennet. It is aged for a minimum of 30 days, allowing the flavours to develop and mature. Only 10-15% of Cropwell Bishop's Stilton production follows this artisanal process.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Cropwell Bishop Stilton has a rich and creamy aroma, with a slight spice and long lingering notes. The taste is buttery and complex, with a strong blue finish. This cheese is truly a treat for the senses.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Cropwell Bishop Stilton at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board, paired with crackers and fruit. This cheese pairs well with a variety of foods, including salads, burgers, and pasta dishes. For a complete experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine.

6) Quality Assurance:
Cropwell Bishop Stilton has received numerous awards and accolades, including the prestigious World Cheese Awards. The Skailes Family takes great pride in their cheese-making, using only the best ingredients and following a traditional process. As one of their staff members, Mario, has been hand-ladling the curd for over 40 years, you can trust that this cheese is of the highest quality.
