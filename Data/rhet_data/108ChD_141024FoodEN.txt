

1) Description of Cheese:
Tunworth, a supreme brie-style cheese, is proudly manufactured in the British Isles by Stacey Hedges. This award-winning cheese has a rich history, having been crowned Supreme Champion at the British Cheese Awards in 2006.

2) Description of Product:
Tunworth is a pasteurised, soft cheese with a delicate rind and a fruity tang. Its undulating surface and thin coating make it visually appealing. Made from the finest cow's milk and using traditional rennet, this cheese is a true testament to British cheese-making.

3) Description of Process:
Each batch of Tunworth is carefully prepared and aged by Stacey Hedges, who has been perfecting her craft since 2004. The cheese is aged to perfection, resulting in a creamy and smooth texture that is sure to delight your taste buds.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Tunworth boasts a unique aroma that is both earthy and tangy. Its flavor profile is complex, with a perfect balance of fruity and savory notes. One bite of this cheese will transport you to the rolling hills of the British countryside.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best taste experience, serve Tunworth at room temperature. Its elegant presentation makes it a perfect addition to any cheese board. Pair it with fresh fruits and crackers for a delightful snack, or elevate your meal by using it as a topping for salads or sandwiches. For a truly indulgent experience, pair Tunworth with a crisp white wine.

6) Quality Assurance:
Tunworth has received numerous accolades, including being named Supreme Champion at the British Cheese Awards. Its quality has been praised by cheese experts and customers alike, with one Frenchman even mistaking it for a cheese made in his own terroir. Try Tunworth for yourself and taste the difference in quality and craftsmanship.
