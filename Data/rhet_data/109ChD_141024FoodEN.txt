
1) Description of Cheese: Introducing Lincolnshire Red, a new addition to our range from the renowned manufacturer, Lincolnshire Poacher. This cheese has its roots in an old-fashioned Red Leicester recipe, with a modern twist. It is named after its place of origin, Lincolnshire, a county in the East Midlands of England.

2) Description of Product: Lincolnshire Red is a cheese with a unique shape and size, perfect for any cheese platter or sandwich. Its moist and creamy texture is complemented by a delicate buttery flavor. The cheese is coated with a vibrant red color, achieved by adding annatto to the milk during the cheese-making process. This cheese is made using vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for all dietary preferences.

3) Description of Process: Our Lincolnshire Red is carefully prepared and aged for about 6 months, giving it a distinctive taste and texture. The cheese is made using traditional methods, with the addition of annatto to give it its signature color. The aging process allows the flavors to develop, resulting in a cheese that is full of character and depth.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Upon opening the packaging, you will be greeted with a rich and inviting aroma. The taste of Lincolnshire Red is a perfect balance of creamy and buttery notes, with a clean finish that lingers on your palate. Its unique flavor profile makes it stand out from other cheeses in its category.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, we recommend serving Lincolnshire Red at room temperature. Its vibrant red color makes it a beautiful addition to any cheese platter, and it pairs perfectly with crackers, fruits, and nuts. For a more indulgent experience, try it melted on a grilled cheese sandwich. This cheese also pairs well with a full-bodied red wine, such as a Cabernet Sauvignon.
