

1) Description of Cheese: Ashdown Foresters Organic cheese is a delicious creation from the Ashdown Forest at Duddleswell, East Sussex. It is lovingly made by local producers, using only the finest organic pasteurized cow's milk. This cheese has a rich history and a strong connection to its geographical roots.

2) Description of Product: This full fat, semi-hard pressed cheese boasts a pale creamy color and a natural honey colored rind. It is available in a variety of sizes and weights, making it a versatile choice for any occasion. The texture is smooth and velvety, with a slight firmness that adds to its unique character. The cheese is coated in a delicate layer of natural rind, adding to its overall appeal.

The cheese is aged to perfection, allowing it to develop its full texture and flavor. Each batch is made with precision and care, ensuring a consistent and high-quality product.
