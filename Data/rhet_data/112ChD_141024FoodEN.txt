

1) Description of Cheese: Ashmore Farmhouse Cheese is a locally produced hard cheese made in Ashmore, Wiltshire. It has a rich and creamy taste and was awarded a bronze medal at the British Cheese Awards in 2001.

2) Description of Product: This hand-made cheese has a smooth texture similar to Cheddar. It is available in a variety of sizes and weights and is made from unpasteurized cow's milk. The cheese is suitable for vegetarians and is coated with a natural rind.

3) Description of Process: Ashmore Farmhouse Cheese is carefully prepared using traditional methods and aged to perfection. The cheese is made from high-quality milk and rennet, resulting in a delicious and flavorful product.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The cheese has a distinct aroma and a rich, creamy taste. Its smooth texture melts in your mouth, leaving a satisfying and lingering flavor.

5) Serving Suggestions: For best taste, it is recommended to serve Ashmore Farmhouse Cheese at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheeseboard or grated and added to salads. It pairs well with wholemeal bread and tomato relish, baked potatoes, or slices of eating apple. For a complete experience, pair it with a glass of your favorite wine.
