

1) Description of Cheese: Berkswell is a premium, award-winning cheese hand-made at Ram Hall in the quaint village of Berkswell, Warwickshire. This hard ewe's milk cheese has a rich history, winning a gold medal at the British Cheese Awards in 2001. It is a true testament to the quality and expertise of British cheese-making.

2) Description of Product: Berkswell is a cheese with character. Its unique shape and size, along with its crumbly texture and buttery coating, make it stand out among other cheeses. Made from unpasteurised milk and rennet, this cheese is suitable for vegetarians and has a weight that is perfect for sharing with friends and family.

3) Description of Process: Berkswell is made using a traditional Caerphilly cheese recipe, giving it a distinct and authentic taste. It is carefully prepared by hand at Ram Hall and then aged to perfection, allowing it to develop its full flavour and texture. This process ensures that every bite of Berkswell is a delight for the senses.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Berkswell is enticing, with hints of nuttiness and a subtle sweetness. Its full flavour is complemented by a buttery lingering taste, making it a cheese that is truly unforgettable. Similar to a mature Pecorino or Manchego, Berkswell is a cheese that will leave you wanting more.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Berkswell at room temperature. This allows the cheese to fully develop its texture and flavour. It can be presented on a cheeseboard as the star attraction or grated and added to soups, sauces, or pasta dishes for a burst of flavour. Pair it with a full-bodied red wine or a crisp white for a perfect match.

6) Quality Assurance: Don't just take our word for it, Berkswell has received numerous accolades, including a gold medal at the British Cheese Awards. It has also been praised by cheese experts and customers alike, with its rich flavour and quality ingredients. You can trust that each bite of Berkswell is a taste of excellence.
