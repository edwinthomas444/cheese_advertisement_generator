

1) Description of Cheese: Introducing Gorwydd Caerphilly, a deliciously unique cheese crafted by Todd Trethowen near Llanddewi Brefi, Tregaron in Dyfed. This cheese has a rich history, originating from the Caerphilly region and has been enjoyed by cheese lovers for generations.

2) Description of Product: Gorwydd Caerphilly is a clean and fresh sweet cheese with a creamy, crumbly texture. Its distinctive grey moulded rind is adorned with vivid yellow or orange splodges, making it a visually appealing addition to any cheeseboard. Made from unpasteurised Teifi valley milk, this cheese is suitable for vegetarians and weighs approximately 2 pounds.

3) Description of Process: This delectable cheese is carefully prepared by hand and left to mature for 2 months, allowing it to develop a lasting flavor. The aging process gives Gorwydd Caerphilly its unique taste and texture, making it a must-try for any cheese connoisseur.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Upon opening the package, you will be greeted with a pleasant aroma that is both earthy and tangy. The taste of Gorwydd Caerphilly is a perfect balance of sweet and savory, with a hint of nuttiness that will leave your taste buds craving more.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, we recommend serving Gorwydd Caerphilly at room temperature. To fully appreciate its texture and flavor, remove the cheese from the fridge an hour before serving. This cheese is perfect on its own, but can also be used in cooking to create mouth-watering dishes such as Glamorgan sausages, Welsh Rarebit, or a cheese and leek sauce to accompany chicken or cod. Pair it with a crisp white wine for a truly indulgent experience.

6) Quality Assurance: Don't just take our word for it, Gorwydd Caerphilly has been recognized for its exceptional quality. It won a gold medal at the 2001 British Cheese Awards for the Best Territorial Cheese, solidifying its place as a top choice for cheese lovers. Our cheese also undergoes rigorous quality tests to ensure that each bite is as delicious as the last. As Todd Trethowen himself says, "Our cheese is a labor of love and we are proud to share it with you."
