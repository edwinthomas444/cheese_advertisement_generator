

1) Description of Cheese: Davidstow Cheddar, made by a traditional recipe in north Cornwall for over 50 years, is a premium cheese with a rich history and geographical provenance.

2) Description of Product: This cheddar is made from pasteurised milk and is suitable for vegetarians. It comes in two varieties - Mature and Extra Mature - with a smooth, rich, and creamy full-bodied flavour. The cheese is available prepacked or from the service counter at Waitrose.

3) Description of Process: Davidstow Cheddar is specially matured and selected to ensure its high quality. The aging process gives it a unique, rich, and fuller flavour. At the 2001 British Cheese Awards, it won a silver medal, making it a top choice for cheese lovers.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Davidstow Cheddar is enticing, with a hint of sharpness and a creamy undertone. The taste is smooth and rich, with a full-bodied flavor that lingers on the palate.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Mature or Extra Mature Davidstow Cheddar in the traditional West country style - with a chunk of crusty bread and a glass of cider. For a more sophisticated presentation, create a Cornish cheeseboard and pair it with Cornish Brie and Cornish Village Green Goat's Cheese. To fully appreciate its flavor, remove the cheese from the fridge an hour before serving to allow it to come to room temperature and develop its full texture and taste. It also pairs well with a variety of wines, making it a versatile choice for any occasion.

6) Quality Assurance: Don't just take our word for it - Davidstow Cheddar has been recognized for its exceptional quality. With a silver medal from the 2001 British Cheese Awards, it has been proven to be a top contender in the cheese industry. Its smooth and creamy texture, along with its rich and full-bodied flavor, have been praised by cheese experts and consumers alike. Trust in Davidstow Cheddar for a premium cheese experience.
