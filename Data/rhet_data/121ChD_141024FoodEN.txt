

1) Description of Cheese: Denhay West Country Farmhouse Cheddar is a premium cheese made by Denhay Farm in Dorset, England. It has a rich history and is deeply rooted in the West Country, with Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) protection.

2) Description of Product: This hand-made cheese is crafted from the milk of Friesian cows at Denhay Farm. It comes in three varieties - Traditional, Extra Mature, and Mature - with a distinctive flavor and firm texture. Made from pasteurized milk, it is suitable for vegetarians and weighs approximately one year.

3) Description of the Process: Denhay Cheddar is made using traditional methods and is aged for 12-16 months. The cheese is carefully hand-made on the farm and follows strict PDO guidelines to ensure its authenticity and quality.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Denhay Cheddar boasts a full and distinctive flavor, with a rich aroma that is sure to tantalize your taste buds. Its firm texture adds to the overall sensory experience, making it a delight for cheese lovers.
