

1) Green's Farmhouse Cheddar: A Taste of Tradition from Glastonbury
Since 1920, the Green family has been crafting their signature cheddar in Glastonbury, a small town in the West Country region of England. This cheddar has a Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) status, ensuring that it is made with locally-sourced milk from one of four West Country counties: Dorset, Somerset, Devon, or Cornwall.

2) A Cheddar Like No Other: Characteristics and Ingredients
Green's Farmhouse Cheddar is a true West Country Farmhouse Cheddar, made with pasteurized cow's milk and traditional rennet. It comes in a 50lb cylinder that is cloth bound, giving it a unique shape and texture. This cheddar is available in two varieties: Organic and Traditional. Both are suitable for vegetarians and are sold at Waitrose.

3) Crafted with Care: The Process of Making Green's Cheddar
Green's Farmhouse Cheddar is made by hand on the farm, using authentic methods that have been passed down through generations. The cheese is then aged to perfection, allowing it to develop its distinct flavor and texture. The result is a full-flavored, tangy cheese with a subtle hint of onions.

4) Aroma and Taste that Will Leave You Wanting More
One bite of Green's Farmhouse Cheddar and you'll be hooked. Its strong, mature flavor and open texture make it a standout among cheddars. The aroma is equally enticing, with a pleasing hint of onions. This cheddar is a must-try for any cheese lover.

5) Perfectly Paired: Serving Suggestions for Green's Cheddar
For the ultimate cheese experience, serve Green's Cheddar at room temperature. This allows the cheese to develop its full texture and flavor. It pairs perfectly with organic biscuits or bread, or can be grated and mixed with wholegrain mustard for a delicious cheese on toast. It also makes for a tasty addition to cheese scones.

Don't just take our word for it, Green's Farmhouse Cheddar has received numerous accolades and awards for its exceptional taste and quality. It has also been praised by renowned chefs and food critics. Rest assured, each bite of Green's Cheddar is a testament to its superior quality and craftsmanship.
