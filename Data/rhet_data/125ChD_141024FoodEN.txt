

1) Description of Cheese: Keens West Country Farmhouse Cheddar is a premium cheese crafted by the Keen family at Moorhayes Farm in Wincanton, Somerset. With a rich history spanning over 100 years, this cheese is deeply rooted in the traditions and culture of the West Country.

2) Description of Product: This cheddar is made using unpasteurised milk from the farm's own herd of cows, following traditional methods. It has a moist texture, tangy bite, and full-bodied flavor. The cheese is available in various sizes and weights, coated with a protective layer to preserve its freshness.

3) Description of Process: Keens Cheddar is made by hand on the farm, ensuring the highest quality and authenticity. The cheese is aged to perfection, allowing it to develop its unique flavor and texture. Its Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) status guarantees that it is made using local milk from one of the four West Country counties.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Keens Cheddar is rich and inviting, with hints of grass and earth. Its taste is bold and complex, with a perfect balance of tanginess and creaminess. The cheese has a smooth and creamy texture, making it a delight to savor.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, it is recommended to serve Keens Cheddar at room temperature, allowing its flavors to fully develop. It can be presented on a cheeseboard with biscuits or bread, accompanied by a glass of red wine or cider. This cheese is also a perfect addition to cooking, adding a rich and flavorful touch to sauces, bakes, and pies.

6) Quality Assurance: Keens West Country Farmhouse Cheddar has been recognized for its exceptional quality, winning a bronze medal at the 2001 British Cheese Awards. Its PDO status and use of authentic methods ensure that every bite is a true representation of the West Country's finest cheese. As one satisfied customer puts it, "Keens Cheddar is a true gem of the West Country."
