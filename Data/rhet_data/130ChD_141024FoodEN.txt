

1) Description of Cheese: Introducing Devon Oke, a locally produced cheese made by Rachel Stevens at Stockbearne Farm in Devon. This creamy textured, full bodied cheese is a mature Curworthy cheese, named after the town of Okehampton where it is made. 

2) Description of Product: Devon Oke is a handmade cheese made from pasteurised cow's milk from the farm's own Friesian cows. It has a rich and creamy texture, and comes in a variety of sizes and weights. The cheese is coated with a natural rind and is made using traditional rennet.

3) Description of Process: Each batch of Devon Oke is carefully prepared by hand and aged to perfection. The cheese is made in small batches to ensure quality and consistency. It is aged for a specific amount of time to develop its unique flavor and texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Devon Oke has a distinct aroma that is both creamy and earthy. The taste is rich and full-bodied, with a hint of sweetness and a tangy finish. It is a cheese that is sure to please even the most discerning palate.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, we recommend serving Devon Oke at room temperature. It can be enjoyed on a cheeseboard or grated and used in various dishes such as sauces, salads, sandwiches, and pasta bakes. It also pairs well with a variety of wines and complements a range of foods.
