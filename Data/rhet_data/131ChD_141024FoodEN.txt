

1) Description of Cheese: Double Worcester is a premium cheese crafted by Colin and Alyson Anstey at Broomhall Farm in Worcester, England. This family-run farm has been producing high-quality cheese for four generations, ensuring a rich historical and geographical provenance.

2) Description of Product: This full fat hard cheese boasts a robust and mellow flavour, with a rich golden color. Its smooth, light texture and subtle citrus tang make it a standout among other cheeses. Double Worcester is a smaller version of the classic Double Gloucester, and is made from unpasteurized milk, making it suitable for vegetarians. Available at 55 branches of Waitrose, this cheese is the perfect addition to any cheeseboard.

3) Description of Process: Double Worcester is prepared using traditional methods and aged to perfection. The result is a cheese with a smooth, light texture and a subtle citrus tang that sets it apart from other cheeses on the market.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Double Worcester is enticing, with hints of citrus and a rich, creamy scent. Its taste is equally delightful, with a mellow and robust flavor that is sure to please any cheese lover.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully appreciate the flavor and texture of Double Worcester, it is best served at room temperature. For a cheeseboard, remove the cheese from the fridge an hour before serving. This allows it to develop its full texture and flavor. Double Worcester is also perfect for grilling or used in sauces. Pair it with crackers, bread, or fruit for a delicious snack, or enjoy it with a glass of white wine for a truly indulgent experience.

6) Quality Assurance: Double Worcester has been recognized for its exceptional quality, winning a silver medal at the British Cheese Awards in 2001. It has also undergone rigorous quality tests to ensure its premium taste and texture. As one satisfied customer puts it, "Double Worcester is the perfect balance of flavor and texture, making it a must-try for any cheese lover."
