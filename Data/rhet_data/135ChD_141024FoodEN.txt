
1) Description of Cheese: Kirkham's Lancashire - This award-winning cheese is individually hand-made by the Kirkham family at their farm in Goosnargh, Lancashire. The farm is located on the slopes of Beacon Fell, in the heart of the Lancashire region.

2) Description of Product: Kirkham's Lancashire is a unique cheese made from unpasteurized milk using a traditional three-day curd method. This results in a subtly mottled texture and a hand-rubbed rind. The cheese is part of the Select range of prepacked cheeses available at Waitrose and weighs approximately 200g.

3) Description of Process: The Kirkham family follows a traditional and time-honored process to make their Lancashire cheese. The milk is left to curdle for three days, giving it a distinct texture. The cheese is then hand-turned and rubbed to develop its unique rind. It is aged to perfection before being packaged and made available to customers.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Kirkham's Lancashire has a rich and creamy aroma, with hints of grass and butter. The taste is smooth and buttery, with a subtle tanginess that is characteristic of Lancashire cheese. The unique texture adds to the overall sensory experience of this cheese.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best taste experience, it is recommended to serve Kirkham's Lancashire at room temperature. This allows the cheese to develop its full texture and flavor. It can be presented on a cheeseboard with biscuits and celery, or used to make a deliciously creamy cheese on toast. It also pairs well with a variety of wines, such as a crisp white or a full-bodied red.

6) Quality Assurance: "This cheese is a true representation of the Lancashire region and its traditional cheese-making methods." - The Kirkham Family. Kirkham's Lancashire has won multiple awards, including the prestigious World Cheese Awards. It also undergoes rigorous quality tests to ensure that every piece of cheese is of the highest quality and meets the standards of the Kirkham family.
