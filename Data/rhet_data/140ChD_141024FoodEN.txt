

1) Description of Cheese:
Meldon, a delicious cheese made by Stockbearne Farm near Okehampton in Devon, is a type of Curworthy cheese that has been enjoyed for generations. With its rich history and geographical provenance in the West Country, Meldon is a true taste of tradition.

2) Description of Product:
Meldon is a handcrafted cheese made from pasteurised milk from the farm's own Friesian cows. It is flavoured with wholegrain mustard and Chiltern ale, giving it a unique and flavorful taste. This cheese has a creamy texture and is suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of the Process:
Meldon is made using traditional methods and is aged to perfection. The cheese is carefully prepared and coated with mustard and ale, giving it a distinct appearance. It is then aged to develop its full texture and flavor, resulting in a high-quality cheese.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Meldon has a rich and enticing aroma, with hints of mustard and ale. The taste is bold and flavorful, with a perfect balance of creaminess and tanginess. It is a true delight for the senses.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Meldon at room temperature. It pairs perfectly with crusty white bread and a glass of real ale for a simple yet satisfying lunch. It can also be grated and used to make delicious cheese on toast. For a special occasion, Meldon makes a tasty addition to a cheeseboard, along with other West Country cheeses such as Cornish Yarg, Somerset Camembert, and Dorset Blue Vinny.

6) Quality Assurance:
Meldon has received rave reviews from cheese lovers and food critics alike. It has been awarded numerous accolades, including the prestigious "Best Cheese" award at the West Country Cheese Awards. Our commitment to quality and tradition is reflected in every bite of Meldon. As our satisfied customers say, "Meldon is the best cheese in the West!"
