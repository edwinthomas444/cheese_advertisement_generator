

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Single Gloucester, a delicious and unique cheese made by Charles Martell at Laurel Farm in Dymock, Gloucestershire. This cheese has a rich history, as it is a Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) cheese, guaranteeing its authenticity and traditional production methods. 

2) Description of Product:
Single Gloucester is a light, crumbly hard cheese with a subtle buttery flavor and a hint of sweetness. It is lower in fat than the more widely available Double Gloucester and is suitable for vegetarians. This cheese is made with unpasteurized cow's milk and is available in a variety of sizes and weights, making it perfect for any occasion.

3) Description of Process:
Single Gloucester is made by hand on the farm using authenticated methods, ensuring the highest quality and taste. It is then aged to perfection, giving it a unique texture and flavor that cannot be replicated.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of Single Gloucester is delicate and inviting, with hints of butter and cream. The taste is equally delightful, with a subtle sweetness and a rich, creamy finish that will leave you wanting more.

5) Serving Suggestions:
To fully enjoy the flavors of Single Gloucester, it is best served at room temperature. This allows the cheese to develop its full texture and flavor. It can be served on a cheeseboard, in sandwiches, or grated over soups, pasta dishes, or salads. Pair it with a crisp white wine or a full-bodied red for a perfect combination.

6) Quality Assurance:
Single Gloucester has received numerous accolades, including a gold medal at the British Cheese Awards in 2001. It is also available at select Waitrose branches, known for their high-quality and carefully curated selection of products. Trust in the quality and authenticity of Single Gloucester, a cheese that has stood the test of time and continues to impress cheese lovers around the world.
