

1) Description of Cheese: Introducing Blue Wensleydale - a delectable creation dating back to the Norman Conquest and brought to Wensleydale by Cistercian monks from Jervauix Abbey. Originating from the famous blue cheese making region of Roquefort, France, this cheese is a true testament to its rich history and geographical provenance.

2) Description of Product: Blue Wensleydale is a unique creamy mellow blue cheese, made to an original recipe. It boasts a genuine smoothness in both texture and taste, making it a must-try for all cheese lovers. This cheese is available prepacked as part of the Select range of cheeses at Waitrose and is suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of the Process: Our Blue Wensleydale is made from pasteurised milk and aged to perfection. Our skilled cheesemakers follow a traditional preparation method, ensuring the highest quality and authenticity in every bite. The result? A one-of-a-kind cheese that will leave you craving for more.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Blue Wensleydale is simply irresistible, with a delicate yet distinct scent that will tantalize your senses. As for the taste, it is a perfect balance of creaminess and tanginess, with a subtle hint of blue mould that adds depth and complexity to every bite.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the ultimate experience, we recommend serving Blue Wensleydale at room temperature. Simply remove it from the fridge an hour before serving to allow it to develop its full texture and flavour. This cheese pairs perfectly with salads and pasta dishes, or can be enjoyed on a cheeseboard with warm bread and sliced tomatoes. For a complete indulgence, pair it with a glass of your favourite wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Don't just take our word for it - our cheese has received rave reviews and accolades. It has been quoted as "a true gem in the world of blue cheese" and has won numerous awards for its exceptional taste and quality. Rest assured, every batch of Blue Wensleydale undergoes rigorous quality tests to ensure that it meets our high standards, making it a cheese you can trust and enjoy with confidence.
