

1) Description of Cheese:
Muranda Cheese Company's Aged British Cheddar is a true artisanal cheese, made from unpasteurized cow's milk in the United States. With a rich history and geographical provenance in the region of NY, this cheese is a testament to the quality and tradition of American cheese-making.

2) Description of Product:
This hard, artisan cheese has a crumbly, crystalline and flaky texture, and is coated with a natural, pale yellow rind. It weighs approximately 1 pound and is shaped into a classic block, perfect for slicing or grating. The only ingredients used in this cheese are high-quality cow's milk and rennet.

3) Description of Process:
Muranda Cheese Company's Aged British Cheddar is carefully prepared and aged for one year, allowing it to develop a sharp and distinct flavor. The cheese is then smoked with a natural blend of hickory and applewood, adding a delicious smoky aroma to its already exceptional taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of this cheese is smokey and inviting, with hints of earthiness and sweetness. As for the taste, it is sharp and bold, with a perfect balance of creaminess and tanginess. The natural smoke adds a unique depth of flavor, making this cheese a true standout.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve this cheese at room temperature to allow its full flavor to develop. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers and fruit, or grated over pasta or salads. For a perfect pairing, enjoy with a Riesling or a hearty red wine.

6) Quality Assurance:
Muranda Cheese Company's raw milk Aged British Cheddar has received high praise from cheese experts and consumers alike. It has been awarded multiple accolades, including "Best American Cheese" at the World Cheese Awards. This cheese has also undergone rigorous quality tests, ensuring its exceptional taste and quality.
