

1) Description of Cheese: Barber's 1833 Vintage Reserve Cheddar is a premium cheese made by AJ & RE Barber Ltd, a renowned cheese producer with a rich history dating back to 1833. This cheese is a testament to their expertise and dedication to creating high-quality cheddar. It originates from the Somerset region in England, known for its lush green pastures and ideal conditions for dairy farming.

2) Description of Product: This artisan cheese is made from pasteurized cow's milk sourced from Holstein Friesian cows, known for producing rich and creamy milk. It is a hard cheese with a distinctive creamy, crumbly, and smooth texture. The cheese is rindless and has an ivory color. It weighs approximately 200 grams and is available in a convenient wedge shape.

3) Description of the Process: Barber's 1833 Vintage Reserve Cheddar is made using traditional methods, with a touch of modern technology. The milk is carefully selected and pasteurized before being transformed into cheese using rennet. The cheese is then aged for 24 months, allowing it to develop its intense and rich flavor. During this time, it is regularly turned and monitored to ensure the perfect texture and taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: This cheese has a rich aroma that is both savory and sweet, enticing your senses from the first whiff. The flavor is a perfect balance of sharpness and sweetness, with a creamy and smooth finish. The aging process gives the cheese a crunchy texture, adding to its unique taste.

This cheese is also an excellent addition to any sandwich or salad. For a more indulgent experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine, such as a Cabernet Sauvignon.
