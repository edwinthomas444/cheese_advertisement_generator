

1) Description of Cheese: Baron Bigod - Handmade in England by Fen Farm Dairy
Baron Bigod is a unique cheese with a rich history and geographical provenance. Made by Jonathan and Dulcie Crickmore in Bungay, Suffolk, England, it is the UK's only raw milk farmhouse Brie. Its name comes from the 12th century Norman nobleman, Hugh Bigod, who once owned the land where the cheese is now produced.

2) Description of Product: Creamy, Bloomy-Rind Cheese
Baron Bigod is a soft, artisan cheese made from unpasteurized cow's milk. It is a white bloomy-rind cheese with a creamy, smooth texture and a golden paste. It is available in various sizes and weights, ranging from small rounds to larger wheels. The cheese is made using traditional methods and is free from any additives or preservatives. Only red and white Montbeliarde cows, known for their rich and flavorful milk, are used to produce this exceptional cheese. Vegetarian rennet is used in the production process.

3) Description of Process: Handmade and Aged to Perfection
Baron Bigod is a cheese that is made with care and attention. The milk is gently ladled by hand and then aged for up to 8 weeks in a natural, cave-like environment. This allows the cheese to develop its unique flavors and textures. The result is a cheese that is delicate, silky, and bursting with complex flavors.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Earthy, Mushroomy, and Creamy
Baron Bigod has a distinctive aroma that is barnyardy and mushroom-like. Its taste is rich and creamy, with hints of earthy and mushroomy flavors. The cheese has a long-lasting finish that lingers on the palate, making it a true delight for cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions: Perfect for Any Occasion
To fully enjoy the flavors of Baron Bigod, it is recommended to serve it at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board with some crackers, fruits, and nuts for a delicious appetizer. This cheese pairs well with a variety of food, including cured meats, figs, and honey. For a complete experience, pair it with a glass of red wine, such as Pinot Noir or Chardonnay.

6) Quality Assurance: Recognized and Award-Winning Cheese
Baron Bigod has received numerous accolades and awards, solidifying its reputation as a top-quality cheese. It has been featured in prestigious publications and has been praised by renowned chefs and food critics. The cheese has also undergone strict quality tests to ensure its safety and authenticity. As Jonathan Crickmore puts it, "We are passionate about making cheese and are proud to share our labor of love with the world."
