

1) Description of Cheese: Acorn cheese, produced by Little Acorn Products, is a traditional hard cheese from the United Kingdom. It is made in the small village of Bethania in Ceredigion, using unpasteurized sheep's milk.

2) Description of Product: This artisan cheese is made from 100% Friesland sheep's milk and is coated with a vegetarian rennet. It has a firm, crumbly texture and weighs approximately 1kg.

3) Description of Process: Acorn cheese is carefully crafted by Karen and Don Ross at their farmhouse in Bethania. The cheese is aged for 3-6 months, allowing it to develop its unique flavor and texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Acorn cheese is fruity, with hints of burnt caramel. The taste is a perfect balance of herbaceous notes, citrusy undertones, and a deep, creamy finish.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best flavor and texture, Acorn cheese should be served at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board or grated over dishes for added depth. This versatile cheese pairs well with traditional Welsh vegetables and leeks, and also goes perfectly with a crisp white wine.

6) Quality Assurance: "Acorn cheese is a true representation of the rich flavors and quality of Welsh sheep's milk." - Karen Ross, Little Acorn Products.
