

1) Description of Cheese:
Abbot's Gold, made by the renowned Wensleydale Creamery in England, is a traditional English cheese with a rich history and geographical provenance. This creamy, classic Cheddar is infused with sweet caramelised onions, giving it a unique and irresistible flavour.

2) Description of Product:
Abbot's Gold is a semi-hard cheese made from pasteurised cow's milk. It has a pale yellow colour and a natural rind. The texture is creamy, crumbly, dense, and semi-firm, making it perfect for slicing, grating, or melting. This cheese is available in various sizes and weights, making it suitable for any occasion.

3) Description of Process:
Abbot's Gold is prepared using traditional techniques and is aged to perfection. The cheese is carefully crafted by expert cheesemakers and aged for just the right amount of time to develop its distinct flavour and texture. This cheese is made with the highest quality ingredients and is free from any additives or preservatives.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of Abbot's Gold is truly aromatic, with a hint of tanginess. The taste is mild, sweet, and tangy, with a burst of flavour from the caramelised onions. This cheese is perfect for those who enjoy a balance of sweetness and tanginess in their cheese.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Abbot's Gold at room temperature to allow the full flavour to develop. This cheese pairs well with a crisp Riesling or a refreshing pale ale. It can also be used to add a touch of flavour to sandwiches, hot dogs, and grilled steaks. For a more elegant presentation, serve it on a cheese platter with crackers and fruit.
