

1) Description of Cheese: Applewood is a traditional farmhouse Cheddar from Ilchester, England. It was first invented in 1965 and is also known as Applewood smoked cheddar.

2) Description of Product: This dense semi-hard cheese is made from pasteurized cow's milk and is coated with a natural rind. It has a crumbly texture and a weight of approximately 200g. The cheese is produced by the renowned Ilchester Cheese Company.

3) Description of Process: Applewood is prepared using traditional methods and aged to perfection. It is not actually smoked, but instead, a dusting of paprika is added to give it a golden-amber appearance and a delicious smoky flavor.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Applewood is distinctly smoky, while the flavor is a perfect balance of smokiness and spiciness. The cheese has a rich and creamy taste that is sure to tantalize your taste buds.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Applewood at room temperature to bring out its full flavor. It can be crumbled or grated over pasta, melted on pork chops, or paired with sweet apples and raisins. For a gourmet experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine.

It has also undergone rigorous quality testing to ensure that every bite is of the highest quality.
