

1) Description of Cheese: Basing, made by Lower Basing Farm in Kent, United Kingdom, is a Caerphilly-style organic cheese with a rich history and geographical provenance.

2) Description of Product: Basing is a hard cheese made from unpasteurized goat's milk, with a crumbly texture and weighing approximately 200g. It is coated with a light press to retain its moisture and is made with vegetarian rennet.

3) Description of Process: Basing is carefully crafted using traditional methods and aged for two months. This allows the cheese to develop a mild and pleasant acidity, with hints of herbaceous goat's milk. As it matures, it becomes smoother and creamier.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Basing has a distinct herbaceous aroma and a smooth, creamy taste with a subtle acidity. The use of unpasteurized goat's milk gives it a unique and flavorful profile.

5) Serving Suggestions: Basing is best served at room temperature, allowing its flavors to fully develop. It can be presented on a cheese board or paired with fruits and crackers. For a perfect pairing, try Basing with Pancherenc du Vic-Bih wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Basing has received numerous accolades, including quotations from renowned cheese experts. It has also won awards for its exceptional taste and quality. Rest assured, Basing has been thoroughly tested to ensure the highest standards of quality.
