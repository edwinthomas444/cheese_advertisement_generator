

1) Description of Cheese: Introducing Bath Blue, a creamy and smooth blue cheese from the renowned Bath Soft Cheese Ltd. This classic blue veined cheese hails from the historic city of Bath in England, Great Britain and United Kingdom.

2) Description of Product: Bath Blue is a soft, artisan cheese made from pasteurized cow's milk. It comes in a creamy and smooth texture with a natural cream-colored rind. This cheese belongs to the Blue family and is known for its strong and creamy flavor. It weighs around 8-10 weeks and is made with organic cow's milk and traditional rennet.

3) Description of Process: Bath Blue is carefully prepared and aged in traditional stone built rooms, allowing it to develop its signature creamy and blue-veined taste. This meticulous process takes 8-10 weeks, resulting in a high-quality and flavorful cheese.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Bath Blue has a clean and inviting aroma, making it irresistible to cheese lovers. Its creamy and strong flavor will leave your taste buds longing for more. The blue veins add a unique depth to the taste, making it a must-try for all cheese enthusiasts.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Bath Blue at room temperature to fully appreciate its creamy and smooth texture. This cheese makes a stunning presentation on any cheese board and pairs perfectly with fruits, crackers, and bread. For a truly indulgent experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Don't just take our word for it, Bath Blue has been recognized for its excellence with a Super gold award at the 2014 World Cheese Awards. It has also been listed as one of the world's top 62 cheeses. Rest assured, every bite of Bath Blue is of the highest quality and will leave you wanting more.
