

1) Description of Cheese: Bath Soft Cheese, produced by Bath Soft Cheese Ltd, has a rich history dating back to 1801 in Somerset, England. It is a modern, farmhouse cheese that is known for its high quality and delicious taste.

2) Description of Product: Bath Soft Cheese is a soft-white cheese made from pasteurized cow's milk. It is shaped into a 4" square and weighs approximately 250g. The cheese has a creamy texture and is coated with a white bloomy rind. The only ingredients used are cow's milk and rennet, making it suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of Process: Bath Soft Cheese is carefully prepared by cheese-making veteran Graham Padfield. The cheese is aged to perfection, resulting in a soft and runny texture with a white bloomy rind. This process allows the cheese to develop earthy, mushroomy, and aromatic flavors, with hints of citrus and grass.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Bath Soft Cheese has an enticing aroma that is both earthy and fresh. The taste is a perfect balance of citrusy and lemony notes, with a hint of mushroom. The cheese is less chalky and has a fresher taste compared to traditional brie.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of Bath Soft Cheese, it is best served at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board, accompanied by crackers and fresh fruits. For a perfect pairing, it is recommended to serve Bath Soft Cheese with Champagne or Sparkling Vouvray.

6) Quality Assurance: Bath Soft Cheese has received numerous accolades and awards, including the Great Taste Award and the World Cheese Award. It has also been praised by renowned cheese experts, with one stating "Bath Soft Cheese is a true masterpiece of cheese-making." Every batch of Bath Soft Cheese undergoes strict quality tests to ensure its exceptional taste and quality.
