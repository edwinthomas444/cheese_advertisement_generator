

1) Description of Cheese: Bermondsey Hard Pressed is a delicious and unique cheese made by Bill Oglethorpe, an urban cheesemaker in London. This cheese has a rich history and is named after the region of Bermondsey, London where it is produced.

2) Description of Product: Bermondsey Hard Pressed is a hard-pressed, hard-rind cheese made from unpasteurized cow's milk. It has a creamy and crumbly texture and a natural, yellow rind. This artisan cheese is made in the shape of a wheel and weighs approximately 2 pounds. It is made using traditional methods and only the finest ingredients, including rennet from locally sourced cows.

3) Description of Process: Bermondsey Hard Pressed is carefully crafted using a traditional Alpine-style recipe. The cheese is pressed to remove excess moisture and then aged for several months to develop its unique flavor and texture. This process results in a cheese that is both creamy and nutty, with a complex and rich flavor.
