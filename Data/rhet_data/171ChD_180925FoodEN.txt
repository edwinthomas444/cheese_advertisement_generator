
1) Description of Cheese: Burwash Rose by Traditional Cheese Dairy is a semi-soft, artisan cheese that hails from Stonegate, East Sussex in England. It is made using unpasteurized cow's milk and is a tribute to British monastic cheeses.

2) Description of Product: Burwash Rose has a creamy and springy texture, with a washed rind and a beautiful cream color. It is a semi-soft cheese that is made from grass-fed, free-range raw milk from British Frisian cows. The cheese weighs approximately 250g and is shaped into a round wheel.

3) Description of Process: The cheese-making process involves using traditional methods and techniques, resulting in a rich, soft and creamy cheese. The cheese is then washed in English rose water from Wadhurst during the first few weeks of its maturation process, giving it a unique orange sticky rind with a distinct floral scent.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Burwash Rose has an aromatic and floral aroma, with a creamy and rich flavor. The cheese has a melt-in-the-mouth texture and a wonderful bouncy feel, making it a perfect addition to any cheeseboard.
