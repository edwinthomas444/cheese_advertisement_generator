

1) Description of Cheese: Introducing Burwood Bole, a delicious semi-hard cheese made by James McCall of James Cheese. This artisanal cheese hails from the beautiful region of Dorset in England, Great Britain and United Kingdom.

2) Description of Product: Burwood Bole is a log-shaped cheese made from unpasteurized cow's milk. It has a firm texture and is coated with a washed rind. Each roundel of this cheese weighs approximately 200 grams. The cheese is made using vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of Process: Burwood Bole is carefully crafted by hand and matured in a special washed rind room at Child Okeford. This unique process gives the cheese its distinct flavor and texture, making it a true artisanal cheese.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: As you open the packaging, you will be greeted with a delightful lemony aroma. Upon tasting, you will experience a perfect balance of nuttiness and sweetness, making it a truly unique and delicious cheese.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best taste, we recommend serving Burwood Bole at room temperature. It can be beautifully presented on a cheese board, paired with oatcakes, salads, or apples for a simple yet satisfying lunch. To elevate your experience, try pairing it with a glass of white wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Don't just take our word for it, Burwood Bole has received numerous accolades and awards for its exceptional taste and quality. As James McCall himself says, "Our cheese is a reflection of the love and care we put into every step of the process." Rest assured, you are getting the best quality cheese with Burwood Bole.
