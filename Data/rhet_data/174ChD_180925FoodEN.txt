

1) Description of Cheese: Caerphilly, made by Caws Cenarth, Neal's Yard Dairy and Westcombe Dairy, is a hard, white cheese originating from the town of Caerphilly in South Wales. It is known as "the crumblies" and is a popular cheese in the United Kingdom.

The texture is close, crumbly, dense, firm, springy, and supple. It has a natural rind and a weight that can range from 8 to 10 to 14 days. It can also be aged for up to a year to develop a harder texture and stronger taste.

The cheese is aged for 8 to 10 to 14 days, and some variants can be aged for up to a year. This aging process helps to develop the unique texture and flavor of Caerphilly cheese.
