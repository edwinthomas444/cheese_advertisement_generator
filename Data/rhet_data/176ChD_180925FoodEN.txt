

1) Description of Cheese: Celtic Promise is a premium, award-winning cheese produced by Teifi Farmhouse Cheese in Ceredigion, Wales. This artisanal cheese is made from unpasteurized cow's milk and has a rich historical and geographical provenance, originating from the United Kingdom.

2) Description of Product: This semi-soft cheese is hand-made by expert cheesemaker John Savage, resulting in a unique and slightly varying batch each time. Shaped like a dumpling, Celtic Promise has a creamy texture and is coated with a beautiful yellow, washed rind. It weighs approximately 1kg and has a high fat content of 48%. The cheese is made using vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for all diets.

3) Description of Process: After being hand-made on the Teifi Farmhouse, Celtic Promise is matured for 8 weeks at Eastside in East Sussex by Pat Jackson. During this time, the cheese is ripened in cider, resulting in a slightly moist and sticky orange-red rind. This process also gives the cheese its distinctive fruity aroma.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: As Celtic Promise ripens, its soft buttery texture develops a pungent aroma and a delicate, yet full-flavored taste. The cheese has a fruity and slightly tangy flavor, making it a unique and delicious addition to any cheeseboard.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Celtic Promise at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. This cheese is best enjoyed on a cheeseboard, paired with crackers or bread. It also goes well with cider, enhancing its fruity notes. For a complete experience, pair it with a crisp white wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Celtic Promise has been recognized as a champion at the British Cheese Awards in 2005, solidifying its quality and excellence. It has also been quoted as a must-try cheese by cheese experts and has undergone rigorous quality tests to ensure its premium taste and texture. With its rich history, unique production process, and delicious taste, Celtic Promise is a cheese that is sure to impress.
