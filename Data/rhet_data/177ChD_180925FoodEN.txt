
1) Description of Cheese:
Cerney Pyramid is a highly acclaimed cheese produced by the renowned artisans in the Cotswolds, England. This cheese is named after the picturesque village of Cerney, located just north of Cirencester. It has a rich history dating back to 1983 when it was first created by Lady Angus of Gloucestershire, who was inspired by the famous Valencay cheese from the Loire valley.

2) Description of Product:
Cerney Pyramid is a fresh soft goat's cheese made from unpasteurized goat's milk, giving it a unique and distinctive flavor. This artisanal cheese is shaped like a truncated pyramid and coated with a mix of oak ash and sea salt. It weighs approximately 200 grams and is suitable for vegetarians. The rennet used in its production is of the highest quality, ensuring the best taste and texture.

3) Description of the Process:
Crafted with utmost care and precision, Cerney Pyramid takes 3 days to make. The cheese is carefully prepared by hand and then aged for four weeks, allowing it to develop a more intense and complex flavor. The result is a cheese with a soft, mousse-like texture and a fresh, mild taste. As it ages, the flavor becomes richer and more robust.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Cerney Pyramid has a delightful aroma with hints of citrus and lemon. The taste is mild and sweet, with a subtle tanginess that is characteristic of goat's cheese. As it ages, the cheese develops deeper flavors, making it a perfect choice for those who enjoy a more complex taste.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Cerney Pyramid at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. It can be presented on a cheese board or as a centerpiece at any gathering. This versatile cheese pairs well with a variety of foods, such as crackers, fruits, and nuts. It also makes a delicious addition to salads, sandwiches, and pasta dishes. For a truly indulgent experience, pair it with a crisp white wine or a light-bodied red.

6) Quality Assurance:
Cerney Pyramid has received numerous accolades, including gold, silver, and bronze medals at the World Cheese Awards. It was also awarded the prestigious title of Supreme Champion at the Global Cheese Awards in 2001. In 2014, it won the Super Gold Award at the World Cheese Awards, solidifying its position as one of the world's top 62 cheeses. With such recognition and acclaim, you can be assured of the highest quality and exceptional taste in every bite of Cerney Pyramid.
