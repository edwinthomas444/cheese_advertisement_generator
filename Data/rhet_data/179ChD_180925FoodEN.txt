

1) Description of Cheese:
Childwickbury is a delicate and delicious goat's milk cheese produced by Oliver Hudson on the Childwickbury Estate near St. Albans, England. This artisan cheese has a rich history and is made in the beautiful region of Odell, Bedfordshire.

2) Description of Product:
Childwickbury is a fresh, soft cheese made from pasteurized goat's milk. It has a creamy and smooth texture and a natural white rind. This cheese is crafted in small batches, ensuring the highest quality. It is also vegetarian-friendly, as it is made using vegetarian rennet.

3) Description of Process:
Childwickbury cheese is carefully prepared using traditional methods, resulting in a delicate and moist cheese. It is then aged to perfection, allowing the flavors to develop and mature. The result is a fresh and young cheese that is bursting with flavor.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Childwickbury cheese has a unique and delightful aroma, with hints of fresh flowers and a mild, milky scent. The taste is equally delightful, with citrusy and lemony notes that are balanced by a mild and creamy flavor. It is a cheese that is sure to please the palate of any cheese lover.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Childwickbury cheese at room temperature to allow the flavors to fully develop. This cheese can be presented on a cheese board, paired with crackers and fresh fruit for a light and refreshing snack. It also pairs well with a crisp white wine, such as a Sauvignon Blanc, for a perfect combination of flavors.
