2) Description of Product: Our Cottage Cheese is a soft, artisanal cheese made from pasteurized or unpasteurized cow's milk. It has a creamy and crumbly texture, with a weight of 4.30 g/100g and a fat content of 4%. It is rindless and has a beautiful white color, with a sweet and mild flavor. We use only the highest quality ingredients and traditional rennet to create this delicious cheese.

3) Description of Process: Our Cottage Cheese is carefully prepared by draining the curd, but not pressing it, to retain its moisture. The curd is then washed to remove any acidity, leaving a sweet taste. This process results in a creamy and lumpy cheese that is sold in convenient pots for easy consumption. Our cheese is then aged to perfection, ensuring a rich and flavorful taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of our Cottage Cheese is subtle and inviting, with a hint of sweetness. The taste is equally delightful, with a mild and creamy flavor that is sure to please your palate. Its unique texture adds a delightful dimension to every bite.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, we recommend serving our Cottage Cheese at room temperature. It can be enjoyed on its own, with fruits, or spread on toast. It also makes a great addition to salads, adding a creamy and flavorful touch. For a complete experience, pair it with a crisp white wine or a light and fruity red.
