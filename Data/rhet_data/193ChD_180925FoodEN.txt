

1) Davidstow Extra Mature Cheddar: A Taste of Cornwall
Davidstow Extra Mature Cheddar, manufactured by Dairy Crest, is a true representation of the rich history and geographical provenance of Cornwall, England. Made from locally sourced pasteurized cow's milk, this cheese is a proud product of the beautiful region of Cornwall.

2) A Perfect Blend of Texture and Ingredients
This hard cheese comes in a pale yellow color, with a natural rind and a weight of approximately 200 grams. Its texture is a delightful combination of crystalline, flaky, grainy, and smooth, making it a treat for the senses. Made with vegetarian rennet, this cheese is suitable for vegetarians.

3) Aged to Perfection
Davidstow Extra Mature Cheddar is aged for at least 18 months, giving it a pronounced and sweet flavor. The aging process adds layers of complexity and concentrated bursts of flavor, thanks to the crunchy crystals that form within the cheese. This cheese is a testament to the art of cheese-making.

4) Rich Aroma and Flavor
The rich aroma of Davidstow Extra Mature Cheddar is a prelude to its delectable taste. The sweet and pronounced flavor is balanced perfectly with the crunchy texture, making it a cheese-lover's dream come true. Each bite is a journey through the flavors of Cornwall.

5) Perfect Pairings
This cheese is best served at room temperature, allowing its flavors to fully develop. It can be beautifully presented on a cheese platter, accompanied by some grapes, crackers, and a slice of dark, sticky fruit cake. Pair it with a full-bodied red wine, and you have a match made in heaven.

6) Quality Guaranteed
Don't just take our word for it, Davidstow Extra Mature Cheddar has been recognized for its quality and taste. It has won several awards and has been subjected to rigorous quality tests, ensuring that each block of cheese is of the highest quality. As one satisfied customer puts it, "It tastes like a piece of Cornwall in every bite."
