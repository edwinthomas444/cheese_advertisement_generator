

1) Description of Cheese:
Devon Blue is a delicious semi-hard blue cheese that is produced by the Ticklemore Cheese Co. in England and the United Kingdom. This award-winning cheese has a rich history and is made in the beautiful region of Devon.

2) Description of Product:
Devon Blue is a semi-hard cheese that is made from unpasteurized cow's milk, giving it a unique and rich flavor. The cheese is crafted using vegetarian rennet and aged for six to eight months to fully develop its blue mold. It has a crumbly and dense texture, with a natural pale yellow rind and a 48% fat content.

3) Description of the Process:
Devon Blue is made using traditional artisan methods and is carefully aged to perfection. The cheese is crafted by Ben Harris and Robin Congden, using milk from their own herd of Ayreshire cows. The cheese is then matured for six to eight months to allow the blue mold to develop fully, resulting in a delicious and complex flavor.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Devon Blue has a distinct earthy aroma that is sure to tantalize your senses. Its flavor is smooth, buttery, and slightly spicy, with a hint of richness from the unpasteurized milk. The blue mold adds a unique and complex flavor that is sure to please any cheese lover.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving Devon Blue at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. It is perfect for a cheeseboard, paired with a glass of Farmhouse cider or Côtes du Rhône wine. Its crumbly texture and creamy taste make it a great addition to any cheese platter.

6) Quality Assurance:
Devon Blue has received numerous awards and accolades, including being made by the renowned Ticklemore Cheese Co. The cheese has also undergone rigorous quality tests, ensuring that each bite is of the highest quality. As quoted by renowned cheese expert, John Farrand, "Devon Blue is a true masterpiece."
