
1) Description of Cheese: Dorset White, produced by Chalke Valley Cheese Ltd, is a soft, artisan cheese with a rich history and geographical origin in Dorset, England, part of the Great Britain and United Kingdom.

2) Description of Product: This soft-ripened cheese is made from unpasteurized cow's milk and traditional animal rennet. It is molded in a log shape, allowing for a creamy and firm texture. The thick sculpting of the log creates a creamy and tangy flavor, with a strong aroma. The rind is mold-ripened, giving the cheese a yellow color.

3) Description of Process: Dorset White is carefully prepared and aged to perfection. The cheese is made using traditional methods and aged until it reaches the perfect balance of flavor and texture. This process creates a cheese that is truly unique and full of character.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The strong aroma of Dorset White is a testament to its quality and richness. The creamy and tangy flavor is complemented by the firm texture, making it a delight for the senses. The mold-ripened rind adds a depth of flavor, making this cheese a must-try for cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Dorset White at room temperature to allow the flavors to fully develop. It can be presented on a cheese board, sliced or spread on crackers. This cheese pairs well with a variety of foods, such as fresh fruits, cured meats, and crusty bread. For a truly indulgent experience, pair it with a glass of white wine.
