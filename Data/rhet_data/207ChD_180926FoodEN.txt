

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Baber's West Country Farmhouse Mature Cheddar, a PDO cheese from England's Somerset region. Made by AJ & RE Barber Ltd, this cheddar has a rich history and is made with pasteurized cow's milk.

2) Description of Product:
This artisanal cheese comes in a semi firm, ivory-colored block with a smooth texture. It has a rindless coating and is made from milk sourced from Holstein Friesian cows. The cheese weighs approximately 12 months and has a creamy and smooth texture.

3) Description of Process:
Baber's West Country Farmhouse Mature Cheddar is carefully prepared and aged for 12 months. This process allows the cheese to develop a distinctive rich flavor, finishing off with a rounded character and smooth texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
With a rich aroma and smooth flavor, this cheddar is sure to please any cheese lover. The creamy texture and distinct flavor make it a standout among other cheddars.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve this cheese at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers, fruits, and nuts. Pair it with a full-bodied red wine for a perfect combination of flavors.

6) Quality Assurance:
Baber's West Country Farmhouse Mature Cheddar has received numerous accolades, including being a PDO cheese. It is also vegetarian-friendly. With its rich flavor and smooth texture, it's no wonder it's a favorite among cheese connoisseurs. As AJ & RE Barber Ltd says, "Our cheese is our pride and joy, and we take great care to ensure its quality."
