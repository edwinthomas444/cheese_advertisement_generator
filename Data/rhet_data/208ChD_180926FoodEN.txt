

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Vulscombe, a delectable cheese made from pasteurized goat's milk by Joyce and Graham Townsend in the picturesque region of Tiverton, England. With a rich history and origin in the United Kingdom, this artisanal cheese is a true representation of traditional cheese-making techniques.

2) Description of Product:
Vulscombe is a fresh, soft cheese with a creamy texture and a rindless coating. It is handcrafted and comes in various sizes and shapes, including plain and bold flavors like black pepper, herbs, and sun-dried tomatoes. The herbs used in the cheese are freshly picked from the cheese makers' own garden. Plus, it is suitable for vegetarians as no animal rennet is used.

3) Description of Process:
The milk used in Vulscombe cheese comes from a herd of goats in the stunning upper Exe Valley. It is carefully pasteurized and aged to perfection, resulting in a rich and mild flavor that is simply irresistible.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Vulscombe has a distinct aroma of goat's milk, herbs, and richness. Its flavor is a perfect balance of herbaceous, mild, and spicy, making it a unique and flavorful experience for your taste buds.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Vulscombe at room temperature to fully appreciate its creamy texture and rich flavor. It can be beautifully presented on a cheese platter or paired with crackers and fresh fruits. For a complete indulgence, pair it with a glass of your favorite wine.

As quoted by the cheese makers themselves, "The cheese looks and tastes amazing." Plus, it has passed rigorous quality tests, ensuring that you receive only the best cheese.
