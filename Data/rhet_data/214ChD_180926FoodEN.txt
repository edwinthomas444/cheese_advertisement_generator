2) Description of Product: Trelawny is a semi-soft, artisan cheese with a crumbly texture and a washed rind. It is made from pasteurized cow's milk and vegetable rennet, making it suitable for vegetarians. The cheese is shaped into a wheel and has an ivory color. It weighs approximately 250 grams.

3) Description of Process: Trelawny is made using traditional methods, starting with pasteurizing the cow's milk. The cheese is then mould ripened, giving it a unique citrus flavor. It is aged for several weeks, allowing the flavors to develop and mature.
