

1) Description of Cheese: Tunworth is a traditional Camembert-style cheese handmade by Stacey Hedges and Charlotte Spruce from pasteurized cow's milk. It has a rich history and is produced in the Herriard region of Hampshire, England, in the United Kingdom.

2) Description of Product: This artisan cheese is soft-ripened and has a creamy texture. It is coated with a thin, wrinkled rind and comes in a Camembert-style wooden box. Made from pasteurized cow's milk, it is also infused with a special type of rennet for added flavor.

3) Description of Process: Tunworth is carefully crafted using traditional methods and aged to perfection. It is made from high-quality pasteurized cow's milk and undergoes a slow ripening process to develop its creamy, soft texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Upon opening the box, you will be greeted with a rich, milky aroma. The cheese has a creamy, nutty, and sweet flavor with a hint of vegetal notes. The flavors are long-lasting and harmonious, leaving a mouth-filling sensation.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Tunworth at room temperature. To add to its presentation, place the cheese on a wooden board and pair it with crackers, fresh fruits, and nuts. It pairs perfectly with a glass of white wine or champagne.

6) Quality Assurance: Tunworth has been recognized for its exceptional quality, winning awards such as Supreme Champion at the British Cheese Awards, the Waitrose Small Producer Award, and a gold medal at the World Cheese Awards. It has also received rave reviews from renowned cheese experts and food critics. Trust us, you won't be disappointed with this award-winning cheese.
