

1) Description of Cheese: Seator's Orkney is a unique and delicious cheese that is proudly made by Hilda Seator at her Grimbister Farm on the Orkney Islands. With a rich history and strong ties to the Great Britain, Scotland, and United Kingdom, this cheese is a true representation of the region's rich dairy heritage.

2) Description of Product: Seator's Orkney is a hard, artisan cheese that is handcrafted using unpasteurized cow's milk. It has a crumbly texture and a beautiful cream color. The cheese is sold in a variety of sizes and weights, making it perfect for any occasion. It is coated in a natural rind that adds to its unique flavor.

3) Description of the Process: The cheese-making process of Seator's Orkney is a labor of love. The milk is carefully selected and aged for 4 days before being sold in the market. This allows the cheese to develop its distinct flavor and texture. The cheese is made using traditional methods, ensuring its authenticity and quality.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Seator's Orkney has a sharp and acidic flavor that is reminiscent of other popular British cheeses such as Wensleydale, Caerphilly, and Cotherstone. As it ages, it develops a fruity and yeasty taste with a lactic aroma. The cheese's strong and unique flavors make it a favorite among cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, Seator's Orkney should be served at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. It can be presented on a cheese board or used in various recipes. This cheese pairs well with fruits, crackers, and crusty bread. For a perfect pairing, enjoy it with a glass of dry white wine.
