
1) Description of Cheese: Introducing Ford Farm's award-winning Wookey Hole Farmhouse Cheddar, a true masterpiece of artisanal cheese-making. This cheese is made in Dorset, England by the renowned Ford Farm, and is named after the famous Wookey Hole Caves in Somerset where it is aged to perfection. With a rich history and a unique geographical provenance, this cheese is a must-try for all cheese lovers.

2) Description of Product: This hard, artisanal cheese is hand-crafted using pasteurised cow's milk and vegetarian rennet, ensuring a high-quality and ethical product. It is shaped into a traditional wheel and coated in cloth before being aged in the Wookey Hole Caves for up to six months. The result is a creamy and crumbly texture, with a natural golden yellow rind that adds to its rustic charm.

3) Description of Process: Ford Farm's Wookey Hole Farmhouse Cheddar undergoes a meticulous process to achieve its distinct flavour and texture. Made in their Dorset Cheese Farm, the cheese is then taken to the Wookey Hole Caves where it is aged at a constant temperature. This traditional method allows the cheese to develop its unique earthy undertones and nutty, tangy flavour.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: With its earthy aroma and complex flavour, Wookey Hole Farmhouse Cheddar is a treat for the senses. The nutty and sharp taste is balanced with a tangy kick, making it a versatile cheese that can be enjoyed on its own or paired with other foods.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve this cheese at room temperature to fully appreciate its creamy and crumbly texture. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers, fruits, and nuts for a delicious snack or appetizer. For a more indulgent experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine or a crisp white wine, such as a Chardonnay.

6) Quality Assurance: Don't just take our word for it, Wookey Hole Farmhouse Cheddar has been recognized for its exceptional quality. It is protected by PDO, meaning it can only be produced in the counties of Dorset, Somerset, and Devon & Cornwall. This cheese has also won numerous awards, solidifying its reputation as a top-quality cheese. As Ford Farm proudly states, "Our cheese is the best it can be, because we love what we do."
