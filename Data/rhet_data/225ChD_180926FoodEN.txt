

1) Description of Cheese: Sharpham Rustic, produced by Sharpham Wine & Cheese, is a semi-hard, artisan cheese hailing from the beautiful region of Devon, England, in the United Kingdom. This cheese has a rich history and is deeply rooted in the traditions of the area.

2) Description of Product: Sharpham Rustic is a semi-hard cheese made from unpasteurized Jersey cow's milk. It has a distinctive shape and size, with a creamy texture and natural rind. Each cheese is handcrafted and aged for a month, resulting in a unique and delicious flavor. The cheese weighs approximately one month and is coated with a layer of white.

3) Description of Process: Sharpham Rustic is made using traditional methods, ensuring the highest quality and taste. The cheese is carefully prepared and aged for a month, allowing it to develop its signature lemony and nutty flavor. The cheese is then packaged and ready to be enjoyed.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Sharpham Rustic has a fresh aroma, reminiscent of the lush pastures of Devon. When young, it has a creamy, lemony taste, which deepens into a nutty and mature flavor as it ages. The cheese is a perfect balance of tangy and savory, making it a true delight for the taste buds.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully appreciate the flavors of Sharpham Rustic, it is best served at room temperature. The cheese can be presented on a cheese board, accompanied by crackers, fruits, and nuts. It pairs wonderfully with a crisp white wine or a full-bodied red, making it a versatile addition to any meal.

6) Quality Assurance: Sharpham Rustic has received numerous awards, including gold at the Global Cheese Awards 2014 and British Cheese Awards 2013, and bronze at the Taste of the West Awards 2014. This is a testament to the exceptional quality and taste of this cheese. As a quote from one satisfied customer puts it, "Sharpham Rustic is a true gem and a must-try for all cheese lovers." With its rich history, traditional preparation, and award-winning taste, Sharpham Rustic is a cheese that guarantees a delightful experience for all.
