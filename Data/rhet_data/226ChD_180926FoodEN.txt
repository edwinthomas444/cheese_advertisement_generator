

1) Description of Cheese: Sharpham Chive & Garlic Rustic is a semi-hard, unpasteurized cheese produced by Sharpham Wine & Cheese in Devon, England. This award-winning cheese has a rich history and is made in the traditional artisanal style.

2) Description of Product: This cheese is made from unpasteurized Jersey cow's milk, giving it a creamy texture and rich flavor. It is shaped into a wheel and coated with a natural rind. Each wheel weighs approximately one month and has a shelf life of around one month.

3) Description of Process: Sharpham Chive & Garlic Rustic is carefully crafted using a traditional aging process. After around a month, the cheese develops a savory flavor and creamy texture. Chives and garlic are added before the cheese is molded, giving it a unique and delicious taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of this cheese is a combination of garlicky and nutty scents, while the flavor is creamy, garlicky, and savory. The intense flavor of the cheese is perfectly balanced by its creamy texture, making it a delight for the senses.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, we recommend serving Sharpham Chive & Garlic Rustic at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board or used in cooking to add a burst of flavor to any dish. This cheese pairs well with a variety of foods, including crackers, bread, and fruits. For a perfect pairing, we suggest enjoying it with a glass of white wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Sharpham Chive & Garlic Rustic has been recognized for its exceptional quality, winning awards such as gold at the Global Cheese Awards 2013, bronze at the British Cheese Awards 2014, and silver at the Taste of the West Awards 2014. Our cheese undergoes rigorous quality tests to ensure that each wheel meets our high standards, making it a top choice for cheese lovers worldwide. As one satisfied customer put it, "This cheese is a true masterpiece."
