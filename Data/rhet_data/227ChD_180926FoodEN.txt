

1) Description of Cheese: Stoney Cross, produced by Lyburn Farm, is a delicious cheese with a rich history and geographical provenance. Named after an old World War II airfield in England's Landford region, this cheese is a tribute to the country's heritage.

2) Description of Product: This artisan cheese is made from pasteurized cow's milk and is a semi-hard truckle coated with mold. It has a creamy and smooth texture, weighing in at approximately 2 pounds. The use of vegetarian rennet ensures that this cheese is suitable for all dietary preferences.

3) Description of Process: Stoney Cross is carefully prepared and aged to perfection. The cheese is made using traditional methods, resulting in a unique and flavorful product. It is then aged for the perfect amount of time, allowing the flavors to develop and mature.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The earthy and subtle flavors of Stoney Cross are complemented by a sweet finish. The cheese has a distinct aroma, adding to its overall appeal. Its creamy texture and smooth taste make it a favorite among cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Stoney Cross at room temperature. Its presentation is enhanced when sliced into wedges and served on a cheese board with crackers and fruit. This cheese pairs well with a variety of foods, including cured meats and honey. For a perfect pairing, enjoy it with a glass of red wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Don't just take our word for it, Stoney Cross has received numerous awards and accolades. It has won a Bronze at the British cheese awards, a Silver at Nantwich, and a One Star Gold at the Great Taste. These awards are a testament to the quality and taste of this exceptional cheese.
