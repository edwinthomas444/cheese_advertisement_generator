

1) Description of Cheese: The Wyfe of Bath, made by Bath Soft Cheese Ltd, is a farmhouse style cheese with a rich history originating from the beautiful city of Bath in the United Kingdom. This cheese is named after the famous Wife of Bath character from Chaucer's Canterbury Tales.

2) Description of Product: The Wyfe of Bath is a semi-hard, artisan cheese made from pasteurized cow's milk. It has a buttery texture and a natural cream-colored rind. This cheese is made in a traditional Dutch gouda style, but with a unique twist - it is gently pressed to retain more moisture, resulting in a delightful buttery finish. The Wyfe of Bath is also made using organic milk, ensuring the highest quality and taste. The rennet used in the production of this cheese is also of the highest quality, adding to its exceptional taste.

3) Description of Process: The Wyfe of Bath is carefully crafted using traditional methods and aged to perfection. The organic cow's milk is pasteurized and then gently pressed to remove excess moisture. The cheese is then aged in a controlled environment, allowing it to develop its unique flavor and aroma.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The Wyfe of Bath has a sweet and buttery aroma, reminiscent of the lush fields of the farm where the cows graze. Its flavor is rich and creamy, with a hint of sweetness and a lingering buttery finish. This cheese is sure to delight your taste buds with every bite.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, we recommend serving the Wyfe of Bath at room temperature. Its beautiful cream color and natural rind make for an attractive presentation on any cheese platter. This cheese pairs perfectly with a variety of foods, such as crusty bread, crackers, and fresh fruits. For a complete sensory experience, we suggest pairing it with a light and fruity white wine.

Our commitment to using only the highest quality ingredients and traditional production methods ensures that every bite of the Wyfe of Bath is of exceptional quality.
