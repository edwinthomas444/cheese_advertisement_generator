

1) Description of Cheese: Wigmore by Village Maid Cheese from Risley, England and United Kingdom. 
2) Description of Product: This semi-soft, artisan cheese is made from sheep's milk and has a creamy and crumbly texture. It is coated with a bloomy rind and has a weight of 48%. 
3) Description of Process: Wigmore is carefully prepared and aged for 8 weeks to fully ripen, resulting in a delicious and high-quality cheese. 
4) Description of Smell and Taste: With a fruity and mild flavor and a yeasty aroma, Wigmore is a delightful and unique cheese that will tantalize your taste buds. 
5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Wigmore at room temperature and pair it with a Cabernet Merlot. Its white color and bloomy rind make for a beautiful presentation on any cheese platter. 
6) Quality Assurance: Wigmore has won numerous Gold Medals at the British Cheese Awards, solidifying its reputation as a top-quality cheese loved by many. As Anna and Andy Wigmore say, "Our cheese is made with love and passion, and we're proud to share it with the world."
