

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Sharpham, a delectable English cheese made from unpasteurized cow's milk by the renowned Sharpham Wine & Cheese producers. This artisan cheese hails from the picturesque region of Devon in England and is a proud representation of the country's rich cheese-making heritage.

2) Description of Product:
Sharpham is a soft, artisan cheese with a creamy and smooth texture. Each wheel is made using fresh Jersey cow milk and vegetarian rennet, staying true to its original 1980 recipe. This Coulommiers type cheese has a distinctive mold-ripened rind and a weight of approximately 200g.

3) Description of Process:
Handmade at the Sharpham Dairy, this cheese undergoes a meticulous preparation process before being aged to perfection. The use of vegetarian rennet and the finest quality cow's milk results in a uniquely buttery flavor that has won numerous awards.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of Sharpham is fresh and inviting, while the taste is rich and buttery. The creamy texture melts in your mouth, leaving behind a lingering flavor that will have you reaching for more. This cheese is truly a treat for the senses.

5) Serving Suggestions:
To fully enjoy the flavors of Sharpham, it is recommended to serve it at room temperature. This cheese pairs perfectly with bread or biscuits, making it a great addition to any cheese platter. For a complete experience, try it with a glass of your favorite wine.

6) Quality Assurance:
Sharpham has been recognized for its exceptional taste and quality, winning awards such as Super Gold at the World Cheese Awards 2010 and Best Soft White at the British Cheese Awards. Our commitment to using only the finest ingredients and traditional cheese-making techniques ensures that each bite of Sharpham is a true delight.
