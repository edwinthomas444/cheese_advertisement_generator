

1) Description of Cheese: Duddleswell is a high fat sheep's cheese made at the High Weald Dairy in West Sussex, United Kingdom. This award-winning cheese has a rich history and is produced in the beautiful region of West Sussex.

2) Description of Product: Duddleswell is a hard, artisan cheese made from pasteurized sheep's milk. It has a crumbly texture and a natural, edible rind. The cheese is pale yellow in color and has a fat content of 35%. Only the finest quality ingredients are used, including vegetable rennet, making it suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of Process: Duddleswell is carefully prepared and aged for 5 months in temperature controlled conditions. During this time, it is turned daily to ensure the perfect flavor and texture. For the smoked version, the cheese is lightly smoked over oak shavings for 12 to 36 hours, resulting in a unique and delicious taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Duddleswell has a delightful aroma of sweet caramel and Brazilian nuts, with hints of fresh hay. The taste is slightly acidic, with a buttery and sweet finish. This cheese is truly a treat for the senses.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of Duddleswell, it is best served at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers and fruits, or grated over pasta dishes and salads. For wine pairing, we recommend a bold Merlot to complement the cheese's rich and complex flavors.

6) Quality Assurance: Duddleswell has received numerous awards, including Gold at the International Cheese Awards in 2010 and 2011, and Bronze at the British Cheese Awards in 2010. Our commitment to quality is evident in every bite of this exceptional cheese.
