

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Dunlop, a traditional Scottish cheese made by Dunlop Dairy in the village of Dunlop, Scotland. Developed in the 1700s by Barbara Gilmour, this cheese has a rich historical and geographical provenance, making it a truly authentic and unique cheese.

2) Description of Product:
Dunlop is a hard cheese made from pasteurized cow's milk of Ayrshire breed. Its shape and size may vary, but it typically has a close, crumbly texture and a natural yellow rind. This cheese is coated in a layer of buttery, mild, nutty, sharp, and sweet flavors, making it a perfect choice for cheese lovers.

3) Description of the Process:
This cheese is carefully crafted using traditional methods and aged for a minimum of 6 months. It is made using vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for vegetarians. The result is a cheese with a smooth and close texture when young, and as it matures, it develops a firm texture and sharp flavors.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Dunlop has a pleasant aroma that will awaken your senses. Its flavors are a perfect balance of buttery, nutty, and sharp, with a hint of sweetness. As the cheese matures, the flavors become more pronounced, making it a delight for cheese connoisseurs.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Dunlop at room temperature to fully appreciate its flavors. It can be presented on a cheese board or used in various recipes. This cheese pairs well with whisky, making it a perfect choice for a cheese and whiskey tasting. It also goes well with a variety of wines, enhancing the overall dining experience.

6) Quality Assurance:
Dunlop has been recognized for its exceptional quality, with numerous awards and accolades. It has been quoted as a must-try cheese by cheese experts and has undergone rigorous quality tests to ensure its top-notch quality. So, you can rest assured that you are getting the best cheese with Dunlop.
