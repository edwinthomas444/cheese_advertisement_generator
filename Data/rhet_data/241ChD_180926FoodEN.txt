
1) Description of Cheese: Finn by Neal's Yard Dairy is a soft, artisan cheese that originated in Herefordshire, England and is now produced in the United Kingdom. It has a rich history and is made with unpasteurized cow's milk.

2) Description of Product: This creamy cheese has a bloomy rind and a white color. It is sold in a 200g cylinder and has a fat content of 75%. The texture is both creamy and firm, making it a unique and indulgent cheese. Finn is made with only the finest ingredients, including high-quality cow's milk and rennet.

3) Description of Process: Finn is carefully crafted by expert cheesemaker Charlie Westhead at Neal's Yard Creamery. The cheese is ripened for 2-3 weeks, allowing it to develop its signature buttery, nutty, and salty flavors. During the cheesemaking process, an additional 10% cream is added to give it a rich and creamy texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Finn is earthy and fresh, making it a delight to the senses. As it matures, the cheese takes on a mushroomy and nutty taste, adding depth and complexity to its flavor profile. The mild and lactic taste when young evolves into a rich and indulgent experience as it ages.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors and textures of Finn, it is best served at room temperature. This allows the cheese to reach its full potential and enhances its creamy and firm texture. Finn is perfect for a cheese platter, paired with fruits and crackers. It also makes a delicious addition to sandwiches or melted on top of a warm baguette. For a complete experience, pair Finn with a crisp white wine or a light red.

6) Quality Assurance: Finn has received numerous accolades, including being the only triple cream cheese made in England. Its quality and taste have been recognized by experts in the industry, making it a top choice for cheese lovers. As quoted by Neal's Yard Dairy, Finn is "firm with a rich, double creamy texture" and is a must-try for any cheese connoisseur.
