

1) Description of Cheese: Flower Marie by Golden Cross Cheese Company Ltd is a soft, artisan cheese made from unpasteurized sheep's milk. It is inspired by the Corsican sheep cheese, 'Fleur de Maquis', and is produced at Greenacres Farm near Lewes, Sussex in England and United Kingdom.

2) Description of Product: Flower Marie is a delicate little cheese with a square shape and a bloomy, pinkish rind. It is made from milk sourced from a herd of Friesland-Dorset sheep and is matured for a minimum of three weeks. The cheese has a creamy texture and is coated with a mold-ripened ivory white paste. It is suitable for vegetarians as vegetable rennet is used in its production.

3) Description of the Process: Flower Marie is handcrafted by Kevin and Alison Blunt at their Greenacres Farm. The milk is coagulated using vegetable rennet and then matured for three weeks. During this time, the square molds develop a bloomy, pinkish rind and the paste inside becomes creamy and ivory white.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Flower Marie has an aromatic aroma and a creamy, mushroomy flavor with hints of sweetness and tanginess. As it ages, the cheese develops more intense flavors while maintaining its creamy texture.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Flower Marie at room temperature and present it on a cheese board with crackers, fruits, and nuts. It pairs well with a variety of foods such as figs, honey, and prosciutto. For wine pairing, try a dry white wine such as Sauvignon Blanc or a light-bodied red wine like Pinot Noir.

6) Quality Assurance: Flower Marie has received numerous accolades, including quotations from renowned cheese experts and awards for its exceptional quality. It has also undergone rigorous quality tests to ensure it meets the highest standards. Trust in the quality of Flower Marie to elevate your cheese experience.
