
1) Description of Cheese: Gorwydd Caerphilly is a highly acclaimed artisanal cheese, hand-crafted by Todd, Maugan and Kim Trethowan at their Gorwydd Farm in Llanddewi Brefi, Ceredigion. With a rich history and geographical provenance in Great Britain, United Kingdom and Wales, this cheese is a true representation of traditional Welsh cheese-making.

2) Description of Product: This semi-soft cheese is made from unpasteurized cow's milk, giving it a creamy and springy texture. It is coated with a natural ivory rind and has a weight of approximately 2.2 pounds. Gorwydd Caerphilly is made using traditional animal rennet, ensuring an authentic and unique flavor.

3) Description of Process: Gorwydd Caerphilly is carefully prepared and aged for three months, resulting in a delectable combination of a creamy, mushroomy layer and a tangy, citrusy core. The cheese is handcrafted using traditional methods, passed down through generations, making it a truly artisanal product.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The earthy and musty aroma of Gorwydd Caerphilly is a testament to its natural aging process. Its flavor profile is a perfect balance of milky and mushroomy notes, making it a delight for cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Gorwydd Caerphilly at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. This cheese can be presented on a cheese board or paired with crackers and fruits. It pairs exceptionally well with Viogner and Chardonnay, enhancing its creamy and tangy flavors.

6) Quality Assurance: Gorwydd Caerphilly has been recognized for its exceptional taste and quality, winning numerous prestigious cheese awards. It has also undergone rigorous quality tests, ensuring that every bite of this cheese is of the highest standard. As quoted by one satisfied customer, "Gorwydd Caerphilly is a true gem among Welsh cheeses."
