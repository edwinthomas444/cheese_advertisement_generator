

1) Description of Cheese: Gospel Green, produced by James and Cathy Lane, is a delicious hard cheese made from unpasteurized cow's milk. This cheese has a rich history and comes from the beautiful region of Surrey, England and the United Kingdom.

2) Description of Product: Gospel Green is a unique cheese that stands out from other cheddars. It has a slightly soft and less dense texture, with a crumbly texture and a cloth-wrapped rind. This cheese weighs approximately 2-3 months to ripen completely and has a fat content of 48%. The ingredients used to make this cheese include high-quality cow's milk and rennet.

3) Description of Process: Gospel Green is carefully prepared and aged to perfection. It takes 2-3 months for this cheese to ripen completely, giving it a distinct flavor and texture. The cheese is made using traditional techniques, ensuring the highest quality and taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Gospel Green is a delightful combination of floral and grassy scents. The fruity smell of apples fills the kitchen when this cheese is being prepared. When it comes to taste, this cheese has a unique and delicious fruity flavor that is sure to please any palate.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of Gospel Green, we recommend serving it at room temperature. This cheese can be presented on a cheese board or grated and used in cooking and grilling. It pairs perfectly with a glass of Merlot and can be enjoyed with a variety of foods, making it a versatile choice for any occasion.
