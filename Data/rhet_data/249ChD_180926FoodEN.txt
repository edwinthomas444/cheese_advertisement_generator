
1) Description of Cheese: Harbourne Blue, produced by Neal's Yard Dairy and Ticklemore Cheese, has a rich history and comes from the beautiful region of Devon in England and United Kingdom.

2) Description of Product: This artisanal cheese is made from pasteurized goat's milk and has a semi-soft texture. It is crumbly and dense, with a natural straw-colored rind. The cheese has a fat content of 48% and is available in various sizes and weights.

3) Description of Process: Harbourne Blue is handcrafted and takes around 10 weeks to mature. The cheese is carefully prepared by Robin Congdon and then aged by Ticklemore Cheese, ensuring its unique and delicious taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: With a strong aroma and spicy, sweet flavor, Harbourne Blue is a treat for the senses. The cheese retains its natural, handcrafted taste and smell, making it a must-try for all cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Harbourne Blue at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. It can be presented on a cheese board or added to dishes for a touch of creaminess. This versatile cheese pairs well with wines such as Sauternes, Port, and Bordeaux.

6) Quality Assurance: Harbourne Blue has received high praise and recognition, with quotations from satisfied customers and awards from prestigious cheese competitions. It has also undergone rigorous quality tests to ensure its superior taste and quality. Don't miss out on this award-winning cheese!
