

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Hereford Hop, a delicious British cheese handcrafted by Charles Martell & Son Limited, the makers of the renowned Stinking Bishop. This cheese has a rich history, first created in 1990 on Hunts Court Farm, just a few miles from the Herefordshire border in England.

2) Description of Product:
Hereford Hop is a hard, artisan cheese made from pasteurized cow's milk. With a creamy and firm texture, it is coated with toasted hops, giving it a unique and distinctive appearance. Weighing in at a perfect size, this cheese is sure to satisfy any cheese lover's cravings.

3) Description of Process:
Using only natural rennet and full-fat pasteurized cows' milk, Hereford Hop is carefully prepared and aged to perfection. The use of toasted hops adds a touch of bitterness to the cheese, complementing the buttery, citrusy, and sharp flavors that develop during the aging process. For those looking for a raw milk option, we also offer a version made with raw milk.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The strong, yeasty aroma of Hereford Hop is a testament to its quality and freshness. Upon tasting, you'll experience a burst of buttery and citrusy flavors, followed by a sharp and sweet finish. The addition of hops adds a unique twist to the overall taste, making it a must-try for cheese connoisseurs.

5) Serving Suggestions:
To fully enjoy the flavors of Hereford Hop, we recommend serving it at room temperature. When presenting, cut the cheese into thin slices and arrange them on a platter with crackers and fresh fruits. For the perfect food pairing, try it with cured meats, pickles, or fig jam. And for a complete experience, pair it with a glass of crisp white wine.
