

1) Description of Cheese: Huntsman by Long Clawson Dairy Limited is a unique and delicious cheese hailing from the English countryside. It is a combination of two classic cheeses, Double Gloucester and Stilton Blue, both with a rich history and origin in England.

2) Description of Product: Huntsman is a semi-hard to hard cheese made from pasteurized or unpasteurized cow's milk. It has a creamy, crumbly, and dense texture with a natural golden orange rind. This cheese weighs around 2-3 pounds and is available in a round shape.

3) Description of Process: To create Huntsman, the cheesemakers layer Stilton Blue between two layers of Double Gloucester, resulting in a unique and powerful flavor combination. The cheese is then aged to perfection, allowing the flavors to develop and intensify.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Huntsman has a strong and sweet aroma, with hints of nuttiness and spiciness. The flavor is a perfect balance of mild, tangy, and strong, making it a versatile cheese that can be enjoyed by all.
