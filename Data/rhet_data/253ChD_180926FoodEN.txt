

1) Description of Cheese: Innes Brick is a unique and delicious artisanal cheese made from unpasteurized goat's milk. It is handcrafted by Stella Bennett and her son Joe at their Highfields Farm Dairy, located in Staffordshire, England, Great Britain and United Kingdom.

2) Description of Product: This semi-soft cheese has a creamy and dense texture, with a mold-ripened rind. It is a pale yellow geotrichum-rinded cheese, with a smooth and slightly runny breakdown under the rind. Innes Brick is made using traditional animal rennet, giving it a mild, milky flavor with nutty undertones.

3) Description of the Process: Innes Brick is made using time-honored traditional craft methods. The cheese is aged to perfection, allowing it to develop its unique flavor and texture. The use of unpasteurized goat's milk and traditional animal rennet ensures the highest quality and authenticity of this artisanal cheese.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The fresh, lactic aroma of Innes Brick is just a preview of its delicious flavor. The cheese has a mild and creamy taste, with subtle nutty notes that add depth to its flavor profile.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of Innes Brick, it is best served at room temperature. This cheese makes a beautiful addition to any cheese platter, and can be paired with crusty bread, crackers, or fresh fruit. For the perfect pairing, try Innes Brick with a light and fruity white wine.
