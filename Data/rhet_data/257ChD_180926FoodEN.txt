

1) Description of Cheese: Kirkham's Lancashire is a renowned cheese made by Mrs Kirkhams and Neal's Yard Dairy in Goosnargh, Lancashire. It is a traditional cheese with a rich history, crafted by Graham Kirkham, a third-generation Lancashire-maker and original inventor.

2) Description of Product: This artisanal cheese is made from unpasteurized cow's milk, giving it a unique and authentic flavor. It has a semi-soft texture, with a buttery, creamy, and crumbly consistency that is perfect for spreading. The cheese has a natural yellow rind and is typically sold in wheels or blocks.

3) Description of Process: Kirkham's Lancashire is carefully prepared and aged for 2-3 months, allowing it to develop its distinct flavors. However, for those who prefer a more intense taste, it can be aged for up to 6 months, resulting in a soft, silky, and custard-like texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The cheese has a delightful aroma that is both creamy and tangy. Its flavors are a perfect balance of lemony, savory, and tangy notes, with a lingering Lancashire tang that will leave your taste buds wanting more.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Kirkham's Lancashire at room temperature to allow its full flavors to develop. This cheese is perfect for a cheese board, paired with crackers, fruits, and nuts. It also goes well with a Barbera, tawny port, or Sauternes/Barsac, enhancing its creamy and tangy taste.

6) Quality Assurance: Kirkham's Lancashire has been recognized for its exceptional quality, winning numerous awards and accolades. According to cheese expert, Juliet Harbutt, "Kirkham's Lancashire is in a class of its own." It has also undergone rigorous quality tests, ensuring that every bite is of the highest quality.
