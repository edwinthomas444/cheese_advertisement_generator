2) Description of Product:
This semi-soft, artisan cheese is made from the highest quality cow's milk, ensuring a rich and creamy texture. It is aged for over 14 months, resulting in a crumbly and flavorful cheese that weighs approximately 200 grams. The cheese has a pale yellow color and is coated with a natural rind, making it an aesthetically pleasing addition to any cheese board.

3) Description of Process:
Lake District Extra Mature Cheddar is carefully crafted using traditional methods and aged to perfection. The cheese is made from cow's milk and rennet, a natural enzyme that helps to curdle the milk and give the cheese its unique texture. The cheese is then aged for over 14 months, allowing it to develop a rich and complex flavor.

This cheese has a rich and inviting aroma, with hints of sweetness and a slight tang. The flavor is equally delightful, with a perfect balance of sweet and savory notes that linger on the palate. The texture is crumbly yet creamy, making it a versatile cheese for any occasion.
