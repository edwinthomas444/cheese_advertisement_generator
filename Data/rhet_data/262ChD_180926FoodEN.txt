

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing the award-winning Little Black Bomber, crafted by the renowned Snowdonia Cheese Company Limited in Wales. This extra mature Cheddar, originating from the Rhyl region in Great Britain, has a rich history of being loved by cheese connoisseurs worldwide.

2) Description of Product:
The Little Black Bomber is a hard cheese made from pasteurized cow's milk, with a creamy and smooth texture. It is coated in a layer of black wax, giving it a unique and eye-catching appearance. This delicious cheese is available in a variety of sizes and weights, perfect for any occasion. It is made with vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for all dietary preferences.

3) Description of Process:
Crafted with care and expertise, the Little Black Bomber undergoes a meticulous preparation process before being aged to perfection. The cheese is carefully monitored and turned regularly to ensure a consistent and balanced flavor. Aging for a minimum of 18 months allows the cheese to develop its intense and strong taste, making it a favorite among cheese lovers.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Upon unwrapping the black wax coating, the Little Black Bomber releases a rich and enticing aroma. The full-flavored, strong taste of this cheese is a delight to the senses. Its creamy and smooth texture adds to the overall experience, leaving a lasting impression on your taste buds.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving the Little Black Bomber at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese platter, accompanied by a crusty baguette and pickles. This cheese pairs perfectly with a variety of wines, such as a bold red or a crisp white, enhancing its flavor and complexity.

6) Quality Assurance:
Don't just take our word for it, the Little Black Bomber has won numerous awards, including the 2010 Silver Medal at the Nantwich International Cheese Show and the 2007 World Cheese Award Bronze winner. Its quality has also been tested and approved by cheese experts and critics, who have praised its unique taste and texture.
