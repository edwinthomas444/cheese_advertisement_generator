

1) Description of Cheese: Little Colonel, produced by James's Cheese, is a delicious semi-soft cheese with a rich history and geographical provenance. Originating from Dorset, England, this cheese is a proud representation of the artisanal cheese-making tradition of the region.

2) Description of Product: Little Colonel is a semi-soft cheese made from pasteurized cow's milk. Its shape is similar to a French Livarot, with a smooth and supple texture. The cheese is coated with a washed rind, giving it a beautiful pale yellow color. Each piece of Little Colonel weighs between 6-8 weeks, allowing it to develop a complex and sophisticated flavor.

3) Description of Process: Little Colonel is exclusively made by Lyburn Cheesemakers for James's Cheese. The cheese is prepared using traditional methods and then aged in Child Okeford for 6-8 weeks. This careful aging process results in a smooth and supple rind, and a harmony of flavors that make Little Colonel stand out from other cheeses.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Little Colonel has a pungent aroma that is sure to awaken your senses. Its spicy flavor is a perfect balance of richness and complexity, making it a truly unique cheese. With each bite, you will experience a burst of flavors that will leave you wanting more.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Little Colonel at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. It can be presented on a cheese board or paired with crackers and fruits for a delicious appetizer. This cheese also pairs well with a variety of wines, such as a bold red or a crisp white.

6) Quality Assurance: Little Colonel has received numerous accolades, including quotations and awards for its exceptional quality. It has also undergone rigorous quality tests to ensure that each piece of cheese meets the highest standards. You can trust that every bite of Little Colonel will be a truly exceptional experience.
