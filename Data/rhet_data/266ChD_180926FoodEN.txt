

1) Lyburn Garlic and Nettle - A Delicious Blend of Flavors from Lyburn Farm in England, Great Britain and the United Kingdom
Discover the unique taste of Lyburn Garlic and Nettle cheese, crafted by the expert cheese makers at Lyburn Farm. This award-winning cheese is a true representation of the rich history and geographical diversity of the British Isles.

2) A Semi-Soft Artisan Cheese Infused with Garlic, Nettle, and a Blend of Herbs and Spices
Indulge in the creamy and smooth texture of Lyburn Garlic and Nettle, made from pasteurized cow's milk and infused with the perfect combination of garlic, onion, chives, parsley, paprika, ginger, horseradish, and celery. This vegetarian-friendly cheese is coated with a washed rind and boasts a pale yellow color.

3) Crafted with Care - From Preparation to Aging
At Lyburn Farm, we take great pride in our cheese making process. Each batch of Lyburn Garlic and Nettle is carefully prepared and aged to perfection, ensuring a consistent and delicious flavor in every bite.

4) Aroma and Taste that Will Leave You Wanting More
Experience the aromatic and rich aroma of garlic and herbs as you take a bite of this cheese. The pronounced and spicy flavor is perfectly balanced with the creamy texture, creating a truly irresistible taste that will leave you wanting more.

5) Perfectly Paired - Serve Lyburn Garlic and Nettle at the Ideal Temperature and with the Perfect Complements
For the best experience, we recommend serving Lyburn Garlic and Nettle at room temperature, allowing the flavors to fully develop. This cheese is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a simple snack or a fancy dinner party. Pair it with crackers, fresh bread, or fruit, and for the ultimate indulgence, pair it with a glass of your favorite wine.

6) Award-Winning Quality - Recognized by Experts and Loved by Cheese Lovers
Don't just take our word for it, Lyburn Garlic and Nettle has been recognized for its exceptional quality and taste. It won the Silver award at the British Cheese Awards in 2010 and a Bronze award in 2014. Our cheese also undergoes rigorous quality tests to ensure that every batch meets our high standards.
