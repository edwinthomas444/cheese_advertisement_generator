

1) Description of Cheese:
Maisie's Kebbuck is a beloved cheese made by Humphrey Errington at Carnwath in Lanarkshire, Scotland. This artisanal, semi-hard white cheese is named after Humphrey's mother-in-law, Maisie, and has been produced by Errington Cheese Ltd. for generations. It is a true taste of Scotland and the United Kingdom.

2) Description of Product:
This traditional cheese is made from unpasteurized cow's milk, giving it a rich and authentic flavor. It has a creamy and crumbly texture, with a natural rind and a white color. The cheese is shaped and sized to perfection, weighing in at 29.8% fat content. It is made using vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of the Process:
Maisie's Kebbuck is crafted in a traditional, farmhouse manner by wrapping it in cloth and maturing it for 2-5 months. This unpressed style of cheesemaking dates back to before cheddaring was introduced, ensuring a truly authentic taste and texture. The cheese is carefully aged and foil wrapped to preserve its flavors.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Upon maturity, Maisie's Kebbuck has a sharp lactic smell with a hint of turnip. This unique aroma is a result of the traditional preparation methods used. The cheese has a tangy and slightly nutty taste, with a creamy outer edge and a crumbly interior. It pairs perfectly with a glass of whisky, bringing out the best of its flavors.

5) Serving Suggestions:
To truly savor the flavors of Maisie's Kebbuck, it is best served at room temperature. To enhance its presentation, it can be sliced and arranged on a cheese board with some crackers and fruits. This cheese pairs well with a variety of foods, but it is especially delicious with a glass of wine. Its tangy and creamy taste complements the flavors of red wine, making it the perfect addition to any cheese platter.

6) Quality Assurance:
Maisie's Kebbuck is a highly acclaimed cheese, with numerous awards and recognition. It has been praised for its unique taste and traditional preparation methods. The cheese has also undergone rigorous quality tests to ensure the highest standards of production. As Humphrey Errington himself said, "Kebbuck is not just a cheese, it's a tradition."
