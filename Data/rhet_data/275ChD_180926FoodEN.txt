

1) Description of Cheese:
May Hill Green is a delicious and award-winning soft cheese produced by Charles Martell & Son Limited in Dymock, Gloucestershire. This cheese has a rich history, originating in England, Great Britain and United Kingdom, and is made with pasteurized cow's milk.

2) Description of Product:
This artisan cheese is semi-soft in texture and has a unique leaf-wrapped rind. It is made using a mixture of rare-breed Gloucester and Friesian cattle milk, similar to the process of making Stinking Bishop cheese. The rind is topped with chopped nettles and coiled with a Beechwood lath, giving it a distinct character. Each wheel of May Hill Green is full fat and vegetarian, making it a versatile and inclusive choice for all cheese lovers.

3) Description of the Process:
The cheese-making process of May Hill Green involves careful preparation and aging. The milk is pasteurized and then combined with rennet, a natural enzyme used to coagulate the milk. The cheese is then aged to perfection, allowing for the development of its unique and delicious flavors.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
May Hill Green has a strong and inviting aroma, with a creamy and milky flavor. The peppery finish adds a delightful kick to the overall taste, making it a favorite among cheese enthusiasts. The semi-soft texture and runny consistency make it perfect for spreading on bread or crackers.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving May Hill Green at room temperature to allow the flavors to fully develop. This cheese is perfect for a cheeseboard or can be spread on flavored bread for a delicious snack. It also pairs well with a variety of foods, such as fruits, nuts, and cured meats. For a complete sensory experience, we suggest pairing it with a glass of your favorite wine.

6) Quality Assurance:
Don't just take our word for it - May Hill Green has received numerous awards and recognition for its exceptional quality and taste. It has been quoted as a "must-try" cheese by food critics and has undergone rigorous quality tests to ensure its excellence. Trust us, you won't be disappointed with this award-winning cheese.
