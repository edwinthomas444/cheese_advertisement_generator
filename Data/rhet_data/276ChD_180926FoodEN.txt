

1) Description of Cheese: Montgomery's Cheddar is a renowned artisan cheese, handcrafted by James Montgomery in Somerset, England. It is made from unpasteurized cow's milk, sourced from Montgomery's own herd of 200 Friesian cows. This cheese has a rich historical and geographical provenance, originating from the village of North Cadbury in Somerset.

2) Description of Product: Montgomery's Cheddar is a semi-hard cheese with a crumbly and grainy texture. It is available in various sizes and ageing periods, with most cheeses matured for 12 months on wooden shelves. The cheese has a natural rind and a vibrant yellow color. It is made with vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of the Process: Montgomery's Cheddar is made using traditional methods, ensuring its authenticity and unique flavor. The cheese is aged for 12 months in muslin cloth, while some varieties, such as the Extra Mature Cheddar, are aged for 18 months. This results in a special texture and flavor, with a slightly drier and nuttier taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Montgomery's Cheddar is described as grassy, rich, and smokey. It has a fruity and nutty flavor, with a hint of spiciness. The Extra Mature variety has a more intense nutty and peppery flavor. The oak-smoked Montgomery's Cheddar has a distinct smokey aroma that complements its nutty taste.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best taste experience, Montgomery's Cheddar should be served at room temperature. It can be enjoyed on its own, grated, or cooked in various dishes. This cheese is a perfect addition to any cheeseboard, and it pairs well with fruits, crackers, and cured meats. For a complete sensory experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Montgomery's Cheddar is a highly acclaimed cheese, with numerous awards and recognitions. It is one of the few truly traditional, handmade, unpasteurized Cheddar cheeses available. The Village Stores, the producers of Montgomery's Cheddar, take great pride in their cheese, ensuring the highest quality through regular quality tests. As James Montgomery himself says, "Our cheese is made with love and care, and it's this that makes it special."
