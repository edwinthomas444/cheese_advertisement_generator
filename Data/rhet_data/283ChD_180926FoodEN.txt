

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Old Winchester, a delicious cheese made by Lyburn Farm in the Landford region of England, Great Britain and United Kingdom. This cheese, also known as Old Smales, has a rich history and is loved by cheese connoisseurs around the world.

2) Description of Product:
Old Winchester is a hard, artisan cheese made from pasteurized cow's milk. It has a beautiful crystalline texture, with a dry and flaky consistency. The natural rind gives the cheese a beautiful yellow color. This cheese is made with vegetarian rennet, making it a perfect choice for vegetarians. Each wheel weighs approximately 18 months, giving it a full-flavored, sweet taste with a crunchy finish.

3) Description of Process:
Old Winchester is carefully prepared and aged for 18 months to achieve its perfect taste and texture. The cheese is made using traditional methods, resulting in a truly artisanal product. The aging process gives the cheese its characteristic nutty flavor, making it stand out among other hard cheeses.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Upon opening a wheel of Old Winchester, you will be greeted with a delightful aroma that will awaken your senses. The cheese has a unique combination of sweet and nutty flavors, with a crunchy texture that will leave you wanting more. Its full-flavored taste makes it a perfect addition to any cheese board.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving Old Winchester at room temperature. This allows the cheese to fully develop its flavors and aromas. When presenting this cheese, we suggest cutting it into thin slices to showcase its beautiful yellow color and texture. Old Winchester pairs perfectly with a variety of foods, including crackers, fruits, and cured meats. For a complete experience, we recommend pairing it with a full-bodied red wine.

6) Quality Assurance:
Old Winchester has won numerous awards, including Best Modern British Cheese in 2006 and Reserve Champion Cheese at the Bath and West in 2007. It has also consistently received one, two, or three stars at the Great Taste Awards. These accolades are a testament to the quality and taste of this exceptional cheese. As quoted by one of our satisfied customers, "Old Winchester is the best cheese I have ever tasted." Try it for yourself and see why it has won the hearts of cheese lovers all over the world.
