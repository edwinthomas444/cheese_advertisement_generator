

1) Description of Cheese:
Orkney Mature Cheddar is a premium cheese crafted by The Orkney Cheese Company Ltd, known for their dedication to producing high-quality dairy products. This cheese has a rich history, originating from the Orkney Isles in Ireland and the United Kingdom. Its traditional recipe has been passed down through generations, making it a beloved cheese among cheese connoisseurs.

2) Description of Product:
This artisanal cheese is made from the finest pasteurized cow's milk, giving it a creamy and smooth texture. It comes in a variety of shapes and sizes, ranging from small blocks to large wedges. The natural rind adds to its rustic appeal. The cheese is firm to the touch, yet soft and malleable, making it easy to slice or grate. It is also coated with a layer of vegetable color pigment, giving it a vibrant yellow hue. The cheese weighs between 6 to 18 months, allowing for a variety of flavor profiles.

3) Description of Process:
Orkney Mature Cheddar is carefully crafted using traditional methods, ensuring the highest quality. The milk is sourced exclusively from cows in the Orkney Isles, giving the cheese a unique and distinctive flavor. The cheese is then aged for 6 to 18 months, allowing it to develop its rich and complex taste. During this time, it is carefully monitored and turned regularly to ensure an even distribution of flavor.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
This cheese has a clean and nutty aroma, characteristic of a well-aged Cheddar. Its flavor profile is a perfect balance of creamy, mellow, and savory notes with a slight sharpness. The cheese has a bright and even color, with a smooth and silky texture that melts in your mouth. It is a versatile cheese that can be enjoyed on its own or added to your favorite dishes.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best taste experience, serve Orkney Mature Cheddar at room temperature. This allows the cheese to reach its full potential, releasing its rich and complex flavors. It can be presented on a cheeseboard, sliced or grated on top of your favorite dishes. This cheese pairs well with a variety of foods, including fruits, crackers, and cured meats. For a truly indulgent experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine.
