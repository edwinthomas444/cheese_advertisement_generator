
1) Description of Cheese: Orkney Medium Coloured Cheddar from The Orkney Cheese Company Ltd is a semi-hard, artisan cheese made from pasteurized cow's milk. It hails from the Orkney Isles, a group of islands located between Ireland and the United Kingdom. With a rich history and unique geographical provenance, this cheese is truly one-of-a-kind.

2) Description of Product: Orkney Medium Coloured Cheddar has a close, firm, and smooth texture with a natural rind. It is a yellow cheese with a mellow and savory flavor, and a clean aroma. This cheese is available in various sizes and weights, with the option of a white or coloured paste. The bright colour is achieved by adding a vegetable colour pigment after pasteurization. It is made using only the finest cow's milk and rennet.

3) Description of Process: Orkney Medium Coloured Cheddar is crafted using traditional methods and aged for 6 to 18 months. This allows for a variety of flavor profiles to develop, giving each cheese a unique taste. The cheese is carefully monitored and turned during the aging process to ensure a smooth and closed texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Orkney Medium Coloured Cheddar has a clean aroma and a mellow, nutty flavor with moderate levels of sharp acid notes. The taste is well-rounded and savory, making it a crowd-pleaser on any cheeseboard. Its smooth and firm texture adds to the overall sensory experience of this delicious cheese.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best taste experience, serve Orkney Medium Coloured Cheddar at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheeseboard with crackers, fruits, and nuts, or used to elevate sandwiches and other dishes. This cheese pairs well with a variety of wines, such as Chardonnay or Pinot Noir, and complements a range of foods, from meats to vegetables.
