

1) Pant-Ys-Gawn: A Unique Goat's Milk Cheese from Abergavenny, Wales by Abergavenny Fine Foods
Pant-Ys-Gawn, also known as Pantysgawn or Pant-Ysgawn, is a one-of-a-kind cheese hailing from the beautiful region of Abergavenny in Monmouthshire, Wales. Crafted by Tony Craske on his farm, this cheese has a rich history and a strong connection to its geographical origins.

2) Soft, Creamy and Salty: The Perfect Combination in Every Bite
Pant-Ys-Gawn is a fresh, artisan cheese made from pasteurized goat's milk sourced from the Brecon Beacons National Park. Available in logs of 1 kg or 150 grams, this rindless cheese is coated in either black peppercorns or mixed herbs, adding a unique twist to its flavor profile. With a fat content of 22.8 g/100g, this cheese is an indulgent treat for any cheese lover.

3) A Labor of Love: Crafted with Care and Aged to Perfection
The process of making Pant-Ys-Gawn involves careful preparation and aging, resulting in a cheese that is bursting with flavor. The goat's milk is pasteurized before being transformed into a soft and creamy cheese. It is then aged to perfection, allowing the flavors to develop and mature.

4) A Symphony of Flavors and Aromas: Herbaceous, Salty, Sour and Spicy
Pant-Ys-Gawn is a cheese that excites the senses with its unique blend of flavors and aromas. The herbaceous notes are balanced perfectly with a hint of saltiness, followed by a subtle sourness reminiscent of lemon. The cheese finishes off with a spicy kick, making it a truly unforgettable experience.

5) Perfectly Paired: Versatile and Delicious in Every Way
This cheese is best enjoyed at room temperature, allowing the flavors to fully develop. It can be served as a snack, grilled, spread on bread or crackers, or used in salads for a burst of flavor. Pair it with a glass of white wine or a crisp cider for a truly delightful experience.

6) Quality You Can Trust: Award-Winning and Highly Acclaimed
Pant-Ys-Gawn has received numerous accolades and awards, solidifying its position as a top-quality cheese. It has been praised by cheese connoisseurs and critics alike, making it a must-try for any cheese lover. With strict quality control measures in place, you can trust that every bite of Pant-Ys-Gawn is of the highest quality.
