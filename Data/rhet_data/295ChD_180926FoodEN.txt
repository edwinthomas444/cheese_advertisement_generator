

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing the rich and full-flavored Red Leicester cheese, produced by the renowned Leicestershire Handmade Cheese Company and Taw Valley Creamery in the United Kingdom. This traditional hard English cheese has a long history, dating back to the 17th century in the Leicestershire region.

2) Description of Product:
Red Leicester is a hard, artisan cheese made from unpasteurized cow's milk. It is shaped into a round wheel and has a crumbly and dense texture. The cheese is coated with a cloth wrap, giving it a distinctive orange color. With a weight of approximately 5-6 pounds, Red Leicester is a perfect addition to any cheese platter.

3) Description of Process:
The cheese-making process of Red Leicester is steeped in tradition. The farmers in Leicestershire recognized the need to differentiate their cheese from others and thus, began coloring it with annatto, a natural vegetable dye. The cheese is then aged for 6-9 months, resulting in a rich and robust flavor.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of Red Leicester is rich and inviting, while the taste is a perfect balance of burnt caramel and sweetness. As the cheese ages, it develops a more intense and complex flavor, making it a true delight for cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving Red Leicester at room temperature to allow its full flavor to develop. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers, fruits, and nuts, or melted over a slice of warm toast. This cheese pairs beautifully with full-bodied white wines such as Muscadet, Sancerre Chenin Blanc, and Vouvray.

6) Quality Assurance:
Don't just take our word for it, Red Leicester has received numerous awards and accolades, including the prestigious Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) status. Its quality has been recognized by cheese connoisseurs and experts alike, making it a must-try for any cheese lover. As one satisfied customer put it, "Red Leicester is a true taste of England's finest cheese."
