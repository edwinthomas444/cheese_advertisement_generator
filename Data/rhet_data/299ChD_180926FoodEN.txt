

1) Description of Cheese: Sharpham Savour is a delicious and award-winning cheese produced by Sharpham Wine & Cheese, a renowned producer in England, Great Britain and the United Kingdom. Its roots can be traced back to the beautiful region of Devon, where it is made with great care and expertise.

2) Description of Product: This artisan cheese is made from a blend of unpasteurized cow's and goat's milk, giving it a unique and rich flavor. It is a semi-hard cheese with a close and creamy texture, coated with a natural rind. Each round weighs 1.3kg and is available in a convenient size for all cheese lovers.

3) Description of Process: Sharpham Savour is made using traditional methods, without any pressing involved. The milk is carefully selected and mixed, and then left to age for the perfect amount of time. This process results in a cheese that is full of flavor and character.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Sharpham Savour is fresh and inviting, with hints of sweetness. When tasted, the cheese boasts a smooth and sweet flavor that is sure to please any palate. Its mellow taste makes it a versatile cheese that can be enjoyed on its own or paired with other foods.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, Sharpham Savour should be served at room temperature to fully appreciate its flavor and texture. It can be presented on a cheese board or sliced onto crackers for a delicious snack. This cheese pairs beautifully with fruit pastes and is best enjoyed with a glass of wine, such as a crisp white or a light red.

6) Quality Assurance: Sharpham Savour has been recognized for its exceptional quality, winning awards such as silver at Taste of the West 2014 and bronze at British Cheese Awards 2013 & 2014. These accolades are a testament to the dedication and expertise of Sharpham Wine & Cheese in producing a top-notch product. Try Sharpham Savour today and taste the difference for yourself!
