

1) Description of Cheese: Ragstone by Charlie Westhead, a delicious and unique cheese from the British Isles, with a rich historical and geographical provenance.

2) Description of Product: This English goat log is a ripened, pasteurised cheese that boasts a mellow and creamy texture. It is a soft cheese, made with goats' milk, sea salt, starter cultures, animal rennet, and mould cultures.

3) Description of the Process: Ragstone is hand-crafted by Charlie Westhead himself at his dairy in Herefordshire. The curd is set overnight and then hand-ladled into log-shaped moulds. After two to three weeks, the cheese develops a creamy texture and a lemony flavor.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Ragstone has a delightful aroma and a complex, tangy taste. The addition of white mould gives it a unique twist on the traditional French Sainte Maure, making it a must-try for cheese lovers.
