

1) Description of Cheese: Our Fine Cheese Co. Cheddar is a true British classic, produced by a renowned cheese manufacturer. This cheese has a rich history and is made in the British Isles, using traditional methods passed down through generations.

2) Description of Product: Our Cheddar is a hard cheese with a sweet and tangy flavor. It has a creamy texture and is occasionally dotted with crystals, a result of its long maturing process. This cheese is made from pasteurized cow's milk, with a touch of salt, microbial rennet, and starter culture. It is available in a variety of sizes and weights, perfect for any occasion.

3) Description of Process: Our Cheddar is made using traditional methods, ensuring the highest quality and taste. It is then aged for a minimum of 14 months, allowing it to develop a full, sweet flavor with a tangy bite. After grading, the cheese is formed and dipped in burgundy wax, giving it a beautiful appearance and preserving its flavor.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The Fine Cheese Co. Cheddar has a powerful and tangy flavor, with a creamy finish. Its aroma is rich and inviting, making it a delight for the senses. This cheese is perfect for those who appreciate a strong and flavorful Cheddar.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy our Cheddar, we recommend serving it at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers, fruits, and nuts for a delicious snack. It also pairs well with a variety of foods, such as sandwiches, grilled cheese, and mac and cheese. For a perfect pairing, we suggest enjoying it with a glass of red wine.
