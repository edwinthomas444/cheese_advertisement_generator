

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing ﻿Sinodun Hill, a fabulous goats’ milk cheese from Oxfordshire, made by Fraser Norton and Rachel Yarrow. This cheese has a rich history, rooted in the British Isles, and is a testament to the passion and dedication of its makers.

2) Description of Product:
Sinodun Hill is a soft cheese with a delicate, smooth, and complex flavor. It is made from raw goats' milk, with a touch of salt, starter cultures, and vegetarian rennet - Thistle. This cheese is individually crafted, ensuring the highest quality and attention to detail. It comes in a variety of sizes and weights, making it a versatile choice for any occasion.

3) Description of the Process:
The process of making Sinodun Hill is a labor of love. The milk is sourced from their own herd of 24 milking goats, and the cheese is made using a slow, lactic set recipe. This preserves the natural flavors of the milk and gives the cheese its unique texture. The cheese is then aged to perfection, developing a beautiful, wrinkly coat with natural spots of blues, greys, and greens.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Sinodun Hill has a sweet, almondy aroma that is sure to entice your senses. The taste is equally delightful, with a rich and complex flavor that is both creamy and tangy. The texture is smooth and mouth-filling, without being cloying or claggy.

5) Serving Suggestions:
To fully enjoy the flavors of Sinodun Hill, we recommend serving it at room temperature. Its unique appearance makes it a beautiful addition to any cheese platter, and it pairs perfectly with a variety of foods. Try it with crackers, fruits, or even drizzled with honey. For a complete experience, pair it with a crisp white wine or a fruity red.
