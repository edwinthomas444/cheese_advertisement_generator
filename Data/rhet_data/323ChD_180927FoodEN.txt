

1) Description of Cheese: Introducing the Fine Cheese Co. Ewes' Milk Cheddar, a delicious and unique cheese made in Dorset, England. Our company has a long history of producing high-quality cheeses, and this new addition is no exception. Made with delicate ewes' milk and traditional Cheddar methods, this cheese is a true representation of the lush pastures of the South West.

2) Description of Product: This hard cheese comes in a convenient 200g size, perfect for individual consumption. Its shape is round and its texture is firm, with a slight crumble. The cheese is coated in a natural rind, giving it a rustic appearance. Made with 100% ewes' milk and vegetarian rennet, this cheese is suitable for all dietary preferences.

3) Description of Process: Hand-made in Dorset, our Ewes' Milk Cheddar follows the same traditional methods as our English Farmhouse Cheddar. The cheese is matured for six months, allowing for a mellow and slightly sweet flavor to develop. This aging process also gives the cheese a complex and balanced finish.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The delicate ewes' milk gives this cheese a unique and aromatic flavor, with hints of the South West's lush pastures. The taste is fresh and bright, with a long and complex finish. Its aroma is equally delightful, making it a sensory experience for cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best taste experience, we recommend serving our Ewes' Milk Cheddar at room temperature. Its rustic appearance makes it a beautiful addition to any cheese platter, and it pairs perfectly with crackers and fruit. For a more indulgent experience, try it melted on top of a warm baguette. For wine pairing, we suggest a light and fruity white wine, such as a Sauvignon Blanc.

6) Quality Assurance: Our Ewes' Milk Cheddar has received high praise from cheese experts and consumers alike. It has been awarded multiple accolades, including the prestigious Great Taste Award. We take pride in our quality assurance, and all of our cheeses undergo rigorous testing to ensure the highest standards are met.
