

1) Description of Cheese: Renegade Monk is a bold and unique cheese made by Marcus Fergusson at Feltham's Farm in Somerset, United Kingdom. Marcus, a former music industry professional, began making cheese in 2016 and started selling it to the public in 2017.

2) Description of Product: This bold little cheese is bathed in ale and has a washed rind. It is made with blue cultures and is a blend of cow's milk, salt, starter culture, ripening cultures, vegetarian rennet, and ale. It weighs approximately 100g and has a distinctive shape and texture.

3) Description of Process: Renegade Monk is made in small batches using organic milk sourced from a local farm. The cheese is prepared and aged with care, resulting in a wild cheese that is full of flavor.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The pungent aroma of this cheese is balanced by a pleasant sweetness and a subtle blue undertone. Its assertive flavor is complemented by a slightly more floral taste than other washed rind cheeses.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best taste experience, serve Renegade Monk at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers and fruit, or used in cooking to add a unique twist to dishes. This cheese pairs well with a variety of wines, such as a fruity red or a crisp white.

It has been awarded multiple awards for its bold and unique flavor, and has undergone rigorous quality tests to ensure its excellence.
