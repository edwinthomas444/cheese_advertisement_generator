

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Flower Marie, a delicately crafted cheese made in East Sussex by Kevin and Alison Blunt. This cheese has a rich history, created by the late James Aldridge and named after Ann-Marie Dyas, founder of The Fine Cheese Co. Its roots in the British Isles make it a true representation of traditional English cheese.

2) Description of Product:
Flower Marie is a ripened, unpasteurised ewes' milk cheese with a white bloomy rind. Its soft texture and delicate coating make it a visually appealing addition to any cheese board. This cheese is available in individual portions, making it a convenient and indulgent treat.

3) Description of Process:
This cheese undergoes a meticulous preparation and aging process, resulting in a perfectly ripened and flavorful cheese. The milk used is sourced from local ewes, and traditional rennet is used to give it a distinct taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of Flower Marie is a delightful blend of earthy and creamy notes. Its taste is equally exquisite, with a rich and buttery flavor that lingers on the palate. The white bloomy rind adds a subtle tanginess to the overall taste, making it a unique and memorable experience.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Flower Marie at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. Its delicate appearance makes it a beautiful addition to any cheese platter, and it pairs perfectly with fresh fruits and crackers. For a truly indulgent experience, pair it with a crisp white wine.

6) Quality Assurance:
Don't just take our word for it, Flower Marie has been awarded the gold medal at the 2017 Artisan Cheese Awards. Its quality has been recognized and praised by cheese experts and enthusiasts alike. Rest assured, this cheese has undergone rigorous quality tests to ensure its excellence. As one satisfied customer put it, "Flower Marie is a true gem in the world of cheese."
