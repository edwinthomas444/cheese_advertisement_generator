

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing the ﻿Stinking Bishop Baby, a truly unique cheese crafted by Charles Martell on his farm in Gloucestershire, in the British Isles. This cheese has a rich history and geographical provenance, making it a must-try for cheese lovers.

2) Description of Product:
The ﻿Stinking Bishop Baby is a washed rind and aromatic cheese, made from the milk of cows. It has a distinctive shape and size, with a creamy texture and a coating of perry (fermented pear juice) made from Stinking Bishop pears. This cheese weighs in at a perfect size for individual servings.

3) Description of the Process:
Crafted with care, the ﻿Stinking Bishop Baby goes through a unique preparation process. The rind is washed with perry, giving it its rich and powerful aroma and flavor. It is then aged to perfection, allowing the flavors to develop and intensify.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The ﻿Stinking Bishop Baby has a strong and distinct aroma, thanks to the perry wash. Its flavor is equally powerful, with a rich and creamy taste that will leave your taste buds wanting more. The perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess makes this cheese a true delight for the senses.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve the ﻿Stinking Bishop Baby at room temperature. Its unique shape and size make it a great addition to any cheese platter, or it can be enjoyed on its own. Pair it with fresh fruits, crackers, and a glass of crisp white wine for a perfect combination.
