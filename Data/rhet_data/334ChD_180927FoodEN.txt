

1) Description of Cheese: Beenleigh Blue is an award-winning cheese made from ewes' milk in Devon, England. It is produced by skilled cheesemakers who have been perfecting their craft for generations in the British Isles.

2) Description of Product: This blue cheese has a crumbly texture and a complex, fruity flavor. It is shaped into a round wheel and coated in waxed paper, with each piece being hand-cut and wrapped by our trained cheesemongers. Made from ewes' milk, it is vegetarian-friendly and uses traditional rennet.

3) Description of Process: Every piece of Beenleigh Blue is prepared and aged with care, ensuring the highest quality and flavor. Our cheesemakers follow traditional methods to create this unique cheese, resulting in a truly exceptional product.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Beenleigh Blue is distinctive, with hints of earthiness and sweetness. Its flavor is rich and complex, with a perfect balance of saltiness and sweetness. The crumbly texture adds to the overall experience, making it a delight for the senses.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully appreciate the flavors of Beenleigh Blue, we recommend serving it at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers and fruits, or crumbled over salads for a delicious addition. For the perfect pairing, try it with a glass of red wine or a crisp white.
