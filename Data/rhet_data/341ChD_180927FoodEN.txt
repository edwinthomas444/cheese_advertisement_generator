

1) Description of Cheese: Old Winchester by Mike and Judy Smales is a modern British classic, originating from their farm in the scenic New Forest. This cheese is a unique blend of Gouda, Parmesan, and Cheddar, making it a one-of-a-kind creation by the Smales family.

2) Description of Product: Old Winchester is a hard cheese with a powerful, fruity flavor and a full, sweet taste. It is made from 98% cow's milk, with added salt, microbial rennet, starter, and Lysosyme. It is matured for a minimum of 16 months, resulting in a fabulous crystalline texture.

3) Description of Process: Based on a Gouda recipe, the curds are washed after draining, removing lactic acid and giving the cheese a sweeter taste and longer shelf life. The cheese is then aged for over a year, resulting in a richer depth of flavor and Parmesan-like characteristics.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Old Winchester has a full, fruity aroma and an underlying sweetness. On the palate, it offers a wonderful crunch from the crystalline finish, along with a rich, buttery taste.
