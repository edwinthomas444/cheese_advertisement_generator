

1) Description of Cheese: Devon Oke is a delicious, artisanal cheese made by Rachel Stephens on her farm in Stockbeare, in the British Isles. This cheese has a rich history dating back to the 17th century and is thought to be even older than Cheddar cheese. It takes its name from the nearby village of Okehampton, adding to its unique and traditional charm.

2) Description of Product: Devon Oke is a firm, buttery cheese with a natural rind and a deep, sunshine-yellow interior. It is a hard cheese, made from cows' milk and seasoned with salt, animal rennet, starter culture, and lard. Each 100g serving of this cheese provides 1845KJ of energy and 445Kcal, making it a nutritious and satisfying snack.

3) Description of Process: This cheese is carefully crafted using traditional methods and aged to perfection. Rachel Stephens discovered the recipe for Devon Oke only 20 years ago, and she has since perfected the art of making this cheese. The curds are washed during the preparation process, resulting in a mellow and sweet flavor that sets it apart from other cheeses.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Devon Oke has a unique and inviting aroma that will awaken your senses. Its smooth yet firm texture and creamy flavor make it a delight to eat. The cheese has a mellow and sweet taste, making it a favorite among cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, we recommend serving Devon Oke at room temperature, allowing its flavors to fully develop. This cheese can be presented on a cheese board, sliced into cubes, or grated over your favorite dishes. It pairs well with crackers, bread, and fruit, and is the perfect addition to any cheese platter. For a complete experience, pair it with a glass of red wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Devon Oke has been recognized for its exceptional quality and taste. As quoted by Rachel Stephens, "It's a cheese that has stood the test of time." This cheese has also won numerous awards, solidifying its reputation as one of the best artisanal cheeses in the British Isles. We take pride in ensuring that every batch of Devon Oke meets our high-quality standards, providing our customers with a truly exceptional cheese.
