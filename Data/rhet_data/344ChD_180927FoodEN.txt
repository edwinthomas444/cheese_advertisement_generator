

1) Description of Cheese: Baronet is a proud creation of Julianna Sedli, an English cheese maker with over 8 years of experience. Inspired by the French Classic Reblochon, this cheese is a testament to the fusion of British and French cheese-making techniques. 

2) Description of Product: Baronet is a soft cheese made from unpasteurized organic Jersey cow's milk, giving it a rich and creamy flavor. It is molded into a round shape with a delicate white rind, weighing around 250 grams. The cheese is made using traditional rennet, ensuring its authenticity and quality.

3) Description of Process: Baronet is carefully prepared by hand using a combination of English and French techniques, giving it a unique and complex taste. It is then aged for a minimum of 6 weeks, allowing the flavors to develop and mature, resulting in a smooth and buttery texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Baronet has a distinct aroma of earthy mushrooms and a hint of nuttiness. Its taste is a perfect balance of creamy and tangy, with a subtle sweetness that lingers on the palate. The texture is soft and velvety, making it a delight to eat.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully appreciate the flavors of Baronet, it is best served at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers and fruits, or melted on top of a warm baguette. It pairs well with a variety of foods, such as cured meats, figs, and honey. For a complete experience, pair it with a glass of dry white wine.

6) Quality Assurance: "Judy Schadi, an American cheese maker, was so impressed with Julianna's skills that she named a cheese after her." This speaks volumes about the quality and authenticity of Baronet. It has also won several awards, including the prestigious British Cheese Award. Quality tests have consistently shown that Baronet meets the highest standards of taste and texture, making it a must-try for all cheese lovers.
