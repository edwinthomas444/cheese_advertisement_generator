

1) Description of Cheese:
Berkswell Cheese, made by the Fletchers at Ram Hall in the Heart of England, has a rich history dating back to 1989. It is an award-winning cheese, having been named the 2017 Artisan Cheese Awards Supreme Champion, Best English Cheese, and Best Farmhouse Cheese.

2) Description of Product:
This hard cheese is made from unpasteurized ewes' milk, giving it a unique and delicious flavor. It has a distinctive ridged shape, which is a result of being drained in traditional kitchen colanders. The cheese is coated with a co-polymer cheese coating and weighs approximately 100g.

3) Description of Process:
Berkswell cheese takes at least four months to mature, allowing for the perfect balance of flavors to develop. The Fletchers take great care in looking after their sheep, ensuring the highest quality milk for their cheese. By lambing their sheep in succession, they have overcome the issue of a dip in milk production.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Upon first opening the package, you will be greeted with a fruity tang. As you take a bite, you will experience a creamy finish with hints of caramel and a definite tang. Similar in style to a Pecorino, this cheese is sweet and nutty, making it a true delight for the taste buds.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the ultimate experience, serve this cheese at room temperature to allow the flavors to fully develop. It makes a beautiful addition to any cheese board, and pairs perfectly with a variety of foods, such as crackers, fruits, and cured meats. For a complete experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine.

6) Quality Assurance:
Berkswell cheese has received numerous awards for its exceptional quality and taste. In addition to being named the 2017 Artisan Cheese Awards Supreme Champion, it has also been recognized as the Best English Cheese and Best Farmhouse Cheese. With its high-quality ingredients and careful aging process, you can trust that you are getting the best of the best with each bite of this delicious cheese.
