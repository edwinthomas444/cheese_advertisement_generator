

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing the delectable Farm Single Gloucester Cheese by Godsells - a true taste of Gloucestershire. This cheese has a rich history, being made exclusively on Gloucestershire farms and is a product of the renowned Godsells family. Made just ten miles from our shop, this cheese is a true local delicacy.

2) Description of Product:
This medium-flavoured cheese is a treat for the taste buds. Available in sizes of 250g, 500g, 750g, and 1kg, you can choose the perfect amount for your needs. Made without any artificial coloring, this cheese is slightly less mature than its Double Gloucester counterpart. It is also made with vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for all dietary preferences.

3) Description of Process:
The Farm Single Gloucester Cheese is prepared and aged with utmost care and expertise. Made exclusively in Gloucestershire, this cheese is crafted by the Godsells family at Leonard Stanley. This traditional method of production ensures the highest quality and authenticity of the cheese.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The Farm Single Gloucester Cheese has a distinct aroma and taste that sets it apart from other cheeses. With a smooth texture and a medium flavor profile, it is a perfect balance of creaminess and tanginess. The absence of artificial coloring allows the natural flavors to shine through, making it a true delight for the senses.

5) Serving Suggestions:
To truly savor the flavors of this cheese, we recommend serving it at room temperature. For an elegant presentation, pair it with some crackers and seasonal fruits. This versatile cheese also pairs well with a variety of foods, from sandwiches to pasta dishes. For a complete culinary experience, we suggest pairing it with a glass of your favorite red wine.
