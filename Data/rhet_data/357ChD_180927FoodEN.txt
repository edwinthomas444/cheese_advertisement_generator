

1) Description of Cheese: Introducing our White Stilton Cheese, a beloved classic from the United Kingdom. 2) Description of Product: Our White Stilton Cheese is a unique variation of the traditional Stilton, with a crumbly texture and a mild flavor profile. Unlike its blue-veined counterpart, this cheese does not have any blue streaks, making it a perfect choice for those who prefer a milder taste. It comes in four convenient sizes - 250g, 500g, 750g, and 1kg - and is coated with a protective layer to ensure freshness. Made with vegetarian rennet, this cheese is suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of Process: Our White Stilton Cheese is made using a meticulous process, starting with the highest quality milk and vegetarian rennet. The cheese is carefully prepared and aged to perfection, resulting in its distinct crumbly texture and delicate flavor. 4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of our White Stilton Cheese is subtle yet inviting, with hints of cream and butter. Its taste is creamy and mild, with a slight tanginess that adds depth to its flavor.
