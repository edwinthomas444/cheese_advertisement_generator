

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Cornish Yarg Cheese, a firm and mild farmhouse cheese made by the original makers, the Gray family, in Cornwall. This cheese has a rich history dating back to the thirteenth century and is made on a farm near Liskeard, rented from the Duchy of Cornwall.

2) Description of Product:
Cornish Yarg Cheese is a unique cheese coated in edible nettle leaves, giving it a distinct appearance. It comes in various sizes, including 250g, 500g, 750g, and 1kg, making it perfect for any occasion. This cheese is made with vegetarian rennet, ensuring it is suitable for all dietary preferences.

3) Description of Process:
The process of making Cornish Yarg Cheese involves covering it in nettle leaves, a traditional method of keeping it moist and free from flies. The leaves are frozen after picking to remove the sting, making them safe to eat. This cheese also develops an edible white mould as it ages, adding to its unique character.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
When you unwrap Cornish Yarg Cheese, you will be greeted with a delightful aroma that will awaken your taste buds. Its mild and creamy flavor is complemented by the tanginess of the nettle leaves, making it a perfect balance of flavors. The longer it ages, the stronger the taste becomes, making it a must-try for cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Cornish Yarg Cheese at room temperature to allow the flavors to fully develop. Its unique appearance makes it a great addition to any cheese board, and it pairs perfectly with crackers, fruits, and chutneys. For a complete experience, pair it with a crisp white wine or a full-bodied red.
