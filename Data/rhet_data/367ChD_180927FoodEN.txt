

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Dorset Blue Vinny Cheese, a truly unique and flavorful cheese that has been enjoyed for centuries. This cheese is made by Woodbridge Farm near Sturminster Newton, the only farm in the country that still produces it. Its rich history and geographical provenance make it a truly special and sought-after cheese.

2) Description of Product:
Dorset Blue Vinny Cheese is known for its strong and heavily blued flavor, making it even bolder than Stilton. It comes in various sizes, including 250g, 500g, 750g, and 1kg, and each one is priced at an affordable £7.25, £14.50, £21.75, and £29.00 respectively. Made with vegetarian rennet, this cheese has a unique texture and coating, with a fat content of only 15%. 

3) Description of Process:
The process of making Dorset Blue Vinny Cheese is truly special. The milk used is skimmed, and then the cheese is aged to perfection. This traditional method of preparation gives it a distinct flavor and texture that cannot be replicated by mass-produced cheeses.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Dorset Blue Vinny Cheese has a strong and pungent smell, with hints of blue mold and aged milk. Its taste is equally bold, with a sharp and tangy flavor that will leave your taste buds wanting more. The cheese varies in color and may have brown patches, but rest assured, the taste is always exceptional.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving Dorset Blue Vinny Cheese at room temperature. Its unique texture and bold flavor make it a perfect addition to any cheese platter. Pair it with crackers, fruits, and nuts for a delicious snack, or use it to elevate your favorite dishes. This cheese also pairs well with a variety of wines, including reds and whites.
