

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing our Medium Cornish Brie Cheese, made by a renowned manufacturer in Cornwall, England, known for their expertise in producing high-quality cheeses. This cheese has a rich history, originating from the beautiful and fertile region of Cornwall.

2) Description of Product:
Our Medium Cornish Brie Cheese is a creamy, medium-flavoured cheese sold in whole wheels that are approximately 15cm / 6" in diameter. It has a smooth texture and is coated with a thin layer of edible rind. This cheese weighs around 1kg and is made with vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of Process:
Our Brie Cheese is prepared using traditional methods, with a keen focus on quality and taste. It is aged to perfection, allowing it to ripen and develop its distinct flavour. We take great care in ensuring that each wheel of cheese is of the highest quality before it reaches your plate.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The Medium Cornish Brie Cheese has a delightful aroma that is both creamy and slightly tangy. Its taste is rich and buttery, with a subtle hint of earthiness. The cheese has a smooth and velvety texture that melts in your mouth, leaving a lingering, satisfying taste.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving our Brie Cheese at room temperature, allowing its flavours to fully develop. It can be presented on a cheese board, accompanied by crackers, fruits, and nuts. This cheese pairs perfectly with a crisp white wine or a light red wine, enhancing its creamy and tangy flavours.
