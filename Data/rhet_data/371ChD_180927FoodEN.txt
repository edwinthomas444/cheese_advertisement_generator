

1) Description of Cheese: Chatel Cheese from Cornwall's Creamery
Chatel Cheese is a premium, artisanal cheese produced by the renowned creamery in Cornwall. This cheese has a rich history, with its roots dating back to the local farms in Cornwall. The name "Chatel" is derived from the French word "Cattle", paying homage to the cows that produce the milk used in this cheese.

2) Description of Product: Creamy and Delicious 200g Brie
Indulge in the creamy goodness of Chatel Cheese, a 200g individual Brie made with double cream. This soft, mould-ripened cheese has a velvety texture and a golden straw-colored body. It is made using full-fat cows' milk from local farms, along with Cornish double cream, salt, vegetable rennet, and starter cultures. This vegetarian-friendly cheese is a true delight for the taste buds.

3) Description of Process: Handcrafted and Ripened to Perfection
The production of Chatel Cheese involves a meticulous process of handcrafting and aging. The cheese is prepared using traditional methods, and then it is ripened for ten days in special ripening rooms. This careful process results in a mild and creamy cheese with a soft, melting texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Heavenly Aroma and Rich Flavor
Chatel Cheese has a heavenly aroma that will entice your senses. Its rich flavor is a perfect balance of creamy and tangy notes, making it a true delight for cheese lovers. As the cheese matures, it develops a velvety texture and a golden straw-colored body, enhancing its taste even further.

5) Serving Suggestions: Perfect for Any Occasion

Serve it on a cheeseboard, in a sandwich, or use it in your favorite recipes. It pairs well with a variety of foods and wines, making it a must-have for any occasion.
