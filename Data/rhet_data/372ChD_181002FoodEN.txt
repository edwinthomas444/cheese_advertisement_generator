

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Village Gossip Cheese, a delicious creation from the Godsells family, made in the quaint village of Gloucester. This cheese is named after Sir Stanley Spencer, a former resident of the village, and his famous painting "Village Gossips" which depicts a group of villagers and himself enjoying the company of his wife and lady friend. 

2) Description of Product:
Village Gossip Cheese is a strong and flavorful Gloucester cheese, infused with generous amounts of chives. It comes in four sizes - 250g, 500g, 750g, and 1kg, priced at £7.25, £14.50, £21.75, and £29.00 respectively. This cheese is made using vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of Process:
The Godsells family takes great care in preparing and aging this cheese, ensuring its quality and taste. It is made less than ten miles from their shop, using traditional methods and techniques passed down through generations.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Upon opening the package, you will be greeted with a delightful aroma of chives and cheese. The taste is bold and robust, with the perfect balance of tanginess and creaminess. The chives add a unique and refreshing flavor to the cheese, making it a must-try for all cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving Village Gossip Cheese at room temperature. This allows the flavors to fully develop and enhances the texture. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers and fruits, or used in cooking to elevate your dishes. Pair it with a crisp white wine or a full-bodied red for a perfect combination.
