

1) Description of Cheese: Cockadilly Chilli Cheese - Godsells Farm
This unique cheese is made by the Godsells family, who have been producing exceptional cheeses for generations. The Godsells farm is located in the quaint village of Cockadilly, just a stone's throw away from our shop. The name Cockadilly comes from a nearby hamlet called Nympsfield, where this cheese is made using traditional methods.

2) Description of Product:
Cockadilly Chilli Cheese is a bold and fiery cheese that will tantalize your taste buds. It comes in four sizes - 250g, 500g, 750g, and 1kg - so you can choose the perfect amount for your needs. The cheese has a smooth and creamy texture, with a coating of select chillies that give it a vibrant red color. It is made with vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for all cheese lovers.

3) Description of Process:
This cheese is prepared using the finest quality milk and is aged to perfection. The Godsells family takes great care in the preparation of this cheese, ensuring that it is of the highest quality. The aging process gives the cheese its distinct flavor and texture, making it a true delight for cheese connoisseurs.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of Cockadilly Chilli Cheese is irresistible, with hints of Mediterranean tomatoes and a spicy kick from the chillies. The taste is equally bold and intense, with the perfect balance of heat and creaminess. Beware of the delayed reaction - this cheese packs a punch!

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Cockadilly Chilli Cheese at room temperature. Its vibrant color and unique shape make it a perfect addition to any cheese board. Pair it with crackers, bread, or fresh fruits for a delicious snack. This cheese also goes well with a glass of full-bodied red wine, enhancing its bold flavors.
