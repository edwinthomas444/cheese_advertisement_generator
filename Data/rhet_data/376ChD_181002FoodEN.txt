

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Smoked Lincolnshire Poacher Cheese, a delicious smoked mature cheese from the renowned Lincolnshire Poacher Cheese manufacturer. This cheese has a rich history and geographical provenance, hailing from the beautiful countryside of Lincolnshire.

2) Description of Product:
Each block of Smoked Lincolnshire Poacher Cheese is carefully crafted using traditional methods and unpasteurised milk from our own 230 Holstein Cows. Available in 250g, 500g, 750g, and 1kg sizes, this cheese has a distinct shape and texture, similar to a smoked Cheddar but with a unique flavor twist. The cheese is coated with a smoky flavor and is made with rennet, giving it a rich and creamy taste.

3) Description of Process:
At Lincolnshire Poacher Cheese, we are committed to producing the best cheese possible. Our cheese is made using only our own unpasteurised milk and traditional methods, ensuring a depth and complexity of flavor that sets us apart. We take great care in preparing and aging our cheese to ensure it reaches its full potential.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The Smoked Lincolnshire Poacher Cheese has a distinct smoky aroma that will delight your senses. As for its taste, the cheese offers a perfect balance of flavors, with a hint of smokiness and a slight tanginess. The depth and complexity of taste in this cheese will leave you wanting more.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving Smoked Lincolnshire Poacher Cheese at room temperature to allow the flavors to fully develop. This cheese pairs perfectly with a variety of foods, such as crackers, fruits, and cured meats. For a complete sensory experience, we suggest pairing it with a full-bodied red wine.

Our dedication to producing the best cheese has been recognized through various awards and quality tests. As our founders, Simon and Tim Jones, say, "It's all about the cheese!" and we strive to ensure that every block of Smoked Lincolnshire Poacher Cheese represents our passion and dedication to quality.
