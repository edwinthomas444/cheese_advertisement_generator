

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing our delicious Smoked Wensleydale Cheese, made by the renowned Hawes dairy in the picturesque town of Hawes. This cheese has a rich history, dating back to the 12th century when it was first produced by Cistercian monks in the Wensleydale region of North Yorkshire, England.

2) Description of Product:
Our Smoked Wensleydale Cheese is a perfect blend of tradition and innovation. It has a distinctive open-textured shape, coated with a subtle smokiness that adds depth to its flavor. Available in 250g, 500g, 750g, and 1kg sizes, this cheese is made with vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for all dietary preferences.

3) Description of Process:
At Hawes Creamery, we take great pride in our cheese-making process. Our Smoked Wensleydale Cheese is carefully prepared using traditional methods, and then aged to perfection, allowing its flavors to develop and mature. This results in a cheese that is bursting with flavor and character.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
One whiff of our Smoked Wensleydale Cheese and you'll be transported to the rolling hills of Yorkshire. Its aroma is a perfect balance of smokiness and creamy goodness. As for the taste, you'll experience a subtle yet satisfying smokiness, complemented by the rich and creamy texture of the cheese.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best tasting experience, we recommend serving our Smoked Wensleydale Cheese at room temperature. Its unique shape and smoky coating make it a beautiful addition to any cheese platter. Pair it with some crusty bread, crackers, or fruit for a delightful snack. For a more indulgent experience, try it with a glass of full-bodied red wine.

6) Quality Assurance:
Don't just take our word for it, our Smoked Wensleydale Cheese has received numerous accolades and awards, including the prestigious "Best Smoked Cheese" at the International Cheese Awards. We also conduct regular quality tests to ensure that each batch of cheese meets our high standards. As Gromit would say, it's "real cheese" that you can trust.
