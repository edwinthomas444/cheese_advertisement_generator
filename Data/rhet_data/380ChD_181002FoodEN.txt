

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Vulscombe Goat Cheese, a delicious and fresh-tasting individual cheese made in Devon by Joyce and Graham Townsend. This cheese has a rich history, with its roots in the beautiful countryside of Tiverton.

2) Description of Product:
Vulscombe Goat Cheese comes in a convenient 170g size, perfect for enjoying on its own or with your favorite dishes. It has a soft and creamy texture, with a subtle coating that adds to its unique flavor. Made with vegetarian rennet, this cheese is suitable for vegetarians and is a testament to our commitment to using high-quality ingredients.

3) Description of Process:
Our cheese is made using pasteurized goats' milk, ensuring the highest quality and safety standards. The cheese is then pressed for 24 hours to reduce moisture, resulting in a rich and creamy texture. This process also contributes to the cheese's excellent keeping quality, allowing you to enjoy it for longer.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Vulscombe Goat Cheese has a fresh and tangy aroma, with hints of lemon that will awaken your senses. The taste is mild and creamy, with a subtle hint of goats' milk that adds a unique and delicious flavor to the cheese.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving Vulscombe Goat Cheese at room temperature to fully appreciate its flavors and textures. It can be served on a cheese board with crackers and fruits, or used in salads and sandwiches for a delicious twist. This cheese pairs perfectly with a crisp white wine or a light and fruity red.
