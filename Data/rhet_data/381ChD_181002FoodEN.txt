

1) Description of Cheese:
Experience the unique flavors of ﻿Cerney Mini Ash Goat Cheese, crafted by the award-winning Cirencester cheese makers. This local delicacy is a true representation of the rich history and geographical provenance of the Cotswolds region in England.

2) Description of Product:
This 140g goat cheese is coated with ash, giving it a distinctive appearance and a smooth, creamy texture. Made with vegetarian rennet and unpasteurized milk, it is a perfect choice for those seeking a truly authentic and flavorful cheese.

3) Description of Process:
Our ﻿Cerney Mini Ash Goat Cheese is carefully prepared and aged by skilled artisans, using traditional methods that have been passed down for generations. This ensures that each bite is bursting with the true essence of the Cotswolds.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
With its earthy aroma and tangy, slightly nutty flavor, this goat cheese is a true delight for the senses. The ash coating adds a subtle smokiness, making it a unique and unforgettable culinary experience.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve this cheese at room temperature and present it on a cheese board with crackers, fruits, and nuts. It pairs perfectly with a crisp white wine, such as Sauvignon Blanc, or a light red wine, such as Pinot Noir. It also makes a delicious addition to salads or sandwiches.
