

1) Description of Cheese: This Yorkshire Pecorino Fresco is a deliciously creamy cheese made by Mario Olianas in Adel, near Leeds, West Yorkshire. Mario comes from a strong family of Italian gastronomes, bringing a rich historical and geographical provenance to this cheese.

2) Description of Product: This young Pecorino cheese is only 30 days old, yielding a soft and yogurty-rich texture. It is a smooth, young cheese with a thin undulating rind and a weight of approximately 200g.
