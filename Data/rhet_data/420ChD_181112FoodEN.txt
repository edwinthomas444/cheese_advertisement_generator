

1) Description of Cheese: Denhay Dorset Drum Cheddar, made by Denhay Farms in Dorset, England, has a rich history dating back over 30 years. It is a smaller version of the traditional 27kg rinded cheddar, making it perfect for cheese boards or as a gift.

2) Description of Product: The Denhay Dorset Drum Cheddar is a 2kg mini truckle with a hand-made, rindless coating. It is made with milk from Denhay's own cows and uses traditional methods. The cheese is matured for 7-12 months, resulting in a distinctive cheddar flavor.

3) Description of Process: Denhay Cheddar is made on the farm using age-old wooden shelves for maturation. The natural micro-flora in the Cheese House, developed over 50 years, gives the cheese a smooth, creamy character with a nutty rounded flavor.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The Denhay Dorset Drum Cheddar has a unique aroma and taste, thanks to the natural micro-flora in the Cheese House. It has a smooth and creamy texture with a nutty, rounded flavor that is not too strong, making it enjoyable for all palates.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve the Denhay Dorset Drum Cheddar at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board or used as a topping for crackers or bread. It pairs well with a variety of foods, such as fruits, nuts, and cured meats. For a complete experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine.

6) Quality Assurance: "Denhay Cheddar is made using only milk from Denhay's own cows, ensuring the highest quality and freshness." This quote from the company highlights their commitment to quality. The Denhay Dorset Drum Cheddar has also won numerous awards, including the prestigious World Cheese Award. It has also been subject to rigorous quality tests, ensuring that every bite is of the highest quality.
