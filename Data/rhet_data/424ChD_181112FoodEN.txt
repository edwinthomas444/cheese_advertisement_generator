

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing the Cheddar Hundred Dram, a creamy and soft textured cheddar made in the West Country by the renowned cheese maker, Paxton & Whitfield. This cheese has a rich historical and geographical provenance, hailing from the West Country region of England.

2) Description of Product:
The Cheddar Hundred Dram is a medium-sized cheese with a weight of 200g, coated in wax for protection and preservation. It boasts a creamy and soft texture, made using cow's milk and vegetarian rennet.

3) Description of the Process:
Paxton & Whitfield follows traditional handmade methods of production, using milk from local herds to create the unique flavor and character of the Hundred Dram Cheddar. The cheese is carefully prepared by hand and aged for over 12 months to reach its optimum maturity and flavor levels.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The Hundred Dram Cheddar has a milky aroma and a medium, milky flavor that is both creamy and tangy. Its long aging process gives it a rich and complex taste that is sure to delight cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best taste experience, serve the Cheddar Hundred Dram at room temperature. Its attractive wax coating makes it an ideal addition to any cheese platter or cheese board. Pair it with a Merlot or Chardonnay for a perfect combination of flavors. It also goes well with crackers, fruits, and meats.

6) Quality Assurance:
The Cheddar Hundred Dram holds a PDO status, guaranteeing its authenticity and quality. It has also won numerous awards and accolades, including being made using traditional methods and aged on a Westcountry farm for at least 9 months. Trust in Paxton & Whitfield's commitment to providing the finest quality cheese.
