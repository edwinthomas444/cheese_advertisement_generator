

1) Description of Cheese:
Montgomery Cheddar is a premium cheese made by Jamie Montgomery in North Cadbury, England. With a rich history dating back to the 19th century, this cheese is a true representation of traditional Cheddar making. The Montgomery family has been making this cheese for three generations, using the same methods and techniques to ensure consistent quality. 

2) Description of Product:
This mature Cheddar is known for its long and complex flavor, with a drier and nuttier profile compared to other Cheddars. It is made from unpasteurized cow's milk and traditional animal rennet, giving it a distinct and authentic taste. The cheese is hand-cheddared, resulting in a crumbly and crunchy texture. It is bound in cloth for natural maturation and aged for a minimum of eleven months.

3) Description of Process:
Montgomery Cheddar is made using traditional methods, with minimal changes made by Jamie Montgomery. The milk is sourced from 140 Holstein cows on his own farm, ensuring the freshest and highest quality ingredients. The curds are cheddared by hand, a process that gives the cheese its characteristic body. It is then aged for 12-18 months, allowing for the development of complex flavors.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of Montgomery Cheddar is strong and nutty, with hints of earthiness and grass. The taste is bold and full-flavored, with a depth and complexity that is unmatched. Each batch has slight variations in flavor, depending on the pastures the cows have grazed on and the starter culture used. The texture is dry and crumbly, with a satisfying crunch.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Montgomery Cheddar at room temperature to allow its full flavor to shine. It can be presented on a cheese board, paired with crackers, fruits, and nuts. This cheese also makes a perfect addition to any sandwich or grilled cheese. For a truly indulgent experience, pair it with our Number 93 Ale, a perfect match for Montgomery Cheddar.

6) Quality Assurance:
Montgomery Cheddar has been recognized as one of the best Cheddars in the world, winning numerous awards and medals at prestigious cheese competitions. It was the Reserve Champion Winner at the 2018 British Cheese Awards and has also won a Super Gold medal at the 2015 World Cheese Awards. With its consistent quality and outstanding taste, it is no wonder that Montgomery Cheddar is highly sought after by cheese lovers worldwide. As Jamie Montgomery himself says, "This is how Cheddar should taste."
