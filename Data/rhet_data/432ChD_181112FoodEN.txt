

1) Description of Cheese:
Pennard Ridge is a delicious hard goats cheese crafted by the Somerset Cheese Company in England's beautiful county of Somerset. With over 40 years of experience, the company's founders Philip Rainbow and Nicholas and Anita Robinson have combined their talents to create this exceptional cheese. The name "Pennard Ridge" refers to the location of the nearby farm where the milk used to make the cheese is sourced.

2) Description of Product:
Pennard Ridge is a handcrafted cheese with a unique open texture and a fresh flavor that develops into fruity hints and a nutty ending. It is made using a Caerphilly recipe, giving it a slightly younger and fresher taste. The cheese is pressed for 24 hours, then placed in a brine bath for another 24 hours before being aged in maturation rooms. It is made from pasteurized goat's milk and uses vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of Process:
The process of making Pennard Ridge is labor-intensive, ensuring that each cheese is truly handmade. After being pressed and brined, the cheese is carefully aged to perfection. This meticulous process results in a high-quality cheese with a unique and delicious flavor.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Pennard Ridge has a distinct aroma, with hints of salt from the brine bath and a fresh, fruity scent. Upon tasting, the cheese reveals a complex flavor profile, starting with a fresh, tangy taste that develops into fruity notes and ends with a nutty finish. The open texture of the cheese adds to its unique taste experience.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Pennard Ridge at room temperature to allow its full flavor to develop. This cheese is perfect for a cheese platter, paired with crackers and fresh fruit. It also pairs well with a variety of wines, such as a crisp white or a bold red. Its unique flavor also makes it a great addition to salads and sandwiches.

6) Quality Assurance:
The Somerset Cheese Company takes pride in their high-quality cheese, and it shows in the numerous awards and recognition they have received. Their expertise and dedication to crafting exceptional cheese have earned them a reputation as one of the best cheese producers in the region. As Philip Rainbow, the head cheese maker, says, "The secret to great cheese is not just in the recipe, but in the love and care that goes into making it."
