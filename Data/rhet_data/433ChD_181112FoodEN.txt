

1) Description of Cheese: Fosseway Fleece by Somerset Cheese Company is a delicious sheeps milk cheese with a rich history and strong geographical ties. Made by long-term friends Philip Rainbow and Nicholas and Anita Robinson, this cheese is crafted in the heart of Somerset, England, using high quality milk sourced directly from local farms.

2) Description of Product: Fosseway Fleece is a firm cheese with a close, smooth-bodied texture. Its pale cream color and clean, rounded flavor make it a standout among other cheeses. This cheese is made with sheep's milk and uses vegetarian rennet. It comes in various sizes and weights, making it a versatile choice for any occasion.

3) Description of Process: The Somerset Cheese Company follows traditional style recipes to produce Fosseway Fleece. The cheese is carefully prepared by a small team, involved in every step of the process, from milk to finished product. It is then aged to perfection, resulting in a remarkably smooth and creamy texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Fosseway Fleece has a fresh and clean aroma, with hints of nuttiness. Its flavor is well-balanced, with a mellow tang that lingers on the palate. The cheese has a unique and distinctive taste that is sure to please any cheese lover.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of Fosseway Fleece, we recommend serving it at room temperature. This allows the cheese to fully express its rich and complex taste. It can be presented on a cheese board or paired with your favorite crackers. For a perfect pairing, we suggest enjoying this cheese with a glass of red wine or a crisp white wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Fosseway Fleece has received numerous accolades and awards, including a silver medal at the British Cheese Awards in 2006 and the World Cheese Awards in 2007. This cheese has been praised for its exceptional quality and taste, making it a must-try for cheese connoisseurs. As the Somerset Cheese Company uses only the highest quality ingredients and follows strict quality standards, you can trust that every bite of Fosseway Fleece will be a delicious and satisfying experience.
