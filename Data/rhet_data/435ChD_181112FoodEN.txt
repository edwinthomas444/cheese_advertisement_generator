

1) Description of Cheese:
Mayfield is a delicious cheese produced by Arthur Alsop and Nic Walker in the heart of East Sussex, England. These highly skilled chefs have a lifelong passion for cheese and have developed some truly unique and distinctive products in their small, modern dairy.

2) Description of Product:
Mayfield is a semi-hard cheese with a creamy, sweet, and nutty flavor. It is made from full-fat pasteurized cow's milk and has a wax coating. The cheese varies in strength from mild to full, depending on its age, and contains naturally developed oval-shaped holes in its interior.

3) Description of Process:
Mayfield cheese is made using traditional cheese-making techniques and is aged for 5-7 months. The cheese is carefully prepared and aged to perfection, resulting in its unique and delicious flavor.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of Mayfield cheese is sweet and gentle, with hints of nuttiness. Its texture is smooth and creamy, with evenly spread holes throughout. The taste is rich and creamy, with a perfect balance of sweetness and nuttiness.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Mayfield cheese at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers, fruits, and nuts, or used in various dishes such as grilled cheese sandwiches or salads. Mayfield pairs well with a variety of wines, including Chardonnay and Pinot Noir.

6) Quality Assurance:
Mayfield cheese has received numerous awards, including a gold medal with 3 Stars at the Great Taste Awards, a gold medal at the 2010 British Cheese Awards, and a gold medal at the 2011 World Cheese Awards. These accolades speak to the exceptional quality and taste of this cheese, making it a must-try for all cheese lovers. As our motto says, "Quality is our top priority."
