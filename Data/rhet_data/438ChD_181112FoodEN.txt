

1) Description of Cheese:
Ticklemore, a white rinded goat's milk cheese, is a product of the Sharpham Estate in the Dart Valley, England. This beautiful estate, spanning 500 acres of farmland, is known for its high-quality dairy products. Ticklemore cheese has a rich history, with production dating back to 1981 when it was made in the estate's creamery. In 2003, production was moved to a purpose-built creamery next to the estate's winery, showcasing a blend of traditional techniques and modern facilities.

2) Description of Product:
Ticklemore cheese is a visually appealing cheese with a distinctive shape and size. It has a smooth and creamy texture, coated with a white rind that adds to its aesthetic appeal. This cheese is made using fresh goat's milk from Button Farm on Dartmoor, where the goats are free-range and a mix of different breeds. The cheese is made using traditional methods and vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of the Process:
Ticklemore cheese is made with great care and attention to detail. The fresh goat's milk is not frozen and is used in the production process. The cheese is made completely by hand, following traditional techniques. After production, the cheese is aged for at least two months, during which time it is carefully nurtured to perfection.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Ticklemore cheese has a distinct and alluring aroma, with hints of citrus and a gentle salty undertone. The cheese has a rich and clean flavor, with a creamy lemon tang and a refreshing finish. The taste of Ticklemore cheese is truly unique and will leave you wanting more.

5) Serving Suggestions:
To fully enjoy the flavors of Ticklemore cheese, it is best served at room temperature. The cheese can be presented on a cheese board, accompanied by crackers, fruits, and nuts. It pairs well with a variety of foods, including salads, sandwiches, and pasta dishes. For a perfect pairing, serve Ticklemore with a crisp white wine, such as a Sauvignon Blanc or a Pinot Grigio.

6) Quality Assurance:
Ticklemore cheese has received recognition for its exceptional quality. It won a Silver Medal at the 2016 British Cheese Awards and a Gold Medal at the Taste of the West in 2015. These awards are a testament to the superior taste and quality of Ticklemore cheese. Additionally, the cheese undergoes rigorous quality tests to ensure that it meets the highest standards, providing you with a truly exceptional cheese experience.
