

1) Description of Cheese: Beauvale by Cropwell Bishop - A Delicious Combination of Stilton and Gorgonzola from the Peak District

2) Description of Product: This award-winning blue cheese is handcrafted with traditional rennet and British milk from the Peak District. It boasts a rich, buttery flavor of Stilton and a soft, spreadable texture of Gorgonzola, with a subtle sweetness from its Italian counterpart. Beauvale is matured for seven weeks, developing an edible rind, and should be stored between 2-5 degrees before being brought to room temperature for optimal taste.

3) Description of Process: Beauvale is hand ladled and carefully aged for seven weeks, allowing its flavors to fully develop. Traditional rennet is used to bring out the smooth, creamy texture and mellow flavor of this unique cheese.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Beauvale is both rich and inviting, with hints of butter and sweetness. Upon tasting, you will experience a creamy, buttery texture with a subtle sweetness and a tangy finish.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Beauvale at room temperature to showcase its soft, creamy, and spreadable qualities. It pairs perfectly with a variety of foods, such as salads, crusty bread, figs, and honey. For a complete indulgence, pair Beauvale with a glass of your favorite wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Beauvale has been recognized as the 'Best Blue Cheese' at the British Cheese Awards 2017. It is made with the highest quality ingredients and handcrafted with precision, ensuring a delicious and unique cheese that is truly worth trying. As one satisfied customer puts it, "Beauvale is really worth a try as words don't do it justice."
