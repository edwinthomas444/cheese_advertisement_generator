

1) Description of Cheese: Black Bomber is a premium cheddar cheese made by the Snowdonia Cheese Company in the picturesque region of Snowdonia, Wales. This cheese has a rich history and is a proud representation of the local cheese-making tradition.

2) Description of Product: Black Bomber is a distinctive cheddar cheese that comes in a convenient 200g size. It is coated in black wax, which not only adds to its unique appearance but also helps to keep the cheese moist and fresh. Made from pasteurized cow's milk and traditional rennet, this cheese is a perfect blend of quality ingredients.

3) Description of Process: The preparation of Black Bomber involves a meticulous process of cheese-making, followed by careful aging to achieve the perfect texture and flavor. The result is a delicious, tangy taste with a creamy texture and crunchy cheese "crystals" that add a delightful crunch to every bite.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Black Bomber has a distinct tangy aroma that is sure to awaken your senses. Its creamy texture and tangy flavors make it a true delight for cheese lovers. The cheese also develops a fudge-like, melt-in-your-mouth texture when left out of the fridge for a few hours.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, we recommend serving Black Bomber at room temperature to fully appreciate its flavors and textures. The cheese can be presented on a cheese board or paired with crackers for a delicious snack. It also pairs perfectly with IPA's, particularly session IPA's like Neck Oil by Beavertown Brewery.
