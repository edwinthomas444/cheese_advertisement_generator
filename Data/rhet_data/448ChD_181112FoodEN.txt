

1) Description of Cheese: Westcombe Cheddar is a hand-crafted cheddar from Evercreech, Somerset, made with unpasteurised cows milk from Lower Westcombe Farm. It has been the winner of 'Best Cheddar' at the 2018 British Cheese Awards and holds European PDO status as well as Artisan Somerset Cheddar designation from Slow Food.

2) Description of Product: This traditional cheddar has a deep, nutty flavor with citrusy, grassy notes and a lovely long, lingering finish with hints of caramel. It weighs 250g and is made with unpasteurised cow's milk using traditional rennet.

3) Description of Process: Westcombe Cheddar is hand-crafted and aged to perfection. The cheese is made using traditional methods and is carefully aged to develop its rich, complex flavors.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Westcombe Cheddar is reminiscent of hazelnuts, while the taste is full of deep, nutty flavors with hints of citrus and grass. The cheese has a lovely long finish with subtle caramel notes.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Westcombe Cheddar at room temperature. It can be enjoyed on a cheeseboard or as part of a classic Ploughman's Lunch. This versatile cheese pairs well with many beers, but a pale ale is recommended for the perfect pairing.

6) Quality Assurance: "This is a cheddar that has won practically all awards going" and is highly praised by cheese experts. It holds European PDO status and has been designated as an Artisan Somerset Cheddar by Slow Food. These honors are a testament to the quality and excellence of Westcombe Cheddar.
