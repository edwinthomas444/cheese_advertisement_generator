

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing the award-winning Strathearn cheese, crafted by the renowned cheese maker in Perthshire, Scotland. This semi-soft cow's milk cheese is a true representation of the innovation and quality present in the British cheese industry.

2) Description of Product:
Strathearn is a 250g cheese with a unique shape and size, perfect for any cheese platter. Its texture is both creamy and lactic, with a pungent and flavorsome rind. Made with pasteurized cow's milk and vegetarian rennet, this cheese is suitable for all cheese lovers.

3) Description of Process:
To create this exceptional cheese, the cheese is matured for 4 weeks and washed every two days in brine containing Glenturret 10 year old single malt whisky. This process results in a cheese with a sweet and lactic paste, perfectly balanced with the pungent and flavorful rind.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of Strathearn cheese is a delight to the senses, with hints of whisky and a creamy, milky scent. The taste is equally impressive, with a perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess, leaving a lasting impression on your taste buds.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the ultimate cheese experience, we recommend serving Strathearn at room temperature to allow the flavors to fully develop. Pair it with oatcakes and a single malt whisky for a perfect combination of flavors. This cheese also pairs well with a variety of wines, such as a crisp Sauvignon Blanc or a bold Cabernet Sauvignon.

6) Quality Assurance:
Don't just take our word for it - Strathearn has received numerous accolades, including a Gold Medal at the World Cheese Awards in 2016. The quality and unique flavor of this cheese have been recognized by cheese experts and enthusiasts alike. Try it for yourself and taste the award-winning goodness of Strathearn cheese.
