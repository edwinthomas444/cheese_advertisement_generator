

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing the award-winning Ribblesdale Superior Goat Cheese, crafted by the renowned Ribblesdale Cheese Company in Yorkshire, England. This cheese has a rich history and is deeply rooted in its geographical provenance, as it is made in the picturesque town of Hawes, North Yorkshire, using only the finest local milk from the Yorkshire Dales.

2) Description of Product:
This cheese boasts a strikingly white appearance, with a smooth texture and a subtle nuttiness that is characteristic of high-quality goat cheese. Its shape and size are perfect for any cheeseboard, and its wax coating ensures that it stays fresh and delicious. Each piece weighs 250g and is made with vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for all cheese lovers.

3) Description of Process:
The Ribblesdale Superior Goat Cheese is carefully prepared using traditional methods, and aged to perfection to achieve its distinctive taste and texture. The cheese benefits from the natural pastures and high rainfall of the Yorkshire Dales, resulting in a truly exceptional product.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Upon opening the wax-coated packaging, you will be greeted with a delightful aroma of fresh goat's milk. The taste is equally impressive, with a subtle nuttiness and a creamy texture that melts in your mouth. This cheese is a true delight for the senses.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving this cheese at room temperature to fully appreciate its flavor and texture. It can be presented on a cheeseboard alongside crackers, fruits, and nuts for a delicious appetizer. For a more indulgent treat, try baking it with figs and pairing it with a glass of Merlot.
