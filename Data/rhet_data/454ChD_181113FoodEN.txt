

1) Description of Cheese: The Cave Aged Goat Cheddar, produced in Dorset, England, is a premium cheese that has won the prestigious 'Best Goat Cheese' award at the British Cheese Awards 2017.

2) Description of Product: This cheese is a perfect blend of three essential elements - the cheddar recipe for its familiar texture and structure, high quality goat's milk for its rich flavor, and the unique maturing process in caves in Dorset. It has a smooth cheddar texture and a subtle, less 'goaty' taste compared to other goat cheeses. It is available in a standard size and weight.

3) Description of Process: The cheese is made using traditional rennet and pasteurized goat's milk. It undergoes a meticulous aging process in the caves of Dorset, where the ideal temperature and humidity levels enhance its flavor and texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The Cave Aged Goat Cheddar has a rich and distinctive aroma, with hints of earthiness and nuttiness. Its taste is smooth and creamy, with a perfect balance of tanginess and sweetness.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve this cheese at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese platter with crackers, fruits, and nuts. It pairs well with a variety of foods such as salads, sandwiches, and pasta dishes. For a complete indulgence, pair it with a glass of red wine.
