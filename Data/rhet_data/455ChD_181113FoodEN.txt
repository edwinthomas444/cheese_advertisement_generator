

1) Description of Cheese: Suffolk Gold by Creeting St Mary is a semi-hard British cheese with a rich history and geographical provenance. Made in Suffolk, this cheese is a true representation of the region's traditional dairy farming.

2) Description of Product: This semi-hard cheese is made using milk from the farm's own Guernsey cows, known for their creamy and buttery flavor. The cheese has a mild sweetness and a golden hue, thanks to the high butterfat content. It weighs 250g and is suitable for vegetarians, making it a guilt-free indulgence.

3) Description of the Process: Suffolk Gold cheese is prepared using traditional methods and aged to perfection. The result is a smooth and creamy texture, with hints of Caerphilly and a rind reminiscent of St Nectaire. The cheese's attractive rind and color make it a feast for the eyes as well.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Suffolk Gold cheese is inviting, with a hint of butter and cream. Its taste is similar to cheddar, but with a softer texture and a mild sweetness. This cheese is a perfect balance of flavors and is sure to please any palate.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Suffolk Gold cheese at room temperature. Its creamy texture and rich flavor make it a perfect addition to a cheese platter. Pair it with oatcakes and an apple for a delicious snack. And for the ultimate combination, we recommend pairing it with a red ale like Rapture by Magic Rock Brewery.
