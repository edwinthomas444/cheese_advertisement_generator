

1) Description of Cheese:
Rainbow's Gold is a unique, unpasteurised cheese from Somerset, England. It is made by Phil Rainbow of the Somerset Cheese Company, using traditional methods and locally sourced ingredients. With a rich history and geographical provenance, this cheese is a true representation of the Somerset region.

2) Description of Product:
Rainbow's Gold is a cheddar-style cheese with a twist. It is washed in 'Golden Chalice' ale from Glastonbury, giving it a softer, stickier texture. This 250g cheese has a crumbly yet melt-in-your-mouth texture, with a surprisingly strong flavor of grassy, farmyard notes. It is made with unpasteurised cow's milk and vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of the Process:
Rainbow's Gold is made using traditional methods and aged to perfection. The cheddar-style cheese is washed in 'Golden Chalice' ale, which gives it a unique flavor and texture. It is then aged to develop its rich and complex taste, making it a truly special cheese.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The unique process of washing Rainbow's Gold in 'Golden Chalice' ale gives it a distinctive aroma, with hints of grassy and farmyard notes. The taste is surprisingly strong, with a perfect balance of flavors that lingers on the palate. It has a crumbly texture that melts in your mouth, making it a delight to eat.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving Rainbow's Gold at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheeseboard or paired with other complementary flavors such as fruits, nuts, and crackers. For a perfect pairing, we suggest trying it with a hoppy beer like 'Madness IPA' by the Wild Beer Co or a strong Belgian-style beer like Troubador Obscura or Troubador Magma.
