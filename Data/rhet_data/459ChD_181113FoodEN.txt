

1) Description of Cheese:
Ragstone is a delicious goat's cheese that is handcrafted in Hertfordshire by Neals Yard Creamery. This cheese has a rich history and is a proud representation of traditional cheese-making in the United Kingdom.

2) Description of Product:
Ragstone is a log-shaped cheese with a smooth and creamy texture. It is made using unpasteurised goat's milk and traditional kid rennet, giving it a unique and authentic flavor. Each cheese weighs approximately 200g, making it the perfect size for sharing.

3) Description of Process:
Neals Yard Creamery takes a traditional approach to cheese-making, using a windmill on site to provide power for the dairy. The cheese is carefully prepared and aged to perfection, resulting in a delightful nutty flavor that is not overpowering.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Ragstone has a lovely, mellow nutty aroma that will entice your senses. The flavor is goaty but not too strong, with a hint of sweetness. As the cheese matures, the flavor becomes more robust, making it a must-try for cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Ragstone at room temperature. Cut a disc onto a buttermilk cracker to fully appreciate the nuttiness of this cheese. It pairs perfectly with a glass of cold Sauvignon Blanc, making it a great addition to any cheeseboard.

6) Quality Assurance:
Neals Yard Creamery is a reputable name in the cheese-making industry, known for their commitment to quality and traditional methods. Ragstone is one of the most popular cheeses in their London cheese shop and has received numerous awards for its exceptional taste and quality.
