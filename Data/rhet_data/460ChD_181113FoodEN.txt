

1) Description of Cheese: Gorwydd Caerphilly is a delicious cheese made by Trethowan's Dairy in North Somerset, United Kingdom. It has a rich historical and geographical provenance, originating from the town of Caerphilly in Wales.

2) Description of Product: This cheese is crafted with care, using unpasteurised cow's milk and traditional rennet. It is matured for a minimum of 2 months, resulting in a creamy and slightly springy texture. Each block weighs 250g, making it the perfect size for sharing or indulging on your own.

3) Description of Process: The production of Gorwydd Caerphilly follows traditional methods, ensuring the highest quality. The cheese is carefully prepared and aged to perfection, resulting in a unique and delicious flavor.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Upon opening, the cheese releases a fresh and clean aroma, with subtle hints of lemon. The taste is equally delightful, with a perfect balance of creaminess and tanginess. The traditional rennet used in the process adds to the depth and complexity of the flavor.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, we recommend serving Gorwydd Caerphilly at room temperature. It can be enjoyed on its own or paired with simple crackers. For a more indulgent treat, try it in a toastie with roasted leeks and a touch of English mustard. This cheese also pairs well with a variety of wines, such as Sauvignon Blanc or Pinot Noir.
