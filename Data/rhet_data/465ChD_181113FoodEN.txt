

1) Celtic Promise: A Taste of Tradition from Caws Teifi in the Teifi Valley
Celtic Promise is a raw-milk washed-rind cheese with a rich history and a distinct geographical provenance. Crafted by John Savage-Onstwedder on his organic family farm in the Teifi Valley, this cheese is a true representation of traditional cheese-making methods.

2) A Delicate and Pungent Cheese with a Unique Pink Rind
Celtic Promise is a cheese that stands out in both appearance and taste. Its delicate buttery texture and distinctively pungent aroma make it a true sensory experience. This washed-rind cheese weighs 550g and is coated with a moist pink rind, giving it a unique and eye-catching appearance. Made from organic, unpasteurized cow's milk and using traditional rennet, this cheese is a testament to the quality of its ingredients.

3) Crafted with Care: From Caws Teifi Caerphilly to Moist Pink Rind
The process of making Celtic Promise is a labor of love. Starting as a Caws Teifi Caerphilly, the cheese is then washed with cider twice a week for seven weeks. This careful and meticulous process allows the cheese to develop its moist pink rind, giving it its distinctive appearance and flavor.

4) A Sensory Experience: Delicate Smell and Bold Taste
Celtic Promise is a cheese that appeals to all senses. Its delicate smell and bold taste make it a true delight for cheese lovers. The pungent aroma is balanced by the buttery texture, creating a perfect harmony of flavors.

5) Perfectly Paired: Serving Suggestions for the Ultimate Experience
For the best experience, we recommend serving Celtic Promise at room temperature to allow the flavors to fully develop. Its unique appearance also makes it a great addition to any cheese board. Pair it with crackers, fruits, and nuts for a delicious snack or serve it with a glass of dry cider or a full-bodied red wine for a perfect pairing.

6) Award-Winning Quality: A Testimony to Excellence
Celtic Promise has received numerous awards over the years, including the prestigious title of Supreme Champion twice at the British Cheese Awards. It is also one of only six British cheeses to have been awarded a 3-star Great Taste Award in 2018. These recognitions are a testament to the exceptional quality and taste of this cheese, making it a must-try for any cheese connoisseur.
