

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Hadrian cheese, a creamy and fresh-tasting cow's milk cheese made by the renowned manufacturer Coquetdale. Named after the famous wall in Northumberland, this cheese is a tribute to its historical and geographical provenance.

2) Description of Product:
Hadrian cheese is a unique and flavorful cheese that stands out with its addition of caraway seeds, traditionally used in Gouda cheese from Holland. It comes in various sizes, making it perfect for any occasion. The texture is smooth and creamy, with a delicate coating that adds to its visual appeal. Made from pasteurized cow's milk and vegetarian rennet, this cheese is suitable for all dietary preferences.

3) Description of Process:
Crafted with care, Hadrian cheese follows the Coquetdale recipe and is aged to perfection. The cheese-making process involves adding caraway seeds to the creamy cow's milk cheese, giving it a distinctive and aromatic taste. It is then aged to enhance its flavors and achieve the perfect balance of taste and texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The addition of caraway seeds gives Hadrian cheese a fragrant and aromatic smell that is sure to entice your senses. The taste is creamy and fresh, with a subtle hint of nuttiness from the caraway seeds. This unusual combination of flavors makes Hadrian cheese a true delight for cheese enthusiasts.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Hadrian cheese at room temperature to fully appreciate its flavors and texture. It can be presented on a cheese platter with crackers and fruits for a perfect appetizer. The caraway seeds make it a great pairing with a variety of foods, from salads to sandwiches. For a complete indulgence, pair it with a glass of your favorite wine.

6) Quality Assurance:

It is also suitable for pregnant women, making it a safe and delicious choice for everyone. Rest assured, each bite of Hadrian cheese is a testament to our commitment to providing the finest quality cheese.
