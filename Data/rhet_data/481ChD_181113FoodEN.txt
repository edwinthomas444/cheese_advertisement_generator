

1) Description of Cheese: Introducing Stoney Cross, a delicious creation from Lyburn Farmhouse Cheesemakers. This award-winning cheese is named after the old World War II airfield located just 2 miles south of the farm, giving it a rich historical and geographical connection.

2) Description of Product: Stoney Cross is available as a 1Kg truckle or a 3.0Kg wheel, with a creamy and buttery texture. This mould-ripened cheese is coated with a delicate layer, similar to French Tomme De Savoie. Made from the finest quality milk and rennet, this cheese is a perfect blend of sweet and earthy flavors.

3) Description of Process: Stoney Cross is carefully prepared and aged for 8 weeks, allowing it to develop its unique taste and texture. The aging process gives it a smooth and creamy consistency, making it a favorite among cheese lovers.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Stoney Cross is irresistible, with a subtle hint of earthiness. The taste is equally delightful, with a perfect balance of sweet and savory notes that linger on your palate.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Stoney Cross at room temperature. Its beautiful presentation makes it a perfect addition to any cheese platter. Pair it with crackers, fruits, or nuts for a delicious snack. For a more indulgent experience, pair it with a glass of your favorite wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Don't just take our word for it, Stoney Cross has been awarded a Bronze medal at the British Cheese Awards in 2017. It also received a Gold at the International Cheese Awards in Nantwich, followed by a Two Star Gold at the Great Taste Awards. These prestigious awards are a testament to the exceptional quality and taste of this cheese. Try Stoney Cross today and experience the award-winning flavor for yourself!
