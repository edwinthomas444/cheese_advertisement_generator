

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing the ﻿Cornish Smuggler, a delicious cheese crafted by a skilled manufacturer in the beautiful region of Cornwall, known for its rich dairy traditions.

2) Description of Product:
This creamy farmhouse cheese boasts a stunning red marbling throughout, made from pasteurized cow's milk and vegetarian rennet. Its traditional recipe results in a balanced acidity and a smooth texture, protected by a natural pink/grey mould rind. The cheese is coated with annatto, a water-soluble food coloring derived from the Bixa Orellana tree, giving it a unique soft red veining.

3) Description of Process:
The ﻿Cornish Smuggler is carefully crafted using a traditional farmhouse recipe, ensuring a rich and authentic flavor. The cheese is aged to perfection, allowing its unique characteristics to develop and mature.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Upon opening the package, you will be greeted with a delightful aroma of a creamy and slightly tangy cheese. The taste is a perfect balance of acidity and creaminess, with a hint of sweetness from the annatto coating. The natural pink/grey mould rind adds a depth of flavor to this already delicious cheese.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving the ﻿Cornish Smuggler at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. This cheese is perfect for a cheeseboard, adding a pop of color and flavor. It can also be cubed into salads or melted on toast for a mouthwatering treat. Pair it with a crisp white wine or a full-bodied red for a perfect combination.
