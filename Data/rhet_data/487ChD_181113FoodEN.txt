

1) Description of Cheese: Godminster's Organic Brie
Godminster's Organic Brie is a delicious and high-quality cheese made by skilled artisan cheesemakers Malcolm and Steve Dyer in the beautiful market town of Bruton, Somerset. This cheese is made with fresh organic milk from Godminster Farm, which is located only one mile away from the dairy. With a rich history and a strong geographical provenance, Godminster's Organic Brie is a true testament to the traditional methods of cheese-making.

2) Description of Product:
Godminster's Organic Brie is a round-shaped cheese with a creamy and smooth texture. It weighs approximately 250 grams and is coated with a thin layer of white mold. This cheese is made with organic cow's milk and uses traditional rennet, giving it a unique and authentic flavor.

3) Description of the Process:
The process of creating Godminster's Organic Brie is a labor of love that takes seven days from start to finish. The milk is collected from Godminster Farm and the cheese is manufactured on the same day, ensuring the freshest possible ingredients. The cheese is then drained overnight, transferred to a drying room for 24 hours, and then placed in ripening rooms for six days. Finally, it is wrapped by hand on the seventh day, ready for sale.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Godminster's Organic Brie has a distinct and inviting smell, with hints of earthy and nutty notes. When at its full maturity, the cheese has a smooth and creamy texture with a rich and buttery taste. As it ages, it becomes more crumbly and tangy, offering a different but equally delicious experience.

5) Serving Suggestions:
To fully enjoy Godminster's Organic Brie, it is best served at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers, fruits, and nuts for a perfect appetizer or dessert. This cheese pairs well with a variety of foods, including apples, pears, and figs. For a complete culinary experience, it is recommended to pair Godminster's Organic Brie with a glass of dry white wine, such as a Sauvignon Blanc or Chardonnay.

6) Quality Assurance:
Godminster's Organic Brie has received numerous accolades and awards, including the prestigious Great Taste Award. It has also been praised by food critics and customers alike, earning rave reviews for its exceptional taste and quality. Additionally, this cheese undergoes strict quality tests to ensure that every bite is as delicious as the last.
