

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing our Heart 400g Organic Cheddar, crafted by our skilled cheesemakers and proudly made in the UK. This cheese has a rich history, with roots dating back to the 12th century in the English county of Somerset.

2) Description of Product:
Our Heart 400g Organic Cheddar is a soft and creamy vintage cheese, shaped in our iconic heart shape and coated in our signature burgundy wax. Made from the highest quality organic milk and using traditional rennet, this cheese is a true testament to our commitment to using only the finest ingredients.

3) Description of Process:
Matured for up to 12 months, our award-winning cheese is carefully selected and crafted into truckles. This process allows for a full and rounded flavor, without any harsh acidity often found in vintage cheddar. Our cheese is then packaged in an attractive gift box, perfect for any occasion.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
With a smooth and creamy texture, our Vintage Organic Cheddar has a rich depth of flavor that is sure to delight your taste buds. The cheese has a distinct aroma, with hints of earthy and nutty notes, making it a true sensory experience.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best taste experience, we recommend serving our cheese at room temperature. The heart shape and distinctive burgundy wax coating make for an eye-catching presentation on any cheese platter. Pair it with your favorite crackers or fruit for a delicious snack, or use it to elevate your cooking by grating it over pasta or vegetables. For a truly indulgent experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine.

6) Quality Assurance:
Our commitment to organic farming is certified by the Soil Association, ensuring that our cheese is free from artificial preservatives. Our cheese has also won numerous awards for its exceptional taste and quality, and all our best before dates are well into the new year, making it the perfect gift for any cheese lover.
