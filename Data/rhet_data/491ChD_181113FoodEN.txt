2) Description of Product: ALPHA TOLMAN is a semi-hard cheese with a buttery, fruit & nut flavor when young, developing bold meaty, caramelized onion flavors as it matures. It has a dense and pliant texture, making it easy to portion and ideal for melting. This cheese is made from cow's milk and uses traditional rennet.

3) Description of Process: Inspired by Alpine cheeses like Appenzeller, our recipe for ALPHA TOLMAN involves careful preparation and aging. Our cheese is made in small batches and aged for a specific amount of time to ensure the perfect balance of flavors.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of ALPHA TOLMAN is reminiscent of butter and nuts, while the taste is a combination of fruity and nutty notes with a hint of meatiness and caramelized onions. As the cheese ages, the flavors become more pronounced and complex.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of ALPHA TOLMAN, we recommend serving it at room temperature. This cheese is perfect for melting, making it a great choice for fondue or raclette. Pair it with a robust ale, plummy red wine, or onion jam for a delicious combination. For a satisfying meal, melt it onto a plate of fingerling potatoes, cured meat, and natural sour pickles.
