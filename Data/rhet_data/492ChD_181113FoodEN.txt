
1) Description of Cheese: Cabot Clothbound Cheddar from Cabot Creamery, a renowned cooperative creamery with a rich history dating back to 1919 in Cabot, Vermont. This cheese is a result of a revolutionary collaboration between Cabot Creamery and Cellars at Jasper Hill, where it has become the cornerstone of their collection.

2) Description of Product: This approachable but complex natural-rind, bandaged cheddar is aged for 9-14 months in a specially calibrated vault in the Cellars at Jasper Hill. It is coated in lard and an additional layer of cloth, giving it a crumbly texture and nutty aroma. Made from pasteurized cow milk and using traditional rennet, this 32-pound wheel is a true artisanal cheese.

3) Description of Process: After being aged for over a decade, the young cheese is carefully coated and ripened in the Cellars at Jasper Hill, where it is constantly brushed, turned, and monitored for quality. This meticulous process results in a savory and slightly tangy flavor with a caramel sweetness to the finish.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Cabot Clothbound Cheddar has a signature tang and caramel nuttiness with a rustic crystalline texture that becomes creamy on the palate. Its deeply savory flavor makes it a standout cheese, perfect for any cheese lover.

5) Serving Suggestions: For optimal enjoyment, serve this award-winning cheese at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. It pairs perfectly with charcuterie, apple jelly, or a rich chestnut honey. For a complete sensory experience, pair it with an off-dry sparkling apple cider, hoppy ale, or an oaked Cabernet Sauvignon.

6) Quality Assurance: Cabot Clothbound Cheddar has received numerous accolades, including the American Cheese Society's 2006 Best in Show. This is a testament to its exceptional quality and taste. As quoted by Cellars at Jasper Hill, "Clothbound is an approachable but complex natural-rind, bandaged cheddar with a crumbly texture and nutty aroma." With its rich history, meticulous aging process, and award-winning taste, Cabot Clothbound Cheddar is a must-try for any cheese connoisseur.
