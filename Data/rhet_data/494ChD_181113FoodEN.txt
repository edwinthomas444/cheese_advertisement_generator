

1) Description of Cheese: Kinsman Ridge by Landaff Creamery is a bold and flavorful cheese that showcases the skill and creativity of Deb and Doug Erb, the masterminds behind Landaff Creamery. This artisanal cheese takes inspiration from the funky French tommes, such as St. Nectaire, and is proudly made in the United States.

2) Description of Product: Kinsman Ridge is a semi-soft, tomme-style cheese with a weight of 5 pounds. Its rustic, mottled rind is cultivated naturally through a process of washing with brine. Made from unpasteurized cow milk and rennet, this cheese boasts a creamy and smooth interior that transitions to a giving and creamy consistency as it ages.

3) Description of the Process: Each wheel of Kinsman Ridge is carefully crafted and aged for 3-5 months. The cheese is made using traditional methods and is aged to perfection, resulting in a rich and savory flavor profile. The rind is delicately cultivated, adding to the overall complexity of the cheese.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Kinsman Ridge has a delightful aroma of fresh butter, forest, and cured meat. Its flavors are rich, savory, and softly floral, with hints of roasted artichoke and asparagus. As the cheese ages, its texture becomes even more decadent and its flavors deepen, making it a true delight for the senses.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors and textures of Kinsman Ridge, we recommend serving it at room temperature. Its rustic appearance and decadent texture make it a perfect choice for a composed cheese board or plate. This cheese pairs well with a Sauvignon Blanc, wheat beer, or saucisson sec, and its delicate rind does not need to be removed before serving.

6) Quality Assurance: Kinsman Ridge has received high praise from cheese lovers and experts alike. Its unique flavor and expert craftsmanship have earned it numerous awards and accolades.
