

1) Description of Cheese: Beenleigh Blue is a unique and flavorful cheese, handcrafted by Robin Congdon near the Dart River in Devon, England. With a rich history and geographical provenance, this cheese is a true representation of the English countryside.

2) Description of Product: This full-flavored cheese is made from the milk of ewes, giving it a distinctive taste and texture. It is shaped and sized perfectly for easy serving and enjoyment. The moist and slightly crumbly texture is complemented by a hint of Brazil nuts in the finish. Made with pasteurized milk and rennet, this cheese is suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of the Process: The process of making Beenleigh Blue involves careful preparation and aging for 6-8 weeks. This allows for the perfect balance of flavors to develop, making it a truly exceptional cheese. The cheese is not overly blued, giving it a unique and delicious taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Beenleigh Blue is strong, sweet, and spicy, with a hint of Brazil nuts. Upon tasting, the flavors are pronounced and bold, with a perfect balance of saltiness and creaminess. It can be compared to a Roquefort in style, yet with a unique twist that sets it apart.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of Beenleigh Blue, we recommend serving it at room temperature. This allows for the cheese to soften and release its full flavor profile. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers, fruits, and nuts, or used in recipes such as salads or pasta dishes. For the perfect pairing, we suggest a bold red wine, such as a Cabernet Sauvignon.

6) Quality Assurance: Beenleigh Blue has been recognized and awarded for its exceptional quality. It has been quoted as "strong, sweet, and spicy" and has received numerous awards for its taste and texture. It has also undergone rigorous quality tests to ensure that every bite is of the highest quality. Try Beenleigh Blue today and experience the taste of England's finest cheese.
