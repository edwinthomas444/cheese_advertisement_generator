

1) Description of Cheese: Blue Monday No.7 is a rich and sophisticated blue cows milk cheese created by Alex James, former band member of Blur, turned cheesemaker. This cheese is a tribute to the English cheese-making tradition and is produced in Thirsk, Yorkshire.

2) Description of Product: Blue Monday No.7 is a round-shaped cheese with a creamy texture and a thin natural rind. It is matured for up to 8 weeks, giving it a mellow and creamy texture. This cheese is made with pasteurised cow's milk and uses traditional rennet for the perfect balance of flavours.

3) Description of Process: Blue Monday No.7 is made using traditional methods and aged for 6-8 weeks to develop its unique flavour. The cheese is carefully pasteurised and matured, allowing it to develop a wonderful soft and creamy texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: This cheese has a distinct and intense aroma, with hints of sweetness and spiciness. The taste is equally complex, with a perfect balance of savoury and sweet notes. The creamy texture adds a luxurious touch to the overall experience.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Blue Monday No.7 at room temperature. This cheese is best enjoyed on a cheese platter, paired with dark bitter ale or stout. It also goes well with crackers, bread, and fruits. For a complete indulgence, pair it with a full-bodied red wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Blue Monday No.7 has received numerous accolades, including being named one of the top blue cheeses in England. It has also been awarded for its exceptional taste and quality. As a testament to its quality, this cheese is also suitable for vegetarians.
