

1) Description of Cheese: Colston Bassett Stilton Truckle is a top-quality, traditionally made cheese from Colston Bassett & District Dairy Limited, located in Nottinghamshire, England. Named after the town of Stilton, this cheese can only be produced in the three adjacent counties of Nottinghamshire, Derbyshire, and Leicestershire.

2) Description of Product: This quarter stilton truckle weighs approximately 2kg and is matured for at least eight weeks using traditional methods. It has a natural crust and is known for its good blueing and rich, deep, herbaceous flavors. Made from cow's milk and vegetarian-friendly rennet, this Stilton is mellow and creamy in texture.

3) Description of the Process: Colston Bassett Stilton is made using traditional methods that have been passed down for generations. The milk used to make this cheese is produced from the same pastures, ensuring consistency in flavor and quality. The cheese is aged for at least eight weeks, allowing it to develop its distinct flavor profile.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The Colston Bassett Stilton Truckle has a strong and pungent aroma, with hints of herbs and earthiness. On the palate, it is rich and creamy, with a good amount of blueing and a lingering herbaceous flavor. The cheese has a medium to strong strength, making it a perfect choice for those who enjoy bold flavors.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best taste experience, we recommend serving this Stilton at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese platter with crackers, fruits, and nuts, or used as a topping for salads and sandwiches. Pair it with a full-bodied red wine, such as a Cabernet Sauvignon, to complement its bold flavors.

6) Quality Assurance: Colston Bassett Stilton has received numerous accolades, including being regarded as one of the best traditionally made Stilton cheeses. It has also been awarded the prestigious Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) status, ensuring its authenticity and quality.
