

1) Description of Cheese: The Dambuster cheese, made by an English manufacturer, is a tribute to the RAF 617 squadron of Scampton, Lincolnshire. This firm textured, robust black waxed cheddar is a must-try for all cheddar connoisseurs.

2) Description of Product: This 200g cheese has a good bite and acidity, with a full-flavoured, rich and creamy taste. Its good keeping qualities make it an extremely tasty and versatile cheese. Made from pasteurized cow's milk, this cheese has a medium strength and is coated in black wax.

3) Description of Process: The Dambuster cheese is aged for 4 months or more, allowing it to develop its firm texture and robust flavor. It is carefully prepared and aged in Lincolnshire, ensuring its high quality and taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The Dambuster cheese has a strong and inviting aroma, with hints of acidity. Its taste is full-flavoured and creamy, with a good balance of acidity and a satisfying bite.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve this cheese at room temperature to fully appreciate its texture and flavor. It pairs well with fruit chutney or fruit bread, making it a great addition to any cheese platter. For a complete experience, pair it with a glass of wine.

6) Quality Assurance: The Dambuster cheese has received rave reviews from cheese experts, with its firm texture, robust flavor, and good keeping qualities being highly praised. It has also won awards for its quality and taste, making it a top choice for cheese lovers. As a vegetarian-friendly cheese, it is a must-have for any cheese board.
