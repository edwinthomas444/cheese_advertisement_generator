

1) Description of Cheese: Golden Cross is a specialty cheese produced by Kevin and Alison Blunt on their farm in Whitesmith, East Sussex. This soft, ash-coated log-shaped Goats cheese has a rich history and is crafted with care and expertise.

2) Description of Product: Each log of Golden Cross weighs approximately 225g and is made with unpasteurized goat's milk. The cheese is coated with a light layer of charcoal, providing a unique contrast to its creamy white interior. The dense texture and full flavor of Golden Cross make it a standout among other goat's cheeses.

3) Description of Process: Golden Cross is hand-made on the Blunt's farm and aged for 3-4 weeks. During this time, the cheese develops a more distinctive and complex flavor profile. The charcoal coating also aids in the maturation process, altering the acidity of the surface and enhancing the overall taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Golden Cross has a pleasant, subtle aroma and a rich, creamy taste. As the cheese ages, it becomes more robust and flavorful, making it a favorite among cheese connoisseurs.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Golden Cross at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. This cheese makes a beautiful addition to any cheese board or can be enjoyed on its own. Pair it with a crisp white wine or a fruity red for a perfect balance of flavors.

6) Quality Assurance: Golden Cross has received numerous awards and accolades for its exceptional taste and quality. As a testament to its excellence, it is also a favorite among top chefs and food critics. Trust in the quality and craftsmanship of Golden Cross for a truly unforgettable cheese experience.
