

1) Description of Cheese: Gurney Gold is a premium cheese made in England by skilled cheese makers in Norfolk. It is named after the Gurney family who have been making cheese for generations. This cheese has a rich history and is deeply rooted in the local culture of Norfolk.

2) Description of Product: Gurney Gold is a semi-soft cheese with a distinctive golden rind, similar to a peach skin. It is made from pasteurized cow's milk and is coated with a special blend of ingredients to promote the growth of the golden rind. This cheese is available in various sizes and weights, making it perfect for any occasion.

3) Description of Process: Gurney Gold is carefully prepared using traditional methods and is aged for several weeks to achieve its unique flavor and texture. The cheese is washed twice a week to develop its characteristic golden rind, giving it a distinct appearance and flavor.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Gurney Gold has a pungent aroma and a strong, creamy flavor that is reminiscent of the famous French Epoisse cheese. Its semi-soft texture and creamy notes make it a delight for cheese lovers. The golden rind adds a subtle sweetness to the overall taste, making it a truly unique experience.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of Gurney Gold, we recommend serving it at room temperature. For a special treat, try baking it in the oven and dipping crispy roasted potatoes into the warm, creamy cheese. This cheese pairs well with a variety of foods, including cured meats, crusty bread, and fruit. For a perfect pairing, we suggest serving it with a glass of full-bodied red wine.
