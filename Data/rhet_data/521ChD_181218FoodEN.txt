

1) Description of Cheese: Kit Calvert Wensleydale is a handcrafted cheese made by the Wensleydale Creamery in Hawes, Yorkshire, England. It is named after the father of Wensleydale cheese, Kit Calvert, who played a crucial role in saving the creamery from closure in 1935.

2) Description of Product: This cheese is a buttery, textured Dales cheese that is aged for at least 4 months. It is made with natural rennet and is crumbly in style. The cheese is made from cow's milk and is not organic. It is produced in a medium strength and has a weight of approximately 200g.

3) Description of Process: Kit Calvert Wensleydale is handcrafted using traditional methods. The cheese is made with care and attention to detail, resulting in a high-quality product. After production, the cheese is aged for at least 4 months to develop its unique flavor and texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: This cheese has a distinct aroma of butter and cream. Its taste is rich and buttery, with a slight tanginess that adds depth to its flavor. The texture is crumbly and melts in the mouth, leaving a creamy finish.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best taste experience, serve Kit Calvert Wensleydale at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board or crumbled over salads and pasta dishes. This cheese pairs well with fruity wines such as Pinot Noir and can be enjoyed with crackers or bread.

6) Quality Assurance: Kit Calvert Wensleydale has received numerous awards and accolades, including being named the Best Cheese at the Great Yorkshire Show in 2019. It has also been praised by cheese experts for its superior taste and quality. Rest assured, every bite of this cheese is a testament to its excellence.
