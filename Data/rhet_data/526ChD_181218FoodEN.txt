
1) Description of Cheese: Montgomery Cheddar is a premium cheese made by the traditional, handmade methods of the Montgomery family in N Cadbury, Somerset, England. With a rich history and geographical provenance, this cheddar is a true representation of the finest Somerset cheddar.

2) Description of Product: This cheddar is a hard cheese with a medium to strong strength, wrapped in muslin cloth and aged for a minimum of 12 months on wooden shelves. It has a distinct shape and size, with a crumbly yet creamy texture. Made from the milk of cows and using unpasteurised rennet, this cheese is a true artisanal product.

3) Description of the Process: Each batch of Montgomery Cheddar is carefully prepared and aged for a minimum of 12 months. The cheese is wrapped in muslin cloth and placed on wooden shelves to mature, allowing its flavors to develop and intensify. This traditional process is what sets Montgomery Cheddar apart from other cheddars on the market.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The deep, rich, and nutty flavors of Montgomery Cheddar have won worldwide acclaim, including the prestigious title of Supreme Champion at the British Cheese Awards. Its aroma is enticing and its taste is bold and complex, with a perfect balance of sharpness and creaminess.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of Montgomery Cheddar, it is best served at room temperature. When presenting this cheese, its unique shape and texture make for an impressive display. It pairs perfectly with a variety of foods such as crackers, fruits, and cured meats. For a truly indulgent experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Montgomery Cheddar has been recognized and praised by cheese experts and connoisseurs around the world. Its numerous awards and accolades, including the Supreme Champion at the British Cheese Awards, are a testament to its exceptional quality and taste. With every bite, you can be assured that you are indulging in the finest, handmade, unpasteurised Somerset cheddar.
