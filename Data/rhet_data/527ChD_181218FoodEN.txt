

1) Description of Cheese: Mrs Kirkhams Traditional Lancashire is a delicious cheese made by the Kirkham family in Lancashire, England. This cheese has a rich history and is deeply rooted in the traditions of the region.

2) Description of Product: This cheese is a full fat, hard pressed, crumbly style Lancashire with a unique character. It is handmade from the raw milk of their own 100 strong Holstein Friesian cows on Beesley Farm. The cheese is then clothbound and buttered, and allowed to fully mature. It is recognized as one of the best Lancashire cheeses on the market.

3) Description of Process: The cheese is prepared using traditional methods and aged for 6 months to achieve its perfect texture and flavor. The Kirkham family takes great care in every step of the process, resulting in a high-quality cheese.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Mrs Kirkhams Traditional Lancashire has a distinct aroma and a rich, creamy taste. The crumbly texture adds a unique element to the cheese, making it a delight for the senses.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of this cheese, we recommend serving it at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers and fruits, or melted on a warm crusty bread. This cheese pairs well with a variety of foods, including cured meats, pickles, and chutneys. For a perfect wine pairing, we suggest a full-bodied red wine such as a Merlot or Cabernet Sauvignon.

6) Quality Assurance: Mrs Kirkhams Traditional Lancashire has received numerous accolades and awards, including being recognized as one of the best Lancashire cheeses on the market. The Kirkham family takes pride in their cheese-making process and ensures the highest quality with every batch.
