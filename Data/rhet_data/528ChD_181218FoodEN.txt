

1) Description of Cheese: Introducing the Norfolk White Lady, a delectable cheese hand-crafted by Jane Murray in Norfolk, England. This soft, bloomy Brie-like cheese is made from the milk of a 60-strong herd of white Friesland sheep, grazing in the lush Black Fens of Wymondham. With a rich history and geographical provenance, this cheese is a true taste of Norfolk.

2) Description of Product: The Norfolk White Lady is a 200g wheel of pure indulgence. Its soft and creamy texture is coated with a delicate bloomy rind, giving it a beautiful appearance. Only the finest ingredients are used, including pasteurized ewe's milk and traditional rennet, ensuring a high-quality and authentic taste.

3) Description of Process: Each wheel of Norfolk White Lady is carefully hand-made, using traditional techniques passed down through generations. The cheese is aged for a minimum of 4 weeks, allowing it to develop its unique flavor profile. This process results in a cheese that is truly one-of-a-kind.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Upon opening, the Norfolk White Lady delights the senses with its mild and creamy aroma. The flavor is rich and buttery, with a subtle tang and a smooth finish. Each bite is a perfect balance of flavors, making it a true delicacy for cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the Norfolk White Lady, serve it at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. Its beautiful appearance makes it a stunning addition to any cheese board, and it pairs perfectly with crusty bread, fresh fruit, and nuts. For a truly indulgent experience, pair it with a crisp white wine or a fruity red.

6) Quality Assurance: Don't just take our word for it - the Norfolk White Lady has received numerous awards and accolades, including being named a "Medium Strength" cheese and a "Soft" style cheese. It has also been praised for its quality and taste by cheese experts and customers alike. Trust in the quality and authenticity of the Norfolk White Lady.
