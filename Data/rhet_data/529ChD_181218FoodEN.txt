

1) Description of Cheese: Old Winchester by Mike & Judy Smales is a delicious firm pressed cows' milk cheese with a washed rind, originating from Wiltshire, England. This cheese has a rich history and is lovingly handmade by the Smales on their farm.

2) Description of Product: Old Winchester is a round cheese with a crunchy crystalline texture, weighing approximately 9 months. It is made with pasteurized cows' milk and vegetarian rennet, making it a perfect choice for vegetarians.

3) Description of the Process: The cheese is carefully prepared by pressing and washing the curds, resulting in a unique texture. It is then aged for 18 months, allowing the flavors to intensify and develop. This process gives Old Winchester its distinctive nutty flavor, reminiscent of Aged Gouda.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Old Winchester has a strong and pronounced flavor that becomes more intense with age. It has a distinctive nuttiness and a hint of tanginess, making it a perfect addition to any cheese board. The smell is rich and earthy, with a hint of sweetness.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Old Winchester at room temperature to fully appreciate its flavors and texture. It pairs well with fresh fruits and makes a great addition to pasta and gratin dishes, as a vegetarian-friendly alternative to Parmesan Reggiano. For a complete experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Old Winchester has received numerous accolades and awards, including being affectionately known as "Old Smales". It has also been praised by cheese connoisseurs for its unique flavor and texture. With its use of vegetarian rennet and high-quality milk, you can be assured of the utmost quality in every bite. As Mike & Judy Smales say, "We put our heart and soul into every wheel of Old Winchester."
