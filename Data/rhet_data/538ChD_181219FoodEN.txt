
1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing the delicious Vintage Red Fox, a Red Leicester style cheese crafted by expert cheese makers in England. This cheese has a rich history and geographical provenance, making it a must-try for cheese lovers.

2) Description of Product:
Vintage Red Fox is a unique cheese with a crunchy texture and a smooth, mellow flavor. It has a flaky, open texture and is coated with a flavorless dye from the South American plant annato, giving it its signature red color. Made from cow's milk and using traditional rennet, this cheese is a testament to quality and taste.

3) Description of Process:
Crafted with care and aged for 6 months, Vintage Red Fox undergoes a meticulous preparation process to ensure its exceptional taste and texture. The aging process adds depth and complexity to the cheese, making it a true delicacy.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
As soon as you open the package, you will be greeted with the enticing aroma of Vintage Red Fox. Its smooth and mellow flavor is perfectly balanced with a subtle crunch, making it a delight for the taste buds. The addition of annato gives it a unique earthy undertone, making it a cheese like no other.

5) Serving Suggestions:
To truly savor the flavors of Vintage Red Fox, we recommend serving it at room temperature. Its beautiful red color and flaky texture make it a perfect addition to any cheese platter. It also pairs well with cheesy breads and adds a delicious touch to a potato gratin. For a complete experience, pair it with a full-bodied red wine.
