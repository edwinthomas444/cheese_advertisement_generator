

1) Description of Cheese: Colston Bassett Stilton is a classic and award-winning cheese, produced by Colston Bassett Dairy in the village of Stilton, located in Nottinghamshire, England. With a history of over 100 years, this cheese is a true representation of traditional English cheese-making.

2) Description of Product: This Stilton cheese is rich and buttery, with a perfectly balanced blue flavor. It comes in a 250g size and is made from pasteurized cow's milk using traditional rennet. The cheese is coated in wax paper, which helps preserve its freshness and flavor.

3) Description of Process: The process of making Colston Bassett Stilton involves traditional techniques and skilled production, resulting in a consistent quality all year round. The cheese is aged for around 12 weeks, with the best batches being produced in December when the summer milk reaches perfect maturity.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Colston Bassett Stilton is enticing, with a hint of buttery richness. The taste is perfectly balanced, with a creamy texture and just the right amount of sharpness from the blue veins. It is a true delight for any cheese lover.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, it is recommended to serve Colston Bassett Stilton at room temperature. This allows the flavors to fully develop and the texture to become even creamier. It pairs perfectly with a classic Port wine, but can also be used in summer salads, melted on burgers, or as part of a delicious cheeseboard.

6) Quality Assurance: Colston Bassett Stilton has received multiple awards and recognition for its exceptional quality. With a PDO (Protected Designation of Origin) status, it can only be produced in Nottinghamshire, Derbyshire, or Leicestershire, ensuring the highest standards are met. It is also pasteurized, adding an extra layer of quality control. As one of the most versatile and delicious cheeses, Colston Bassett Stilton is a must-try for any cheese connoisseur.
