

1) Description of Cheese: Perl Las, produced by Caws Cenarth of Cardigan in West Wales, is a blue cheese known as the "blue pearl" for its exceptional quality.

2) Description of Product: This handmade cheese is coated with local sea salt, giving it a subtle saltiness. It weighs 250g and has a light golden paste, complemented by the blue veins. Made with pasteurized cow's milk and vegetarian rennet, Perl Las is suitable for vegetarians.

3) Description of Process: Production of Perl Las started in 1987 as a way to diversify the farm business. The cheese is carefully prepared and aged to perfection, resulting in its magnificent flavor and texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Perl Las has a gentle and refined flavor, with a subtle sweetness that can become more pronounced as the cheese matures. Its aroma is enticing and its taste is addictive, making it difficult to stop eating.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of Perl Las, serve it at room temperature with crusty bread and a bit of fig jam to enhance its light sweetness. For a perfect pairing, we recommend a sweet stout or a glass of port.

6) Quality Assurance: Perl Las has been featured in our 'Most Popular Cheese Selection' box, a testament to its popularity among cheese lovers. It has also received numerous awards for its exceptional quality and taste. Try it for yourself and experience the excellence of Perl Las.
