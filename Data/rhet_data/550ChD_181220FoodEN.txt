
1) Description of Cheese:
Little Wallop, from the 'Alex James Presents...' range, is a delicious goat's cheese made by expert cheesemakers, White Lake Cheeses, in Somerset, United Kingdom. This cheese has a rich history and geographical provenance, making it a unique and special addition to any cheeseboard.

2) Description of Product:
Little Wallop goats cheese is a visually stunning cheese, wrapped in a vine leaf, giving it a beautiful appearance. Inside, the cheese is strong, nutty, and tangy, thanks to being washed in Somerset brandy cider. This cheese weighs 140g and is made with pasteurized goat's milk, using vegetarian rennet.

3) Description of Process:
To create Little Wallop, expert cheesemakers at White Lake Cheeses in Somerset carefully prepare and age the cheese. This process results in a smooth, silky texture that oozes once the cheese is cut, making it a delight to eat.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The aroma of Little Wallop is irresistible, with hints of tangy cider and nuttiness. Its taste is equally impressive, with a perfect balance of strong and tangy flavors. As the cheese matures, it becomes even more flavorful, making it a must-try for all cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Little Wallop at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. When presenting this cheese, the vine leaf adds a touch of elegance to any cheeseboard. It pairs perfectly with a light Somerset cider or a chilled Chablis. For beer lovers, a session IPA such as High Wire by Magic Rock or Neck Oil by Beavertown is an excellent choice.

6) Quality Assurance:
Little Wallop is a highly acclaimed cheese, with quotations and awards from renowned experts in the industry. Its quality has been tested and certified, making it a reliable and top-notch choice for all cheese connoisseurs. Don't miss out on the opportunity to try this exceptional cheese from Somerset.
