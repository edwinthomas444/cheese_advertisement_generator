

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Cerney Ash, a delicious goats cheese made in Cerney, Gloucestershire. This award-winning cheese is crafted with care by skilled cheese makers and has a rich historical and geographical provenance.

2) Description of Product:
Cerney Ash is a pyramid-shaped cheese with a light, citrus tang and a mousse-like texture. Coated in a mix of oak ash and sea salt, this cheese is protected and has a weight of 250g. It is made with unpasteurised goat milk and vegetarian rennet.

3) Description of Process:
The preparation of Cerney Ash involves shaping the cheese into a truncated pyramid, similar to Valencay. It is then coated in a mix of oak ash and sea salt to protect the delicate cheese within. The cheese is aged to perfection, allowing the goat flavour to deepen and develop a nutty taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
When young, Cerney Ash has a light and mild taste, perfect for those who are new to goats cheese. As it matures, the goat flavour becomes more prominent and adds a nutty depth to the cheese. The smell is inviting and complements the taste perfectly.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, serve Cerney Ash at room temperature. Its delicate flavour pairs well with other delicate flavours, making it perfect for salads. For a unique and delicious snack, try it on fried aubergine slices drizzled with honey. It also pairs well with pilsner or a light summery cider.

6) Quality Assurance:
Cerney Ash is a multiple award-winning cheese, including the prestigious 'Best Goat Cheese' at the 2015 World Cheese Awards. Its quality has been recognized by experts and cheese lovers alike. Don't just take our word for it, try it for yourself and see why this cheese has received such high praise.
