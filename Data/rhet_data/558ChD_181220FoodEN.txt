

1) Pavé Cobble: A Supreme Champion Cheese from White Lake Cheeses in Somerset, UK
This award-winning cheese, known as Pavé Cobble, is a soft, ewe's milk cheese that has taken the British cheese scene by storm. Made by the renowned White Lake Cheeses in Somerset, this cheese has a rich history and geographical provenance that adds to its unique and delicious flavor.

2) Delicate Pyramid-Shaped Cheese with a Charcoal Coating, Made from Ewe's Milk
Pavé Cobble is a delicate, hand-ladled cheese that is shaped into a pyramid and dusted with charcoal for a visually stunning appearance. Made from the finest ewe's milk, this cheese is a testament to the skill and expertise of White Lake Cheeses. It is also made with vegetarian rennet, making it suitable for all cheese lovers.

3) Traditional Methods and Three Weeks of Aging Create the Perfect Texture and Taste
White Lake Cheeses follows traditional cheesemaking methods to produce Pavé Cobble, ensuring that each cheese is of the highest quality. The cheese is then aged for around three weeks, allowing it to develop a perfect taste and texture. As you cut into the velvety rind, you'll experience a soft and silky paste that is a true delight for the senses.

4) A Creamy, Silky Cheese with a Deliciously Complex Flavor
Pavé Cobble is a cheese that is as pleasing to the palate as it is to the eye. Its creamy, silky texture is complemented by a complex and delicious flavor that is sure to leave you wanting more. At perfect maturity, the cheese oozes out of the rind, creating a truly indulgent experience.

5) Best Enjoyed with Light Crackers, White Wine, or Somerset Cider
For the ultimate cheese experience, we recommend serving Pavé Cobble with light crackers and a chilled glass of white wine or a light Somerset cider. The crisp and refreshing flavors of these pairings perfectly complement the rich and creamy taste of the cheese. Serve at room temperature for the best results.

6) Award-Winning Cheese that Delights Critics and Customers Alike
Pavé Cobble has received numerous awards and accolades, including Best Speciality Cheese, Best Fresh Cheese, and Best English Cheese at the 2017 British Cheese Awards. Its supreme quality has been recognized by critics and customers alike, making it a must-try cheese for any cheese lover. Don't just take our word for it, try it for yourself and experience the excellence of Pavé Cobble.
