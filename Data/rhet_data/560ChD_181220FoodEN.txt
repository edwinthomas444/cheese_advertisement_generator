

1) Bath Blue: A Taste of History from the Bath Soft Cheese Company
Founded in the picturesque city of Bath, England, the Bath Soft Cheese Company has been producing award-winning cheeses since 1992. Among their range of delicious offerings, Bath Blue stands out as a true testament to the company's dedication to quality and tradition.

2) Indulge in the Creamy Richness of Bath Blue
This distinctive blue cheese is made from pasteurised cow's milk and traditional rennet, resulting in a smooth and creamy texture that melts in your mouth. Each 250g wedge is carefully crafted and aged for 8-10 weeks, allowing the flavours to develop and intensify.

3) A Meticulous Process for a Perfect Cheese
At Bath Soft Cheese Company, every step of the cheese-making process is carefully monitored to ensure the highest quality. From the selection of the freshest milk to the precise aging conditions, each detail is attended to with the utmost care and expertise.

4) Savour the Unique Smell and Taste of Bath Blue
The distinctive blue veins running through the ivory-coloured cheese give Bath Blue its signature aroma and tangy taste. With a delicate balance of creaminess and sharpness, this cheese is sure to please even the most discerning palates.

5) Serve and Enjoy Bath Blue in Style
For the best taste experience, serve Bath Blue at room temperature to allow the flavours to fully develop. This versatile cheese pairs perfectly with a variety of foods, from crackers and fruit to salads and pasta dishes. For a truly indulgent treat, pair it with a crisp white wine or a full-bodied red.

6) Quality Guaranteed with Bath Blue
Don't just take our word for it - Bath Blue has been recognised with numerous awards, including the prestigious World Cheese Awards. With its rich and creamy flavour, impeccable quality, and impeccable track record, Bath Blue is a cheese you can trust and enjoy with confidence.
