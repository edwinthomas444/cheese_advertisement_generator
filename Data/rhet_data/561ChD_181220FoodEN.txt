

1) Description of Cheese:
Introducing Richard III Wensleydale, a beloved cheese made by the traditional Wensleydale dairy in Richmond, England. Named after the childhood home of King Richard III, this cheese has a rich historical and geographical provenance that adds to its charm and flavor.

2) Description of Product:
This near perfect example of Wensleydale cheese weighs over 2kg and comes wrapped in cloth, similar to a cheddar. Its slightly moist texture sets it apart from other Wensleydales, making it less crumbly. The light, milky flavors with a subtle citrusy tang create a truly magnificent cheese. Made with vegetarian rennet and pasteurized cow's milk, it is also safe to eat during pregnancy.

3) Description of Process:
The Wensleydale dairy follows a traditional preparation process to create this delectable cheese. It is then aged to perfection, allowing the flavors to develop and intensify. Each cheese is carefully wrapped, adding to its quality and freshness.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Upon unwrapping, the aroma of Richard III Wensleydale is sure to delight your senses. Its creamy and tangy flavors burst in your mouth, leaving a lingering, pleasant aftertaste. The texture is smooth and velvety, making it a delight to eat.

5) Serving Suggestions:
To truly enjoy the flavors of this cheese, it is best served at room temperature. Cut into slices or cubes and serve on a platter with crackers, fruits, and nuts for a delicious cheese board. Pair it with a light white wine or a fruity red for a perfect combination.

6) Quality Assurance:
Don't just take our word for it, Wallace & Gromit themselves have helped save the production of Wensleydale cheese in the 1990s. With each new film release, sales of this cheese reach new heights. It has also received numerous awards and accolades, solidifying its reputation as a high-quality cheese. Trust us, you won't be disappointed with Richard III Wensleydale.
