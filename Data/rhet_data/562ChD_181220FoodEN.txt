

1) Description of Cheese: Dorset Blue Vinny is a unique British Blue cheese made on Woodbridge Farm in the heart of Dorset. It is produced using milk from the farm's own Friesian cows and has a rich history dating back to when it was traditionally made with the leftover milk after butter production. This cheese is a true representation of the region's culinary heritage.

2) Description of Product: Dorset Blue Vinny is a 250g cheese with a crumbly texture and a strong, tangy taste similar to Stilton. It is lower in fat due to the use of leftover milk and is made using pasteurized milk and vegetarian rennet. The cheese has a distinct shape and is coated in a natural rind, adding to its unique appearance.

3) Description of Process: The cheese-making process of Dorset Blue Vinny involves careful preparation and aging. The milk is pasteurized and then mixed with vegetarian rennet to form curds. These curds are then pressed into molds and aged for several months, allowing the cheese to develop its distinct flavor and texture.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Dorset Blue Vinny has a strong, pungent aroma that is characteristic of blue cheeses. Its taste is rich and tangy, with a slightly crumbly texture. The cheese has a sharp and salty flavor, making it a favorite among cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of Dorset Blue Vinny, it is best served at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers, fruits, and nuts for a delicious appetizer. For a more indulgent experience, try pairing it with slow-baked figs and a glass of port. This cheese also pairs well with a variety of wines, such as a full-bodied red or a crisp white.

6) Quality Assurance: Dorset Blue Vinny has received numerous accolades and awards, including being named the "Best Blue Cheese" by the British Cheese Awards. It has also been praised by renowned chefs and food critics for its unique flavor and quality. Each batch of Dorset Blue Vinny undergoes rigorous quality tests to ensure that it meets the highest standards, making it a cheese you can trust and enjoy.
