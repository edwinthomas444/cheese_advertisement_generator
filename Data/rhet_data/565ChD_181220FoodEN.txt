

1) Goddess Cheese from Alex James Presents... is a delicious and unique creation from Blur bassist Alex James and White Lake Cheeses of Somerset, England. This award-winning cheese is made with the milk of Guernsey cows and is a true representation of the region's rich history and culture.

2) This cheese has a distinctive shape and size, weighing in at 200g. The rind is coated in Somerset cider brandy, giving it a beautiful golden color and a pungent aroma. Made with pasteurized cow's milk and vegetarian rennet, Goddess cheese is a true testament to quality ingredients.

3) The preparation of Goddess cheese involves a special washing process with Somerset cider brandy, which adds depth and complexity to the flavor. It is then aged to perfection, resulting in a fudgy texture reminiscent of Langres and a buttery taste that is simply irresistible.

4) The smell of Goddess cheese is inviting and alluring, with hints of cider and a rich, creamy aroma. The taste is equally delightful, with a perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess that will leave your taste buds wanting more.

5) For optimal enjoyment, serve Goddess cheese at room temperature to allow the flavors to fully develop. The cheese can be presented on a cheese board, paired with crackers and fruit for a delicious snack, or used in various recipes.
