

1) Description of Cheese: Truffled Tunworth is a luxurious creation from Hampshire, United Kingdom, crafted by the skilled cheese-makers of Walthamstow. With a rich history and geographical provenance, this cheese is a true representation of British excellence.

2) Description of Product: Truffled Tunworth is a brilliant British Camembert, weighing 250g and made from cow's milk. It is pasteurised using traditional methods and rennet, giving it a creamy and smooth texture. The cheese is coated with a layer of Brillat Savarin and black truffle mixture, elevating its taste to new heights.

3) Description of Process: To create this masterpiece, the cheese-makers slice a Tunworth in half horizontally and spread a layer of the Brillat Savarin and black truffle mixture on the bottom half. The top is then replaced, and the cheese is left to age for a few hours, allowing the truffle to infuse into the cheese.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Truffled Tunworth has a distinct earthy and mushroomy aroma, which is a result of the black truffle infusion. On the tastebuds, it offers a delightful combination of sweetness and nuttiness, making it a truly spectacular cheese.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Truffled Tunworth at room temperature and present it on a beautiful cheese board. Pair it with a glass of champagne to truly indulge in its luxurious flavors. It also goes well with a variety of foods, making it a versatile addition to any meal. For a perfect pairing, try it with a crisp white wine.
