

1) Description of Cheese: Winslade, from the makers of Tunworth, is a delectable cheese hailing from Hampshire, United Kingdom. Its origins can be traced back to the traditional cheeses of Camembert and Vacherin Mont d'Or.

2) Description of Product: Winslade is a soft and gooey cheese, weighing 250g and coated in a spruce wrapping. Made from pasteurised cow's milk and traditional rennet, this cheese boasts a unique combination of flavors.

3) Description of Process: Winslade is carefully prepared and aged to perfection, resulting in a creamy and rich texture. Its spruce wrapping imparts flavors of pine and alpine flowers, adding a touch of complexity to its taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The smell of Winslade is enticing, with hints of pine and alpine flowers. Its taste is equally delightful, with a lighter flavor than its partner Tunworth. The saltiness of olives pairs perfectly with the cheese, making it a must-try for cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Winslade at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board, or for a more indulgent treat, squish it onto some olive bread. To enhance its flavor, pair it with a light lager or pilsner, or a Gewurtztraminer for wine lovers.

6) Quality Assurance: Winslade has received rave reviews and numerous awards for its exceptional taste and quality. As quoted by cheese enthusiasts, "We love Winslade!" Its pasteurised milk and traditional rennet ensure a consistently delicious cheese every time.
