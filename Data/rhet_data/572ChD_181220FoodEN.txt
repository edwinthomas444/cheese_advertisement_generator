

1) Description of Cheese: Kidderton Ash is a delicious goat's cheese made by Ravens Oak Dairy in Nantwich, Cheshire, United Kingdom. This cheese has a rich history and is a true representation of the quality and tradition of British cheese-making.

2) Description of Product: Kidderton Ash is a small, 150g cheese coated in charcoal ash for protection during the ripening process. The distinctive grey, fluffy rind that forms as the cheese ripens gives it a beautiful appearance on any cheeseboard. This cheese is made with pasteurized goat's milk and vegetarian rennet.

3) Description of Process: The preparation of Kidderton Ash involves coating the cheese in charcoal ash and then allowing it to ripen until it reaches the perfect texture and flavor. This process is carefully monitored by the skilled cheese-makers at Ravens Oak Dairy to ensure the highest quality.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Kidderton Ash has a delightful aroma, with hints of earthiness and a slightly tangy scent from the goat's milk. The taste is creamy and smooth, with a subtle tanginess and a hint of sweetness from the charcoal ash.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Kidderton Ash at room temperature to allow the flavors to fully develop. This cheese is perfect for a cheeseboard, and can also be crumbled over dishes like fried aubergine fritters for a delicious starter. Pair it with a citrusy Sauvignon Blanc or a floral Gewurztraminer for a perfect balance of flavors.
