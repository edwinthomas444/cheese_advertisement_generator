

1) Description of Cheese: Sharpham Brie is a delicious and unique cheese, produced by the Sharpham Estate in Totnes, South Devon since 1980. This award-winning cheese is a cross between brie and coulommiers, giving it a distinct and unparalleled flavor. 

2) Description of Product: Sharpham Brie is a soft cheese with a creamy, mushroomy flavor. It is made from the milk of Jersey cows, known for their rich and luscious texture. This unpasteurized cheese is made with vegetarian rennet and comes in a convenient size of 250g-300g. 

3) Description of Process: Sharpham Brie is carefully prepared and aged to perfection. The cheese is made using traditional methods, ensuring the highest quality and taste. It is then aged to develop its unique flavors and texture. 

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Sharpham Brie is rich and earthy, with hints of mushroom and butter. The taste is creamy and smooth, with a subtle tanginess that lingers on the palate. 

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of Sharpham Brie, it is best served at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese platter with crackers, fruits, and nuts for a delicious appetizer. For a true taste of a micro terroir, pair it with Sharpham's own wines. 

6) Quality Assurance: Sharpham Brie has received numerous accolades and awards, including recognition from prestigious cheese competitions. It has also undergone rigorous quality tests to ensure its exceptional taste and quality. As one satisfied customer puts it, "Sharpham Brie is simply the best cheese I have ever tasted."
