

1) Description of Cheese:
Driftwood is a delectable cheese log made by White Lake, a renowned cheesemaker located in Pylle, Somerset. This award-winning cheese has gained recognition at the World Cheese Awards, solidifying its reputation as a top-quality product.

2) Description of Product:
Driftwood is a goats cheese coated in ash, with a soft and creamy texture. It comes in a convenient size of 215g and is made from pasteurized goat milk. The rennet used in the production process is vegetarian, making it suitable for all dietary preferences.

3) Description of Process:
The preparation of Driftwood involves aging the cheese to enhance its flavors. As it matures, the cheese develops a tangy and intense taste, making it a versatile option for different palates. White Lake's expertise in cheesemaking is evident in the multiple awards their products have received.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Driftwood has a light and lemony aroma, which perfectly complements its creamy texture. The cheese has a delicate and tangy flavor, making it a favorite among cheese lovers. Its taste becomes more robust with age, providing a unique experience with each bite.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving Driftwood at room temperature with light crackers. This allows the flavors to shine without overpowering the delicate taste of the goat cheese. It pairs well with a variety of foods, making it a versatile addition to any cheese board. For a complete experience, we suggest pairing it with a crisp white wine.

6) Quality Assurance:
Driftwood is just one of the many goats milk cheeses produced by White Lake, a company known for its exceptional quality and taste. Their products have received numerous awards, including gold at the World Cheese Awards, showcasing their commitment to excellence. As a pasteurized cheese, it undergoes rigorous quality tests to ensure it meets the highest standards. As Alex James, renowned cheese enthusiast, says, "White Lake is truly a world-class cheesemaker."
