

1) Description of Cheese: Ragstone is a delicious, artisanal goat's cheese made by Neals Yard Creamery in Hertfordshire, United Kingdom. This creamy, log-shaped cheese has a rich history and is rooted in traditional cheese-making techniques.

2) Description of Product: Ragstone is made using unpasteurised goat's milk and traditional kid rennet. It has a smooth and creamy texture and is coated in a lovely, mellow nutty flavor. This cheese weighs 200g and is perfect for serving on a cheeseboard.

3) Description of Process: Neals Yard Creamery takes a traditional approach to cheese-making, using a windmill on site to power the dairy. Ragstone is carefully prepared and aged to perfection, resulting in a delicious and high-quality cheese.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: Ragstone has a delightful goaty aroma, without being overpowering. As the cheese matures, the flavor becomes more intense, making it the perfect choice for cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve Ragstone at room temperature on a buttermilk cracker. Pair it with a glass of cold Sauvignon Blanc for a refreshing and delicious combination. This cheese also looks fantastic on a cheeseboard and is a popular choice at our London cheese shop.

6) Quality Assurance: "A great British version of the French goats cheese Saint Maure" - this quote from a satisfied customer speaks to the quality and taste of Ragstone. It has also received numerous awards and has been tested for quality, making it a top choice for cheese lovers.
