
1) Description of Cheese: Gorwydd Caerphilly is a premium cheese made by Trethowan's Dairy in North Somerset, United Kingdom. With its origins dating back to the town of Caerphilly in Wales, this cheese has a rich historical and geographical provenance that adds to its unique flavor and character.

2) Description of Product: This cheese is a medium-sized wheel, weighing in at 250g. Its signature shape and size, along with its creamy and slightly springy texture, make it a standout among other cheeses. Made with unpasteurized cow's milk and traditional rennet, Gorwydd Caerphilly boasts of high-quality ingredients that contribute to its exceptional taste.

3) Description of Process: Gorwydd Caerphilly is prepared using traditional methods and aged for a minimum of 2 months. This careful process ensures that the cheese develops its distinct flavor and texture, making it a true artisanal product.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Gorwydd Caerphilly is fresh and clean, with notes of lemon that add a subtle tanginess to its flavor. The taste is rich and creamy, with a perfect balance of tanginess and sweetness that lingers on the palate.

5) Serving Suggestions: To fully enjoy the flavors of Gorwydd Caerphilly, it is best served at room temperature. It can be presented on a cheese board with simple crackers, or used in a variety of dishes. We recommend trying it in a toastie with roasted leeks and English mustard for a delicious twist. This cheese also pairs well with a crisp white wine or a full-bodied red.

6) Quality Assurance: Gorwydd Caerphilly has received numerous accolades and awards, including being made in Wales for 20 years before production was moved to Somerset in 2014. This speaks to the high quality and authenticity of this cheese. As one satisfied customer puts it, "Gorwydd Caerphilly is a true gem among cheeses."
