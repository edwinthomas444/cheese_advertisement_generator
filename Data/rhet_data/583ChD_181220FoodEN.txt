

1) Description of Cheese:
Pendragon by the Somerset Cheese Company is a delicious hard buffalo milk cheese with a rich history and origin in Somerset, England. This cheese is a staple in our core range and is highly recommended for any cheese board.

2) Description of Product:
Pendragon has a lovely shape and size, with a subtle, nutty flavor and a creamy, salty finish. Made with buffalo milk, it has a similar texture to cheddar but with a slightly softer consistency. The cheese is coated with a light, creamy color and is suitable for vegetarians as it is made with vegetarian rennet.

3) Description of Process:
Pendragon is made using traditional methods and is carefully aged to perfection. The cheese has a beautiful color and a light, creamy flavor, thanks to the use of buffalo milk. It is one of the many exceptional cheeses produced by the Somerset Cheese Company, showcasing their innovation and expertise.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
Pendragon has a delightful aroma that is both light and floral. The taste is equally impressive, with a creamy and slightly nutty flavor that is perfectly balanced with a subtle saltiness. This cheese is a true delight for the senses.

5) Serving Suggestions:
For the best experience, we recommend serving Pendragon at room temperature to allow the flavors to fully develop. It can be presented on a cheese board with crackers, fruits, and nuts, or paired with a light, floral wine such as red or white. Alternatively, a light pale ale or sparkling cider would also complement this cheese perfectly.

6) Quality Assurance:
Pendragon has received numerous accolades and awards for its exceptional quality and taste. It has also undergone rigorous quality tests to ensure that every bite is as delicious as the last. As the Somerset Cheese Company proudly states, "Pendragon is a beautifully innovative cheese that is sure to impress."
