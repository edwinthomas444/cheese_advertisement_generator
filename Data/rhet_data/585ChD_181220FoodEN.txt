

1) Description of Cheese:
Burt's Blue, handmade by Claire Burt in Knutsford, Cheshire, is a delicious soft and creamy blue cheese with a rich history and geographical provenance. It is a firm favourite in our shop and loved by our customers.

2) Description of Product:
Burt's Blue is a beautiful cheese that is both visually appealing and delicious. It comes in a 180g size and has a soft and creamy texture. The cheese is coated in a unique salty piquancy that sets it apart from other blue cheeses. It is made using pasteurised cow's milk from local Cheshire dairies, ensuring a consistent and high-quality product. The vegetarian rennet used in the production process makes it suitable for all dietary preferences.

3) Description of Process:
Burt's Blue is carefully prepared and aged to perfection. The cheese is made using traditional techniques, and the milk is sourced locally to limit the risk of degradation. Each cheese is consistent in appearance and quality, a testament to the skill and dedication of our small cheesemakers.

4) Description of Smell and Taste:
The distinctive salty piquancy of Burt's Blue gives it a unique aroma that is both tantalizing and inviting. The taste is even better, with a perfect balance of saltiness and creaminess. Unlike other blue cheeses, Burt's Blue lacks the harsh and bitter notes, making it a favourite among cheese lovers.

5) Serving Suggestions:
To truly enjoy the full flavour of Burt's Blue, we recommend serving it at room temperature. Its creamy texture makes it perfect for spreading on a baguette or pairing with crusty bread. It also pairs well with a variety of foods, from fresh fruits to cured meats. For a truly indulgent experience, try it soaked in port for a festive treat. Burt's Blue also pairs beautifully with a glass of red wine, enhancing its flavour and complexity.

6) Quality Assurance:
Burt's Blue has received numerous accolades and awards, including being made with pasteurised cow's milk from local Cheshire dairies, ensuring the highest quality and freshness. Our customers rave about the consistent appearance and taste of each cheese, a testament to our commitment to quality and excellence.
