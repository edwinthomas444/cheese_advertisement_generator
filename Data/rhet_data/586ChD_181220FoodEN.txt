

1) Description of Cheese: Keen's Cheddar is a renowned cheese from Moorhayes Farm in Wincanton, Somerset. Made by Keen's, Montgomery's, and Westcombe, this unpasteurised cheddar has a rich history and is known for its tangy and fruity flavors.

2) Description of Product: This cheese is made from raw milk sourced from the farm's own Fresian herd and has a mellow depth of flavor. It is matured for at least 12 months and has a nice tanginess on the tongue. The cheese comes in a 250g size and is coated with traditional rennet.

3) Description of Process: Keen's Cheddar is made using traditional methods and the milk is used within a day of milking to ensure the highest quality. The cheese is aged for at least 12 months, resulting in a rich and flavorful taste.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The cheese has a strong and tangy aroma, with hints of fruitiness. On the palate, it has a mellow and creamy texture, finishing with a satisfying tanginess. The raw milk used in the cheese adds to its unique and delicious taste.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, we recommend serving Keen's Cheddar at room temperature. It pairs perfectly with a Ploughman's platter or a classic cheese and pickle sandwich on crusty bread. The tanginess of the cheese complements the acidity of pickled onions or pickle. For an elevated experience, pair it with a glass of red wine.

6) Quality Assurance: Keen's Cheddar is made with the highest quality raw milk and traditional methods, ensuring its exceptional taste and texture. It has received numerous awards and accolades, including being championed by other cheddar makers in the area. Trust us, you won't be disappointed with this delicious and authentic farmhouse cheddar.
