

1) Oxford Isis: A Delicious British Cheese from Oxfordshire
Oxford Isis is a unique and flavorful cheese made in Oxfordshire, England. Named after the River Thames, this cheese is a testament to the rich history and geographical beauty of the region. Produced by skilled cheese makers, Oxford Isis has quickly become a popular choice among cheese lovers.

2) Creamy and Sticky: The Perfect Combination
This full-fat, creamy cheese is a delight for the senses. With a soft texture and sticky rind, Oxford Isis is a treat for the taste buds. Made from cow's milk and using vegetarian rennet, this cheese is suitable for vegetarians. Each wheel weighs 200g, making it the perfect size for sharing or indulging on your own.

3) A Labor of Love: The Process of Making Oxford Isis
The production of Oxford Isis began in 2003 and has since gained a reputation for its exceptional quality. Each wheel is carefully prepared and regularly washed in honey mead, giving it a unique and pungent aroma. The aging process adds depth and complexity to the flavor, making Oxford Isis a truly special cheese.

4) A Symphony of Smell and Taste
The aroma of Oxford Isis is both inviting and intriguing. The pungent smell of honey mead and the creamy, buttery notes of the cheese blend together in perfect harmony. As for the taste, it is rich and full-bodied, with a hint of sweetness from the honey mead. This cheese is a true delight for the senses.

5) Perfectly Paired: Serving Suggestions for Oxford Isis
For the best experience, serve Oxford Isis at room temperature to allow the flavors to fully develop. When presenting this cheese, its sticky rind and creamy interior make for an eye-catching display. Pair it with floral white wines such as Guwurtztraminer or strong beers like Barbar, the Belgian honey beer. This cheese also makes a great addition to a cheese board or as a topping for crackers or bread.

6) Quality You Can Taste
Don't just take our word for it, Oxford Isis has received numerous awards and accolades for its exceptional quality. It can be found in some of the finest cheese shops and restaurants in the UK, a testament to its popularity and taste. Try Oxford Isis today and experience the deliciousness for yourself.
