It has a creamy texture and is coated with a delicate blue mold, giving it a beautiful appearance. Made from pasteurized cow's milk and using traditional rennet, Cashel Blue is a true representation of the rich Irish dairy heritage.

When tasted, it offers a burst of flavors, from the initial creaminess to the slightly spicy and salty notes of blue mold. Its unique flavor profile makes it a favorite among cheese lovers.
