

1) Description of Cheese: Beenleigh Blue is a unique blue cheese made from ewe's milk near Totnes in Devon, England. It stands out from other British blue cheeses with its crumbly and moist texture, and its rich and complex flavor profile.

2) Description of Product: This cheese is made in a traditional blue cheese shape, with a weight of approximately 1kg. Its texture is crumbly and moist, with a natural rind coating. The cheese is made from ewe's milk and uses traditional rennet.

3) Description of Process: Beenleigh Blue is carefully prepared using traditional methods, and then aged for a minimum of 3 months. During this time, the cheese develops its unique flavors and textures, making it a truly special cheese.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: This cheese has a distinct aroma of grass and flowers, derived from the ewe's milk. The taste is a perfect balance of sweetness and savory notes, with hints of herbs and grass from the milk, and a tangy flavor from the blue veining.

5) Serving Suggestions: For the best experience, serve this cheese at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. It can be presented on a cheese board, paired with crackers and fruit. For a true taste of Devon, pair this cheese with a local cider.

6) Quality Assurance: Beenleigh Blue has received numerous awards and accolades, including being named "Best Blue Cheese" at the British Cheese Awards. It has also been praised by renowned cheese experts, who have described it as "truly outstanding." This cheese undergoes strict quality tests to ensure its exceptional taste and quality.
