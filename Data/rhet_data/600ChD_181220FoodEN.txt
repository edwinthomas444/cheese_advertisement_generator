

1) Description of Cheese: Introducing "Innes Log" - a delicious cheese made by Stella Bennet and her son Joe in Staffordshire, England. This cheese has a rich history and is a true representation of the region's cheese-making traditions.

2) Description of Product: Innes Log is a soft cheese with a smooth and dense texture. It is coated with ash and has a slightly broken down rind. This cheese is made from goat's milk and is not suitable for vegetarians. It is also unpasteurized, ensuring the authentic and full flavor of the cheese. 

3) Description of Process: Innes Log is carefully prepared and aged to perfection. The cheese is made using traditional methods, giving it a unique and distinct taste. It is aged for the perfect amount of time to develop its rich and complex flavors.

4) Description of Smell and Taste: The aroma of Innes Log is not overpowering, making it a perfect choice for those who are not fond of strong-smelling cheeses. The taste is smooth and creamy with a subtle hint of goat's milk. The ash coating adds a unique earthy flavor to the cheese, making it a delight for the taste buds.
