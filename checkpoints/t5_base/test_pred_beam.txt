Upon opening the package, you will be greeted with a distinct aroma of grass and flowers. The taste of this cheese is a perfect balance of sweetness and savory notes, with a touch of tanginess. The ewe's milk, herbs, and grass give this cheese a distinct aroma of grass and flowers.
###
For the best experience, serve this cheese at room temperature to allow its flavors to fully develop. It can be presented on a cheese board, accompanied by crackers and fruit. This cheese pairs well with a variety of wines, including local cider.
###
Beenleigh Blue has received numerous accolades, including being named the "Best Blue Cheese" at the British Cheese Awards. It has also been praised by renowned chefs and food critics for its exceptional taste and quality. As one satisfied customer puts it, "Beenleigh Blue is a must-try for all cheese lovers."
